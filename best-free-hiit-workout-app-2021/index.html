The Best Free Hiit Workout App 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 01, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking for an effective way to get fit without going to the gym? Do you want to lose weight and build muscle in a short period of time? High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) might be the answer to your prayers. This type of workout involves short bursts of intense exercise followed by rest periods, and it has been proven to be one of the most efficient ways to burn fat and improve cardiovascular health. However, finding the best free HIIT workout app 2021 amidst the sea of options can be a daunting task. In this blog post, we'll explore the top free HIIT workout apps available and help you find the perfect one for your fitness journey.

If you're new to HIIT, it can be overwhelming to choose the right workout routine that will suit your fitness level and goals. Fortunately, there are many free HIIT workout apps available that cater to beginners, intermediate, and advanced users. These apps offer a variety of exercises, from bodyweight workouts to kettlebell routines, to keep your workouts interesting and challenging.

How to Choose the Best Free HIIT Workout App 2021

Choosing the best free HIIT workout app 2021 can be overwhelming, but there are a few things to keep in mind when making your decision. First, consider your fitness level and goals. If you're a beginner, look for an app that offers workouts designed for beginners. If you're more advanced, look for an app that offers challenging workouts.

Second, look for an app that offers a variety of workouts. Doing the same workout every day can get boring and may not provide you with the results you want. Third, look for an app that tracks your progress. Seeing your progress can be a great motivator and can help you stay on track. Look for an app that allows you to track your workouts and provides you with feedback on your progress.

How to Get the Most Out of Your Free HIIT Workout App 2021

Once you've chosen your free HIIT workout app 2021, there are a few things you can do to get the most out of it. First, be consistent. Make a commitment to workout regularly and stick to it. Question and Answer

Q: Is it safe to do HIIT workouts every day?

A: No, it is not recommended to do HIIT workouts every day. HIIT workouts are intense and put a lot of stress on your body. It's important to give your body time to rest and recover between workouts. Aim to do HIIT workouts 2-3 times a week and incorporate other types of exercise on the other days.

Q: Do I need equipment to do HIIT workouts?

A: No, you don't need equipment to do HIIT workouts. Many HIIT workouts use just your bodyweight, so you can do them anywhere, anytime. However, if you want to increase the intensity of your workouts, you can use equipment like dumbbells, resistance bands, or kettlebells.

Q: Can HIIT workouts help me lose weight?

A: Yes, HIIT workouts can help you lose weight. HIIT workouts burn more calories than traditional cardio workouts, which can help you lose weight. Additionally, HIIT workouts help to increase your metabolism, which can help you burn more calories throughout the day.

Q: How long should a HIIT workout be?

A: A typical HIIT workout lasts between 20-30 minutes. However, the length of your workout will depend on your fitness level and goals. If you're a beginner, start with shorter workouts and gradually increase the duration as you get stronger.

Remember to choose an app that suits your fitness level and goals, provides a variety of workouts, and tracks your progress. Follow the tips on how to get the most out of your free HIIT workout app, and you'll be on your way to a healthier and fitter you!
