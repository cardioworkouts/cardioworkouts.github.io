Aerobic Exercise Plan Table Brainly Explained

Aerobic exercise is any form of physical activity that raises your heart rate and gets your blood flowing. This type of exercise is important for maintaining good health as it helps to strengthen your heart, lungs, and muscles, while also reducing your risk of chronic diseases such as obesity, diabetes, and heart disease.

Creating an aerobic exercise plan table brainly involves selecting activities that will get your heart rate up and keep it elevated for an extended period. This can include activities such as running, cycling, swimming, or dancing. The goal is to engage in these activities for at least 30 minutes per day, five days per week.
