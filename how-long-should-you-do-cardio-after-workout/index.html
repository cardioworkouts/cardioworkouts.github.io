How Long Should You Do Cardio After Workout

Written by Aprilia Nov 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you one of those people who are confused about how long should you do cardio after workout? Do you want to know the best practices to follow when it comes to cardio after workout? If yes, then keep reading!

The ideal time duration for cardio after workout is 20-30 minutes. It is important to note that the duration and intensity of cardio after workout depends on various factors such as your fitness level, age, weight, and the workout you performed before cardio. It is always recommended to consult a fitness expert before starting any exercise regime.

In summary, the best time duration for cardio after workout is 20-30 minutes. However, it is important to consider individual factors before deciding on the duration and intensity of cardio after workout.

Why is Cardio After Workout Important?

When I started working out, I used to skip cardio after workout. I thought that lifting weights was enough to keep me fit. However, when I consulted a fitness expert, I learned that cardio after workout is important for various reasons. Firstly, it helps in burning more calories, which is essential for weight loss. Secondly, it helps in reducing muscle stiffness and soreness. Lastly, it improves cardiovascular health, which is important for overall health and fitness.

How to Determine the Right Duration for Cardio After Workout?

As mentioned earlier, the right duration for cardio after workout depends on various factors such as your fitness level, age, weight, and the workout you performed before cardio. A general rule of thumb is to start with 20-30 minutes of cardio after workout and gradually increase the duration and intensity based on your fitness level and goals.

Factors to Consider When Deciding on the Duration of Cardio After Workout

1. Fitness level: If you are a beginner, start with a shorter duration of cardio after workout and gradually increase it as you progress. If you are an advanced athlete, you can increase the duration and intensity of cardio after workout.

2. Age: As we age, our metabolism slows down, and it becomes harder to lose weight. Therefore, older individuals may need to perform longer duration of cardio after workout to achieve their weight loss goals.

3. Weight: If you are overweight, you may need to perform longer duration of cardio after workout to burn more calories and lose weight.

Tips for Effective Cardio After Workout

1. Start with a warm-up: Before starting cardio after workout, it is important to perform a 5-10 minute warm-up to prevent injury and prepare your body for exercise.

2. Choose the right type of cardio: There are various forms of cardio such as running, cycling, swimming, and dancing. Choose the one that you enjoy and can perform consistently.

3. Gradually increase the duration and intensity: As mentioned earlier, start with a shorter duration and gradually increase it over time. Also, increase the intensity of cardio after workout to challenge your body and achieve your fitness goals.

Personal Experience

When I started working out, I used to perform cardio before my workout. However, when I consulted a fitness expert, I learned that cardio after workout is more effective for weight loss and overall health. Therefore, I started performing 20-30 minutes of cardio after my workout, and I noticed a significant improvement in my fitness level and weight loss goals.

Question and Answer

Q1. Can I perform cardio after every workout?
A1. Yes, you can perform cardio after every workout. However, it is important to consider individual factors before deciding on the duration and intensity of cardio after workout.

Q2. How does cardio after workout help in weight loss?
A2. Cardio after workout helps in burning more calories, which is essential for weight loss.

Q3. How can I prevent muscle soreness after cardio after workout?
A3. You can prevent muscle soreness after cardio after workout by performing a proper warm-up, cool-down, and stretching. Also, make sure to hydrate and consume enough protein after your workout.

Q4. Can I perform cardio after weight lifting?
A4. Yes, you can perform cardio after weight lifting. In fact, it is recommended to perform cardio after weight lifting for maximum weight loss and overall fitness.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the ideal duration for cardio after workout is 20-30 minutes. However, it is important to consider individual factors before deciding on the duration and intensity of cardio after workout. Cardio after workout is important for weight loss, reducing muscle stiffness and soreness, and improving cardiovascular health. Remember to start with a warm-up, choose the right type of cardio, gradually increase the duration and intensity, and consult a fitness expert before starting any exercise regime. Happy exercising!
