How Long Should You Workout At The Gym To Lose Weight

Written by Eveline Jul 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you trying to lose weight but unsure of how long you should be working out at the gym? It can be overwhelming to navigate how much time you should spend exercising in order to reach your weight loss goals. In this post, we'll break down the recommended amount of time you should be working out at the gym to lose weight.

Many people struggle with weight loss and may feel discouraged when they don't see results right away. It's important to remember that weight loss is a process and it takes time and effort to see progress. One of the biggest challenges people face is figuring out how long they should be working out in order to lose weight.

How Long Should You Workout at the Gym to Lose Weight?

The amount of time you should be working out at the gym to lose weight depends on a few factors such as your current fitness level, your weight loss goals, and the type of exercise you are doing. The American Heart Association recommends at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous aerobic activity per week for overall cardiovascular health.

If your goal is weight loss, you may need to increase the amount of time you spend working out. The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention recommends 300 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity or 150 minutes of vigorous aerobic activity per week for weight loss.

Personal Experience with Workout Length for Weight Loss

Personally, when I was trying to lose weight, I aimed to exercise for at least an hour per day, five days a week. I found that this amount of exercise helped me reach my weight loss goals and also improved my overall fitness level. It's important to find a workout routine that works for you and that you enjoy doing in order to stick with it long-term.

How to Increase Your Workout Length Safely

If you are new to exercise, it's important to start slow and gradually increase your workout length over time. You don't want to push yourself too hard and risk injury. Start with 20-30 minutes of exercise per day and gradually increase your time by 5-10 minutes each week until you reach your desired workout length.

Tips for Making the Most of Your Workout Time

If you have a busy schedule and struggle to find time to workout, here are some tips to help you make the most of your workout time:

  • Choose high-intensity workouts that burn more calories in less time
  • Break up your workouts throughout the day if you can't find time for a long workout
  • Make sure to include strength training in your workout routine to build muscle and boost your metabolism

How to Measure Your Workout Intensity

It's important to make sure you are working out at the right intensity in order to see weight loss results. There are a few ways to measure your workout intensity:

  • Heart rate: Aim to exercise at 70-85% of your maximum heart rate
  • Talk test: If you can talk during your workout but not sing, you are exercising at a moderate intensity. If you can't talk at all, you are exercising at a vigorous intensity.
  • Perceived exertion: Rate your perceived exertion on a scale of 1-10. Aim for a 5-6 for moderate intensity or a 7-8 for vigorous intensity.

Conclusion of How Long Should You Workout at the Gym to Lose Weight

In summary, the recommended amount of time you should be working out at the gym to lose weight is at least 150-300 minutes of moderate-intensity or 75-150 minutes of vigorous aerobic activity per week. It's important to find a workout routine that works for you and to gradually increase your workout length over time. Remember to include strength training and to monitor your workout intensity for maximum weight loss results.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I workout for too long?

A: Yes, it is possible to over-exercise and risk injury or burnout. It's important to listen to your body and take rest days as needed.

Q: Can I split up my workout time throughout the day?

A: Yes, splitting up your workout time throughout the day can still provide health benefits. Aim for at least 10 minutes of exercise at a time.

Q: Do I need to do cardio or can I just do strength training?

A: While strength training is important for building muscle and boosting metabolism, cardio is also important for weight loss and overall cardiovascular health. Aim to include both types of exercise in your workout routine.

Q: How long will it take to see weight loss results?

A: Weight loss results can vary depending on a variety of factors such as diet, exercise, and genetics. It's important to focus on making healthy lifestyle changes and not just on the number on the scale.
