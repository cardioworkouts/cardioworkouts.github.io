The Best Hiit Exercise Bikes For A Full Body Workout

Written by Frank Jul 08, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old cardio routine? Do you want to spice up your workout with something new and challenging? If so, a HIIT exercise bike may be just what you need. These bikes offer a full body workout that targets multiple muscle groups while also burning calories and improving cardiovascular health.

HIIT stands for High-Intensity Interval Training, which involves short bursts of intense exercise followed by periods of rest or low-intensity exercise. This type of training has been shown to be more effective for weight loss and muscle gain than traditional steady-state cardio.

When it comes to HIIT exercise bikes, there are a variety of options available. Some of the best HIIT exercise bikes on the market include the Schwinn Airdyne, the Assault AirBike, and the NordicTrack Commercial S22i Studio Cycle. These bikes offer a range of features and price points to fit any budget and fitness level.

In summary, HIIT exercise bikes offer a fun and challenging way to get a full body workout. With a variety of options available on the market, there is a HIIT exercise bike to fit any fitness level and budget.

Schwinn Airdyne

As someone who has been using the Schwinn Airdyne for several years now, I can attest to its effectiveness as a HIIT exercise bike. The bike features a fan wheel that creates resistance as you pedal, allowing you to adjust the intensity of your workout. The Airdyne also has moving handlebars that work your upper body while you pedal, making it a true full body workout.

Assault AirBike

The Assault AirBike is another popular choice for a HIIT exercise bike. It features a heavy-duty frame and adjustable seat and handlebars to accommodate users of all sizes. The bike also has a large fan wheel that creates unlimited resistance, allowing you to push yourself to your limits.

NordicTrack Commercial S22i Studio Cycle

The NordicTrack Commercial S22i Studio Cycle is a more high-tech option for a HIIT exercise bike. It features a 22-inch HD touchscreen display that allows you to access live and on-demand classes led by professional trainers. The bike also has adjustable resistance and incline to simulate outdoor riding conditions.

How to Use a HIIT Exercise Bike

Using a HIIT exercise bike is simple. Start by warming up for a few minutes at a low intensity. Then, increase the resistance and pedal as hard as you can for 30-60 seconds. Follow this with a period of rest or low-intensity exercise for 60-90 seconds. Repeat this cycle for 20-30 minutes for a full body workout.

Conclusion of Best HIIT Exercise Bike

Overall, a HIIT exercise bike is a great way to get a full body workout that improves cardiovascular health and burns calories. The Schwinn Airdyne, Assault AirBike, and NordicTrack Commercial S22i Studio Cycle are all excellent options to consider when looking for the best HIIT exercise bike for your fitness goals and budget. So, what are you waiting for? Get spinning!

Question and Answer

Q: Can I do HIIT on a regular exercise bike?

A: Yes, you can do HIIT on a regular exercise bike by adjusting the resistance and pedaling at a high intensity for short intervals followed by periods of rest or low-intensity exercise.

Q: How often should I do HIIT on an exercise bike?

A: It is recommended to do HIIT workouts 2-3 times per week with at least one day of rest in between sessions to allow your muscles to recover.

Q: Can HIIT on an exercise bike help me lose weight?

A: Yes, HIIT workouts on an exercise bike can help you lose weight by burning calories and increasing muscle mass.

Q: Are there any safety precautions I should take when doing HIIT on an exercise bike?

A: Always consult with your doctor before starting a new exercise program. Start slowly and gradually increase the intensity of your workouts. Make sure to stay hydrated and listen to your body to avoid injury.
