How To Use Arm Machines At Planet Fitness For A Killer Workout

Written by Eveline Jun 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you new to the gym and feeling overwhelmed by the variety of machines available? Don't worry, you're not alone! Many people struggle with figuring out how to use arm machines at Planet Fitness. But fear not, because we're here to help you achieve your fitness goals.

Using arm machines at Planet Fitness can be intimidating, especially if you're not familiar with them. But learning how to use them can help you build strength, tone your muscles, and achieve a killer workout. In this post, we'll guide you through the process of using arm machines at Planet Fitness, so you can get the most out of your gym membership.

First and foremost, it's important to understand that arm machines at Planet Fitness are designed to work specific muscle groups. Each machine is labeled with the muscle group it targets, so you can easily identify which one you want to focus on. To get started, select the machine that targets the muscle group you want to work on, and adjust the seat and weight to suit your needs.

Using the Bicep Curl Machine

The bicep curl machine is a popular choice for those looking to build and tone their biceps. To use this machine, adjust the seat to a comfortable height and select the weight you want to work with. Sit down, place your arms on the pad, and grip the handles. Slowly curl your arms toward your chest, hold for a few seconds, and then lower them back down to the starting position. Repeat for your desired number of reps.

Using the Tricep Extension Machine

The tricep extension machine is ideal for targeting the back of your arms. To use this machine, adjust the seat to a comfortable height and select the weight you want to work with. Sit down, place your arms on the pad, and grip the handles. Slowly extend your arms behind you, hold for a few seconds, and then lower them back down to the starting position. Repeat for your desired number of reps.

Using the Shoulder Press Machine

The shoulder press machine is perfect for strengthening and toning your shoulders. To use this machine, adjust the seat to a comfortable height and select the weight you want to work with. Sit down, place your arms on the pads, and grip the handles. Push the handles up and out, hold for a few seconds, and then lower them back down to the starting position. Repeat for your desired number of reps.

Using the Chest Press Machine

The chest press machine is great for building and toning your chest muscles. To use this machine, adjust the seat to a comfortable height and select the weight you want to work with. Sit down, place your arms on the pads, and grip the handles. Push the handles forward, hold for a few seconds, and then slowly bring them back to the starting position. Repeat for your desired number of reps.

Question and Answer

Q: How many reps should I do on each machine?

A: It's recommended to do 3 sets of 10-12 reps on each machine, with a 30-second break in between each set.

Q: How often should I use arm machines at Planet Fitness?

A: You can use arm machines at Planet Fitness up to 3 times a week, with at least one day of rest in between each workout.

Q: Can I adjust the weight on each machine?

A: Yes, you can adjust the weight on each machine to suit your fitness level and goals.

Q: Are arm machines at Planet Fitness suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, arm machines at Planet Fitness are suitable for beginners. In fact, they're a great way to get started with strength training.

Conclusion of How to Use Arm Machines at Planet Fitness

Using arm machines at Planet Fitness is a great way to build strength and tone your muscles. By selecting the right machine and adjusting the weight and seat, you can target specific muscle groups and achieve your fitness goals. Remember to start slow and gradually increase the weight and reps as you get stronger. And most importantly, have fun and enjoy your workout!
