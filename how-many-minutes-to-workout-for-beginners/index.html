How Many Minutes To Workout For Beginners

Written by Eveline Sep 12, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a beginner who wants to start working out but doesn't know how long to do it for? Do you feel overwhelmed by the amount of conflicting information out there? Don't worry, you're not alone. In this post, we'll break down how many minutes to workout for beginners in simple, easy-to-understand terms.

Pain Points

When it comes to starting a new workout routine, there are several pain points that beginners often face. Some common concerns include:

  • Feeling unsure about how much time to devote to working out
  • Worrying about over-exerting themselves
  • Feeling intimidated by more experienced gym-goers

These concerns can make it difficult to know how many minutes to workout for beginners. However, with the right information and guidance, it is possible to start a successful workout routine.

How Many Minutes to Workout for Beginners?

So, how many minutes should a beginner workout? The general recommendation is to aim for at least 30 minutes of exercise per day, five days a week. This can include a mix of cardio and strength training.

However, it's important to remember that everyone's fitness level is different. If 30 minutes feels too challenging at first, start with 10-15 minutes and work your way up. It's also important to listen to your body and take rest days as needed.

In addition, it's important to warm up before starting any workout and stretch afterwards to prevent injury and promote muscle recovery.

Main Points

In summary, as a beginner, you should aim for at least 30 minutes of exercise per day, five days a week. However, it's important to start slow and work your way up. Listen to your body and take rest days as needed. Remember to warm up and stretch before and after your workout.

Why 30 Minutes?

Thirty minutes of exercise per day is a good starting point for beginners because it provides enough time to get the heart rate up and work the major muscle groups. This can help improve cardiovascular health, build strength, and increase overall fitness. Plus, it's a manageable amount of time that can fit into most schedules.

Personally, I started with 15 minutes of exercise per day and gradually worked my way up to 30 minutes. It took time and effort, but it was worth it in the end. I now feel stronger, more energized, and more confident in my abilities.

Strength Training vs. Cardio

When it comes to how many minutes to workout for beginners, it's important to include a mix of cardio and strength training. Cardio exercises, such as running, biking, or swimming, help improve cardiovascular health and burn calories. Strength training exercises, such as weight lifting or bodyweight exercises, help build muscle and increase metabolism.

Personally, I like to alternate between cardio and strength training days. This helps keep my workouts interesting and challenging. I aim for at least 30 minutes of exercise per day, but sometimes I'll do more if I have the time and energy.

Cardio Recommendations

For cardio, beginners can start with low-impact exercises such as walking, cycling, or swimming. As your fitness level improves, you can gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts.

Personally, I like to mix it up with a combination of running, cycling, and swimming. I aim for at least 30 minutes of cardio per day, five days a week.

Strength Training Recommendations

For strength training, beginners can start with bodyweight exercises such as push-ups, squats, and lunges. As your strength improves, you can gradually add weights or resistance bands to your workouts.

Personally, I like to do a mix of bodyweight exercises and weight lifting. I aim for at least 30 minutes of strength training per day, three days a week.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I workout for more than 30 minutes per day?

A: Absolutely! If you have the time and energy, you can increase the duration of your workouts. Just remember to listen to your body and take rest days as needed.

Q: What if I don't have 30 minutes to workout per day?

A: That's okay! Any exercise is better than none. Try to fit in a few minutes of activity throughout the day, such as taking the stairs instead of the elevator, or going for a quick walk during your lunch break.

Q: Can I just do cardio or strength training, or do I need to do both?

A: It's recommended to do a mix of cardio and strength training for overall fitness and health. However, if you have a specific fitness goal in mind, such as running a 5K or building muscle, you can focus more on one type of exercise.

Q: How do I know if I'm over-exerting myself?

A: It's important to listen to your body and pay attention to any signs of pain or discomfort. If you feel dizzy, short of breath, or experience any sharp pains, stop exercising and rest. If symptoms persist, consult with a doctor.

Conclusion of How Many Minutes to Workout for Beginners

As a beginner, it can be challenging to know how many minutes to workout. However, with the right information and guidance, you can start a successful workout routine. Aim for at least 30 minutes of exercise per day, but start slow and work your way up. Listen to your body and take rest days as needed. Remember to warm up and stretch before and after your workout. And don't forget to have fun!
