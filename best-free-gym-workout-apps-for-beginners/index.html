The Best Free Gym Workout Apps For Beginners

Written by Christine Jun 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a beginner looking to start working out at the gym but don't know where to start? Do you feel intimidated by the weight machines and equipment? Fear not! There are many free gym workout apps available to help you get started and feel confident in your workouts.

The target of best free gym workout apps for beginners is to provide guidance and structure to those who may be new to the gym environment. These apps offer a variety of workout plans, exercise demonstrations, and tracking features to help you stay on track and motivated.

In this article, we will be discussing some of the best free gym workout apps for beginners that can help you achieve your fitness goals.

1. Nike Training Club

As a beginner, it can be difficult to know which exercises to do and how to do them properly. Nike Training Club offers a variety of workouts ranging from bodyweight exercises to equipment-based workouts. Each workout is accompanied by a video demonstration to ensure proper form and technique. The app also allows you to set goals and track your progress over time.

2. Fitbod

Fitbod is an app that creates personalized workout plans based on your fitness level, goals, and the equipment available at your gym. The app tracks your progress and adjusts your workouts accordingly. It also offers video demonstrations of each exercise and provides suggestions for alternate exercises if a particular one is not possible.

3. JEFIT

JEFIT is a comprehensive workout tracking app that offers a variety of workout plans for all levels, including beginners. The app also offers a social aspect where you can connect with friends for support and motivation. JEFIT also includes a feature that allows you to track your progress and set goals for yourself.

The Benefits of Using Free Gym Workout Apps for Beginners

Using a free gym workout app can be beneficial for beginners in several ways. First, these apps can provide structure and guidance for your workouts, making it easier to stay on track and achieve your fitness goals. Second, many of these apps offer exercise demonstrations to ensure proper form and technique, which can help prevent injury. Finally, many of these apps offer tracking features to help you monitor your progress and stay motivated.

How to Choose the Best Free Gym Workout App for Beginners

When choosing a free gym workout app, it's important to consider your fitness goals, fitness level, and the equipment available at your gym. Look for an app that offers a variety of workouts and exercise demonstrations, as well as tracking features to monitor your progress. It's also a good idea to read reviews and try out a few different apps to see which one works best for you.

4. MyFitnessPal

While not specifically a workout app, MyFitnessPal can be a valuable tool for beginners looking to track their nutrition and fitness goals. The app allows you to track your food intake and exercise, and provides a breakdown of your macronutrients (protein, carbs, and fat). It also offers a social aspect where you can connect with friends for support and motivation.

Question and Answer

Q: Are these apps really free?

A: Yes, all of the apps listed are free to download and use.

Q: Do I need to have a gym membership to use these apps?

A: While some of the apps may require gym equipment, many of the workouts can be done with just your bodyweight, so a gym membership is not necessarily required.

Q: How often should I use these apps?

A: It's recommended to aim for at least 3-4 workouts per week, but listen to your body and adjust as needed.

Q: Can I use these apps if I have a pre-existing injury?

A: It's always best to consult with a healthcare professional before starting a new workout routine, especially if you have a pre-existing injury. Some apps may offer modifications for certain exercises, but it's important to listen to your body and not push yourself beyond your limits.

Conclusion of Best Free Gym Workout Apps for Beginners

There are many free gym workout apps available to help beginners get started and feel confident in their workouts. From personalized workout plans to exercise demonstrations and tracking features, these apps can provide structure and guidance to help you achieve your fitness goals. When choosing an app, consider your fitness level, goals, and the equipment available at your gym, and don't be afraid to try out a few different apps to see what works best for you.
