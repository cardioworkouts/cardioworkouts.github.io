Best Free Physical Fitness Apps To Help You Stay Active And Healthy

Written by Petter Aug 31, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for ways to stay active and healthy, but don't want to spend money on expensive gym memberships or personal trainers, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll be discussing the best free physical fitness apps that can help you achieve your fitness goals without breaking the bank.

Staying active and healthy is important for both your mental and physical well-being. However, with the busy lives we lead, it can be challenging to find the time and motivation to exercise regularly. That's where these fitness apps come in handy. They offer a range of workouts and exercises that you can do from the comfort of your own home, at any time that suits you.

Target and Best Free Physical Fitness Apps

The target of this article is to provide you with a list of the best free physical fitness apps that you can use to stay active and healthy. These apps have been chosen based on their popularity, functionality, and user reviews. Here are the top 5 best free physical fitness apps:

  1. MyFitnessPal: This app is perfect for those who want to track their calorie intake and monitor their diet. It features a database of over 11 million foods, making it easy to log your meals and keep track of your progress.
  2. Nike Training Club: This app offers a range of workouts and exercises, from beginner to advanced levels. It also features personalized training plans and allows you to connect with friends and share your progress.
  3. Fitbit: This app is designed for those who have a Fitbit device. It tracks your activity, heart rate, and sleep, and allows you to set goals and compete with friends.
  4. Sworkit: This app offers customizable workouts that can be done anywhere, without any equipment. It includes video demonstrations and allows you to track your progress and set reminders.
  5. C25K: This app is perfect for those who want to start running but don't know where to begin. It features a 8-week program that gradually builds up your endurance and prepares you for a 5K run.

In the following sections, we'll be discussing each of these apps in more detail, along with their features and benefits.

MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is one of the most popular fitness apps out there, with over 200 million users. It offers a range of features that can help you achieve your fitness goals, such as:

  • Calorie tracking: You can log your meals and snacks, and the app will calculate the number of calories you've consumed.
  • Exercise tracking: You can log your workouts and track the number of calories burned.
  • Goal setting: You can set goals for weight loss, macronutrients, and more.
  • Community support: You can connect with friends and share your progress, or join groups and get advice and motivation from other users.

I have personally used MyFitnessPal to track my calorie intake and have found it to be very helpful in keeping me accountable. It's easy to use and has a huge database of foods, making it easy to log my meals even when I'm eating out.

Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club is another popular fitness app that offers a range of workouts and exercises designed by Nike trainers. It features:

  • Personalized training plans: You can set your fitness goals and the app will create a customized plan for you.
  • Workout library: You can choose from over 200 workouts, ranging from yoga to HIIT.
  • Video demonstrations: The app features video demonstrations of each exercise, making it easy to follow along.
  • Community support: You can connect with friends and share your progress, or join challenges and compete with other users.

I have used Nike Training Club to do at-home workouts and have found the video demonstrations to be very helpful in ensuring that I'm doing the exercises correctly. The app also allows you to adjust the difficulty level, making it suitable for all fitness levels.

Fitbit

Fitbit is a popular fitness tracker that tracks your activity, heart rate, and sleep. The app offers:

  • Activity tracking: You can track your steps, distance, and calories burned throughout the day.
  • Heart rate monitoring: You can monitor your heart rate during exercise and throughout the day.
  • Sleep tracking: You can track your sleep patterns and get insights into your sleep quality.
  • Goal setting: You can set goals for daily steps and active minutes, and the app will track your progress.

I have used Fitbit to track my daily activity and have found it to be very motivating. The app allows you to set goals and compete with friends, making it a fun way to stay active.

Sworkit

Sworkit is a workout app that offers customizable workouts that can be done anywhere, without any equipment. It features:

  • Customizable workouts: You can choose from a range of workouts and customize them to your fitness level and preferences.
  • Video demonstrations: The app features video demonstrations of each exercise, making it easy to follow along.
  • Goal setting: You can set goals for weight loss, strength training, and more.
  • Workout reminders: You can set reminders to ensure that you don't miss a workout.

I have used Sworkit to do at-home workouts and have found it to be very convenient. The app offers a range of workouts that can be done in as little as 5 minutes, making it easy to fit exercise into a busy schedule.

C25K

C25K is a running app that is designed for beginners who want to work up to running a 5K. It features:

  • 8-week program: The app offers an 8-week program that gradually builds up your endurance and prepares you for a 5K run.
  • Audio cues: The app provides audio cues to tell you when to walk and when to run.
  • Music integration: You can integrate your own music into the app, making your workouts more enjoyable.
  • Goal setting: You can set goals for distance and pace, and the app will track your progress.

I have used C25K to start running and have found the program to be very helpful in building up my endurance. The app provides audio cues to tell you when to walk and when to run, which makes it easy to follow along.

Question and Answer

Q: Do these apps require any equipment?

A: Most of these apps offer workouts that can be done with little to no equipment. However, some workouts may require dumbbells or resistance bands.

Q: Are these apps suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, all of these apps offer workouts and exercises that are suitable for beginners. They also offer personalized training plans that can help you achieve your fitness goals.

Q: Can I use these apps on my smartwatch?

A: Some of these apps are compatible with smartwatches, such as Fitbit and Nike Training Club. However, it's best to check the app's compatibility before downloading.

Q: Are these apps really free?

A: Yes, all of these apps are free to download and use. However, some apps may offer premium features that require a subscription.

Conclusion of Best Free Physical Fitness Apps

Staying active and healthy doesn't have to be expensive. With these best free physical fitness apps, you can achieve your fitness goals from the comfort of your own home, without spending a dime. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced fitness enthusiast, there's an app out there for you. So why not give them a try and see how they can help you stay active and healthy?
