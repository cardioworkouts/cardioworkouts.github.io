How Many Calories Does 20 Minutes Of Working Out Burn

Written by Luffy Aug 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you someone who wants to get fit but doesn't have much time to spare? Or maybe you're someone who wants to know how much calories you burn during a short workout session? Whatever your reason may be, the question "how many calories does 20 minutes of working out burn?" is a common one. In this post, we'll explore the answer to this question, as well as some related keywords to help you understand the topic better.

Working out for a short period of time can be challenging, especially if you're trying to burn a significant amount of calories. The idea of only working out for 20 minutes may seem too good to be true, but it's actually possible to burn a decent number of calories in that amount of time.

The number of calories you can burn during a 20-minute workout session depends on several factors such as your age, gender, weight, and the type of exercise you're doing. On average, a 20-minute workout session can burn anywhere from 100 to 300 calories. Keep in mind that this number is just an estimate and can vary depending on the individual.

In summary, a 20-minute workout session can burn anywhere from 100 to 300 calories. However, the actual number of calories you burn may vary depending on your individual factors such as age, gender, weight, and the type of exercise you're doing. To get a more accurate estimate of the number of calories you burn during your workout, you can use a fitness tracker or consult a fitness professional.

The Benefits of a 20-Minute Workout

While a 20-minute workout session may not seem like much, it can provide several benefits. For one, it's a great way to get started if you're just beginning your fitness journey. A 20-minute workout can also help you maintain your fitness level if you're short on time. Additionally, a 20-minute workout can boost your metabolism and improve your overall health.

My Personal Experience with a 20-Minute Workout

As a busy person, I don't always have time for a long workout session. That's why I decided to try a 20-minute workout routine. I started with a simple routine that included jumping jacks, push-ups, squats, and lunges. To my surprise, I was able to burn around 150 calories in just 20 minutes. Not only did I feel energized, but I also felt accomplished knowing that I was able to fit in a workout despite my busy schedule.

The Best Exercises to Burn Calories in 20 Minutes

If you're looking to maximize your calorie burn in a 20-minute workout session, there are several exercises you can do. Some of the best exercises include burpees, jumping jacks, mountain climbers, kettlebell swings, and high-intensity interval training (HIIT). These exercises are great for getting your heart rate up and burning calories quickly.

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)

One of the most effective ways to burn calories in a short amount of time is through high-intensity interval training (HIIT). HIIT involves short bursts of intense exercise followed by periods of rest. This type of training has been shown to be more effective at burning calories than steady-state cardio. Some great HIIT exercises include burpees, jumping jacks, and mountain climbers.

Kettlebell Swings

Kettlebell swings are another great exercise for burning calories in a short amount of time. This exercise targets multiple muscle groups and can burn up to 20 calories per minute. To perform a kettlebell swing, stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold a kettlebell with both hands. Swing the kettlebell between your legs, then thrust your hips forward to swing the kettlebell up to shoulder height.

Conclusion

In conclusion, a 20-minute workout session can burn anywhere from 100 to 300 calories depending on your individual factors and the type of exercise you're doing. While it may not seem like much, a 20-minute workout can provide several benefits and is a great way to fit in exercise if you're short on time.
