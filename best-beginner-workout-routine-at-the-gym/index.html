Best Beginner Workout Routine At The Gym A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a beginner who wants to start working out at the gym but doesn't know where to start? Starting a new workout routine can be intimidating, especially if you don't have any prior experience. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this guide, we'll walk you through the best beginner workout routine at the gym to help you achieve your fitness goals.

Starting a new workout routine can be challenging, especially if you're not sure where to begin. You may be wondering which exercises to do, how many reps and sets to perform, and how often to train. These are all common pain points that beginners face when starting a new workout routine.

The best beginner workout routine at the gym should focus on full-body exercises that work multiple muscle groups at once. This type of workout is ideal for beginners because it allows you to work out more frequently while giving your muscles enough time to recover. Additionally, full-body workouts are more efficient because they burn more calories and improve your overall fitness level.

Cardiovascular Exercise

Cardiovascular exercise is an essential component of any workout routine, especially for beginners. Cardio exercises, such as running, cycling, and rowing, help improve your cardiovascular health, increase your endurance, and burn calories. For beginners, we recommend starting with 20-30 minutes of cardio exercise per session, three to four times per week.

Personally, I started my fitness journey with 20 minutes of jogging on the treadmill every other day. It was challenging at first, but I gradually increased my pace and duration over time. Eventually, I was able to run for 30 minutes straight without stopping.

Strength Training

Strength training is another vital component of the best beginner workout routine at the gym. Strength exercises, such as squats, lunges, and push-ups, help build muscle, increase bone density, and boost your metabolism. For beginners, we recommend starting with two to three strength training sessions per week, focusing on full-body exercises.

When I first started strength training, I was intimidated by the weight room and unsure of how to use the equipment. I started with bodyweight exercises, such as push-ups and squats, and gradually added weights as I became more comfortable. Don't be afraid to ask a trainer for help or guidance if you're unsure of how to perform an exercise.

Core Training

Core training is an essential part of the best beginner workout routine at the gym. Core exercises, such as planks and crunches, help build a strong and stable core, which is important for overall strength and balance. For beginners, we recommend incorporating core exercises into your strength training routine, two to three times per week.

Personally, I struggled with core exercises when I first started working out. I found planks and crunches to be challenging, but I stuck with it and gradually improved over time. Don't be discouraged if you can't perform an exercise perfectly at first. Consistency and practice are key.

Stretching and Flexibility

Stretching and flexibility exercises are often overlooked but are just as important as cardiovascular and strength training. Stretching helps improve your range of motion, prevent injuries, and reduce muscle soreness. For beginners, we recommend stretching before and after every workout session.

When I first started working out, I neglected stretching and suffered from muscle soreness and stiffness as a result. I learned the hard way that stretching and flexibility exercises are essential for overall fitness and wellness.

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I work out as a beginner?

A: We recommend starting with three to four workouts per week, with at least one rest day in between each session.

Q: Do I need to hire a personal trainer as a beginner?

A: While it's not necessary to hire a personal trainer, it can be helpful to get guidance and support when starting a new workout routine.

Q: How long should my workouts be as a beginner?

A: We recommend starting with 30-45 minute workouts and gradually increasing your duration as you become more comfortable.

Q: Should I do cardio or strength training first?

A: It doesn't matter which one you do first, as long as you do both. Some people prefer to do cardio first to warm up, while others prefer to do strength training first to conserve energy.

Conclusion of Best Beginner Workout Routine at the Gym

Starting a new workout routine can be challenging, but it's also incredibly rewarding. The best beginner workout routine at the gym should focus on full-body exercises, including cardiovascular, strength, core, and flexibility training. Remember to start slow, listen to your body, and be consistent. With time and dedication, you'll achieve your fitness goals and feel stronger and healthier than ever before.
