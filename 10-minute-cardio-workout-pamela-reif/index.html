What is the 10 Minute Cardio Workout by Pamela Reif?

As someone who has tried and tested the 10 minute cardio workout by Pamela Reif, I can attest to its effectiveness. This workout consists of several high-intensity exercises, including jumping jacks, mountain climbers, and high knees. Each exercise is performed for 30 seconds, followed by a 10-second rest period. The workout can be repeated up to three times for a total of 10 minutes.

The 10 minute cardio workout by Pamela Reif is perfect for those who are short on time but still want to incorporate exercise into their daily routine. The exercises are easy to follow and require no equipment, making it the perfect at-home workout.

How to Perform the 10 Minute Cardio Workout by Pamela Reif

To perform the 10 minute cardio workout by Pamela Reif, start by warming up with a few minutes of light cardio, such as jogging in place or jumping jacks. Once you are warmed up, begin the workout with 30 seconds of jumping jacks, followed by a 10-second rest period. Next, perform 30 seconds of mountain climbers, followed by a 10-second rest period. Continue this pattern with high knees, skiers, and side shuffles, each for 30 seconds with 10-second rest periods in between. Repeat the entire circuit up to three times for a total of 10 minutes.
