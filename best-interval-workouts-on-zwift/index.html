Best Interval Workouts On Zwift A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking to take your cycling training to the next level? Zwift, the virtual cycling platform, is the perfect tool for you. With its realistic graphics and immersive experience, Zwift has become a game-changer in the cycling world. But with so many interval workouts available on the platform, it can be overwhelming to decide which one is the best. In this article, we will explore the best interval workouts on Zwift and help you pick the one that suits your fitness goals.

Interval training is essential for cyclists who want to improve their endurance, speed, and power. It involves alternating between high-intensity efforts and periods of rest or recovery. However, not all interval workouts are created equal. Some may be too easy, while others may be too challenging. Finding the right balance is crucial for achieving your goals without risking injury or burnout.

What are the Best Interval Workouts on Zwift?

The best interval workouts on Zwift are those that challenge you without overwhelming you. They should be tailored to your fitness level, goals, and preferences. Here are some of the best interval workouts on Zwift:

1. The FTP Builder

The FTP Builder is an eight-week program that aims to improve your functional threshold power (FTP). It consists of four workouts per week, each lasting around 60 minutes. The workouts vary in intensity and duration, with some focusing on endurance and others on high-intensity intervals. The FTP Builder is designed for intermediate and advanced cyclists who want to improve their FTP and overall fitness.

2. The 6-Week Beginner FTP Builder

If you are new to cycling or interval training, the 6-Week Beginner FTP Builder is an excellent place to start. It is a six-week program that aims to improve your FTP and overall fitness gradually. The workouts are shorter than those in the FTP Builder, lasting between 30 and 45 minutes. They also focus on building endurance and strength rather than high-intensity intervals. The 6-Week Beginner FTP Builder is perfect for beginners or cyclists who want to ease into interval training.

3. The Short Mix

The Short Mix is a high-intensity interval workout that lasts for 45 minutes. It consists of short, intense intervals followed by periods of rest or recovery. The Short Mix is perfect for experienced cyclists who want to improve their anaerobic capacity and sprinting ability.

4. The Long Mix

The Long Mix is a longer version of the Short Mix, lasting for 90 minutes. It consists of longer, sustained efforts at high intensity, followed by periods of rest or recovery. The Long Mix is perfect for experienced cyclists who want to improve their endurance and overall fitness.

My Personal Experience with Interval Workouts on Zwift

As an avid cyclist, I have tried various interval workouts on Zwift, and I can confidently say that they have helped me improve my fitness and performance. One of my favorite interval workouts is the FTP Builder. It challenges me without overwhelming me and has helped me increase my FTP by 10%. The Short Mix and Long Mix are also great workouts that have helped me improve my anaerobic capacity and endurance, respectively.

How to Choose the Best Interval Workouts on Zwift?

Choosing the best interval workouts on Zwift depends on your fitness goals, experience, and preferences. Here are some tips to help you choose the right one:

1. Determine your fitness level

Before choosing an interval workout, determine your fitness level. If you are new to cycling or interval training, start with a beginner program. If you are an experienced cyclist, choose a more advanced program that challenges you.

2. Set realistic goals

Set realistic goals for yourself and choose an interval workout that helps you achieve them. If you want to improve your FTP, choose a program that focuses on building your FTP. If you want to improve your sprinting ability, choose a program that includes high-intensity intervals.

3. Consider your preferences

Consider your preferences when choosing an interval workout. If you enjoy longer rides, choose a program that includes longer intervals. If you prefer shorter, high-intensity efforts, choose a program that focuses on those.

Question and Answer

1. What is the best interval workout for beginners on Zwift?

The 6-Week Beginner FTP Builder is the best interval workout for beginners on Zwift. It is designed for cyclists who are new to interval training and gradually builds their fitness and endurance.

2. What is the best interval workout for improving FTP on Zwift?

The FTP Builder is the best interval workout for improving FTP on Zwift. It consists of four workouts per week, each designed to increase your FTP gradually.

3. What is the best interval workout for improving sprinting ability on Zwift?

The Short Mix is the best interval workout for improving sprinting ability on Zwift. It consists of short, intense intervals that help you develop your anaerobic capacity and sprinting ability.

4. What is the best interval workout for improving endurance on Zwift?

The Long Mix is the best interval workout for improving endurance on Zwift. It consists of longer, sustained efforts at high intensity, followed by periods of rest or recovery.

Conclusion of Best Interval Workouts on Zwift

Zwift offers a wide range of interval workouts that cater to cyclists of all levels and preferences. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced cyclist, there is an interval workout on Zwift that can help you achieve your fitness goals. By choosing the right workout for your level and goals, you can improve your endurance, speed, and power and take your cycling training to the next level.
