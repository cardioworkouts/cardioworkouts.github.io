Fitness Blender Cardio Upper Body Workout: How to Do It Correctly

The Fitness Blender Cardio Upper Body Workout consists of four exercises that are designed to target your chest, shoulders, back, and arms while also providing a cardio boost.

The four exercises are:

  1. Jumping Jacks
  2. Push-ups
  3. Mountain Climbers
  4. High Knees

To perform the Fitness Blender Cardio Upper Body Workout correctly:

  1. Do each exercise for 30 seconds, with a 10-second rest in between each exercise.
  2. Repeat the circuit three times for a total of 12 minutes.
  3. Gradually increase the intensity of your workouts by adding more reps or sets as you get stronger.
