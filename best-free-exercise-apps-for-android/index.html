Best Free Exercise Apps For Android

Written by Robby Jun 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a way to stay fit and healthy without breaking the bank on a gym membership or personal trainer? Luckily, there are many free exercise apps available for Android that can help you achieve your fitness goals from the comfort of your own home.

The best free exercise apps for Android are designed to help you stay motivated and on track with your fitness routine. They offer a variety of workouts, from cardio to strength training, and allow you to track your progress over time.

In this article, we will explore the top free exercise apps for Android and their unique features to help you find the perfect one for your fitness needs.

Overall, the best free exercise apps for Android include Nike Training Club, MyFitnessPal, Fitbit Coach, and 7 Minute Workout. These apps offer a range of workouts, personalized coaching, and tracking features to help you achieve your fitness goals.

Nike Training Club

As one of the best free exercise apps for Android, Nike Training Club offers a wide range of workouts designed by professional trainers. The app allows you to choose from over 185 different workouts, including yoga, cardio, and strength training.

One of the standout features of Nike Training Club is the personalized coaching that comes with each workout. The app offers audio and visual guidance, as well as tips on form and technique. You can also track your progress and set goals to help you stay motivated.

MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is a free exercise app for Android that focuses on nutrition and calorie tracking. The app allows you to log your meals and track your daily calorie intake to help you achieve your weight loss goals.

In addition to tracking your food intake, MyFitnessPal also offers a range of workouts and exercises. The app includes a database of over 350 exercises with detailed instructions and animations to ensure proper form.

Fitbit Coach

Fitbit Coach is another one of the best free exercise apps for Android, designed to help you stay active and healthy. The app offers personalized coaching and a range of workouts, including running, yoga, and strength training.

One of the standout features of Fitbit Coach is the ability to track your progress and set goals. The app also offers audio and visual guidance to help you stay motivated and on track with your fitness routine.

7 Minute Workout

For those who are short on time, the 7 Minute Workout app is a great option. As the name suggests, each workout is only 7 minutes long, making it easy to fit into your busy schedule.

The app offers a range of exercises, including jumping jacks, push-ups, and squats, and allows you to customize your workout to target specific muscle groups. You can also track your progress and set goals to help you stay motivated.

Question and Answer

Q: Are these apps suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, all of the apps offer workouts for beginners, as well as more advanced options for those who are more experienced.

Q: Do I need any equipment to use these apps?

A: It depends on the app and the workout you choose. Some workouts require no equipment, while others may require weights or other fitness equipment.

Q: Can I use these apps offline?

A: It depends on the app. Some apps require an internet connection to access workouts and tracking features, while others offer offline options.

Q: Are these apps completely free?

A: Yes, all of the apps listed in this article are completely free to download and use.

Conclusion of Best Free Exercise Apps for Android

Overall, the best free exercise apps for Android are a great way to stay active and healthy without breaking the bank on a gym membership or personal trainer. With a wide range of workouts, personalized coaching, and tracking features, these apps offer everything you need to achieve your fitness goals from the comfort of your own home.
