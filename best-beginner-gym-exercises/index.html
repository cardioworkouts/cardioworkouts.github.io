Best Beginner Gym Exercises A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you new to the gym and feeling lost or overwhelmed with all the equipment and exercises available? Don't worry, you're not alone. Starting a fitness journey can be intimidating, especially when it comes to selecting the right exercises to target your goals. But fear not, we've got you covered with some of the best beginner gym exercises to get you started on your fitness journey.

When starting out, many people make the mistake of trying to do too much too soon, which can lead to injury or burnout. It's important to start with exercises that are manageable and build up gradually over time. Additionally, it's crucial to choose exercises that target multiple muscle groups, as this will help you get the most out of your workout and achieve your goals faster.

The target of best beginner gym exercises is to help beginners build a strong foundation of strength, balance, and flexibility. These exercises will also help beginners learn proper technique and form, which will prevent injury and ensure that they get the most out of their workout sessions.

In summary, the best beginner gym exercises should be manageable, target multiple muscle groups, and focus on building strength, balance, and flexibility. With that in mind, let's dive into some of the best exercises for beginners.

Squats

Squats are one of the best exercises for beginners as they target multiple muscle groups, including the glutes, quads, and hamstrings. They are also a great way to improve balance and overall strength. When performing squats, it's important to maintain proper form, which means keeping your back straight, feet shoulder-width apart, and knees in line with your toes. Start with bodyweight squats and gradually add weight as you become more comfortable with the exercise.

Push-ups

Push-ups are another great exercise for beginners, as they target the chest, shoulders, and triceps. They can be modified to suit your fitness level, making them accessible to everyone. To perform a push-up, start in a plank position with your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart. Lower your body until your chest touches the ground, then push back up to the starting position. If a full push-up is too difficult, you can start with modified push-ups on your knees or against a wall.

Plank

The plank is a great exercise for building core strength, which is essential for good posture and balance. To perform a plank, start in a push-up position with your forearms on the ground instead of your hands. Keep your body straight and hold this position for as long as you can. As you become more comfortable with the exercise, you can increase the duration of the plank.

Lunges

Lunges are another great exercise for beginners, as they target multiple muscle groups, including the glutes, quads, and hamstrings. They also help to improve balance and coordination. To perform a lunge, start with your feet shoulder-width apart and take a step forward with one foot. Lower your body until your back knee is almost touching the ground, then push back up to the starting position. Repeat with the other leg.

Question and Answer

Q: How many sets and reps should I do as a beginner?

A: It's recommended to start with 2-3 sets of 12-15 reps for each exercise, gradually increasing the weight and reps over time as you become more comfortable with the exercise.

Q: Should I stretch before or after my workout?

A: It's best to warm up with some light cardio and dynamic stretching before your workout, and then cool down with static stretching after your workout.

Q: How often should I workout as a beginner?

A: It's recommended to start with 2-3 workouts per week, with at least one day of rest in between each workout. As you become more comfortable with your routine, you can gradually increase the frequency and intensity of your workouts.

Q: Can I do these exercises at home without any equipment?

A: Yes, all of these exercises can be done at home without any equipment. Bodyweight exercises are a great way to get started on your fitness journey, and can be just as effective as gym workouts.

Conclusion of Best Beginner Gym Exercises

Starting a fitness journey can be intimidating, but with the right exercises and mindset, anyone can achieve their fitness goals. Remember to start slowly and gradually build up, focusing on proper form and technique. By incorporating these best beginner gym exercises into your routine, you'll be well on your way to building a strong foundation of strength, balance, and flexibility.
