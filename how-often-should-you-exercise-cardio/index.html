How Often Should You Exercise Cardio

Written by Bardi Jun 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you find yourself wondering how often you should exercise cardio? Are you confused about the optimal number of sessions you should do in a week? If so, you're not alone. Many people struggle with figuring out how often they should be doing cardio workouts.

There are several pain points associated with figuring out how often you should exercise cardio. For example, you may be worried about overtraining or getting injured if you do too much cardio. On the other hand, you may be concerned that you're not doing enough cardio to see any real results.

The target for how often you should exercise cardio depends on your goals and fitness level. However, most experts recommend that you do cardio at least three to five times per week.

To summarize, the optimal number of cardio sessions you should aim for is three to five times per week. This will help you achieve your fitness goals without risking injury or overtraining.

How Often Should You Exercise Cardio and Why?

When I first started working out, I wasn't sure how often I should do cardio. I tried doing it every day, but I quickly realized that my body needed time to rest and recover. After doing some research, I learned that three to five cardio sessions per week is the sweet spot for most people.

The reason why you should aim for three to five cardio sessions per week is that it allows your body to recover between workouts. Doing too much cardio can put a lot of strain on your muscles and joints, which can lead to injury. On the other hand, doing too little cardio may not be enough to see any real progress.

How Long Should Your Cardio Sessions Be?

Another question people often ask is how long their cardio sessions should be. Again, this depends on your fitness level and goals. However, most experts recommend doing at least 30 minutes of cardio per session.

If you're a beginner, you may want to start with shorter sessions and gradually work your way up. For example, you could start with 15-minute sessions and increase the time by five minutes each week until you reach 30 minutes.

The Benefits of Cardio Exercise

Cardio exercise has many benefits, including improving your heart health, increasing your stamina, and helping you lose weight. It can also boost your mood and reduce stress.

When you do cardio, your heart rate increases, which helps strengthen your heart and lungs. This can lower your risk of developing heart disease or stroke.

Tips for Making Cardio Exercise More Fun

If you're not a fan of cardio exercise, there are ways to make it more enjoyable. For example, you could try listening to music or an audiobook while you work out. You could also do cardio with a friend or join a group fitness class.

How Often Should You Exercise Cardio for Weight Loss?

If your goal is weight loss, you may be wondering how often you should be doing cardio. The answer is that you should aim for at least five cardio sessions per week, each lasting 30 to 60 minutes.

However, keep in mind that weight loss isn't just about cardio. You also need to focus on your diet and strength training to see significant results.

Question and Answer

Q: Is it better to do cardio before or after strength training?
A: It's generally better to do cardio after strength training, as this allows you to use more energy for your strength training exercises.

Q: How many calories can you burn from cardio exercise?
A: The number of calories you burn from cardio exercise depends on several factors, including your weight, the type of exercise you're doing, and how long you're doing it for.

Q: Can you do too much cardio?
A: Yes, you can do too much cardio, which can lead to overtraining and injury. It's important to listen to your body and give yourself enough rest and recovery time between workouts.

Q: Is it okay to do cardio every day?
A: It's generally not recommended to do cardio every day, as your body needs time to rest and recover. Aim for three to five cardio sessions per week, with at least one rest day in between.

Conclusion of How Often Should You Exercise Cardio

In conclusion, the optimal number of cardio sessions you should aim for is three to five times per week, depending on your fitness level and goals. Make sure to listen to your body and give yourself enough rest and recovery time between workouts. Remember that cardio exercise is just one part of a healthy lifestyle, and you also need to focus on your diet and strength training to see real results.
