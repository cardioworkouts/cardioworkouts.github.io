FAQs About Full Body Women's Gym Workout Plan PDF

Q: Can I do a full body workout every day?

A: It's not recommended to do a full body workout every day. Your muscles need time to recover in between workouts, so it's best to aim for 3-4 workouts per week.

A: A full body workout should last 45-60 minutes. This allows enough time to complete all necessary exercises without overexerting your muscles.
