What are the Job Duties of a Cardiovascular Technologist?

A cardiovascular technologist is responsible for performing diagnostic procedures on patients with heart and blood vessel conditions. The job duties include preparing patients for procedures, operating medical equipment, monitoring patients during procedures, and recording test results. The technologist may also assist the physician in interpreting test results and developing treatment plans.
