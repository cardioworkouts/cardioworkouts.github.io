Itu Apa is a common phrase used in Bahasa Indonesia, which translates to "What is it?" in English.

Itu Apa Meaning is a phrase that is often used in casual conversations, especially among friends or family members. It is a question that is commonly asked when someone wants to get more information about something that they are not familiar with.
