Benefits of Circuit Training

Circuit training is a highly effective way to get in a full-body workout in a short amount of time. It involves doing a series of exercises back-to-back with little to no rest in between. This helps to keep your heart rate up, which can lead to increased calorie burn. Circuit training also combines strength and cardio exercises, making it a well-rounded workout.
