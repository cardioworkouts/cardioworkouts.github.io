Free Best Workout Routine For Apple Shape For Everyday

Written by Bardi Oct 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you an apple shape looking for the best workout routine to achieve your fitness goals? Look no further! In this post, we will discuss the best workout routine for apple shape to help you achieve the body you desire.

As an apple shape, you may struggle with excess weight around your midsection, making it difficult to find the right workout routine that targets your problem areas. You may also experience health issues related to your weight, such as high blood pressure or diabetes. It can be frustrating to find the right fitness plan that works for your body type.

Best Workout Routine for Apple Shape

The best workout routine for apple shape should focus on both cardio and strength training to help burn fat and build muscle. You want to target your midsection while also working on other areas of your body to achieve a balanced look.

Here are some tips on the best workout routine for apple shape:

Include Cardio Exercises

Cardio exercises are essential to help burn fat and improve your overall health. You should aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio exercise per week. Some great cardio exercises for apple shape include:

  • Brisk walking
  • Jogging or running
  • Dancing
  • Swimming
  • Cycling

Strength Training

Strength training is essential to help build muscle and increase your metabolism. You should aim to incorporate strength training exercises two to three times per week. Some great strength training exercises for apple shape include:

  • Planks
  • Crunches
  • Push-ups
  • Lunges
  • Squats

Personal Experience

As an apple shape myself, I have found that incorporating both cardio and strength training into my workout routine has been the most effective way to achieve my fitness goals. I have also found that focusing on my diet and making healthy food choices has helped me to see results faster.

Get a Personal Trainer

If you are struggling to find the right workout routine for your body type, consider hiring a personal trainer. They can provide you with personalized workouts and nutrition plans to help you achieve your fitness goals.

Healthy Diet

It's important to remember that exercise alone is not enough to achieve your fitness goals. You also need to focus on your diet and make healthy food choices. Aim for a balanced diet that includes plenty of fruits, vegetables, lean protein, and whole grains.

Stay Consistent and Patient

Remember that achieving your fitness goals takes time and patience. Stay consistent with your workout routine and healthy eating habits, and you will see results over time.

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I do cardio exercises?

A: You should aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio exercise per week.

Q: How often should I do strength training?

A: You should aim to incorporate strength training exercises two to three times per week.

Q: What are some good strength training exercises for apple shape?

A: Some great strength training exercises for apple shape include planks, crunches, push-ups, lunges, and squats.

Q: How important is diet in achieving my fitness goals?

A: Diet is just as important as exercise in achieving your fitness goals. Aim for a balanced diet that includes plenty of fruits, vegetables, lean protein, and whole grains.

Conclusion of Best Workout Routine for Apple Shape

As an apple shape, it can be challenging to find the right workout routine that targets your problem areas. However, by incorporating both cardio and strength training into your workout routine, focusing on your diet, and staying consistent and patient, you can achieve your fitness goals and feel confident in your body.
