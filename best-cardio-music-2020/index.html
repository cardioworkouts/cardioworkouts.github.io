That's why we've done the research and compiled a list of the best cardio music 2020 to help you get the most out of your exercise routine.

The target of best cardio music 2020 is to provide a variety of upbeat songs that will keep you motivated and engaged during your workouts. We've selected songs from different genres and eras to appeal to a wide range of tastes. Our goal is to help you find the perfect playlist to power through your next workout.

In summary, this article will provide an overview of the best cardio music 2020 to help you enhance your workout routine. We will explore the benefits of incorporating music into your exercise, provide a list of top songs, and offer tips for creating your own playlist. Let's get started!

Top Pop Hits for Cardio Workouts

As someone who loves to run, I always start my workout with a jolt of caffeine and a fresh playlist. I have found that pop music is the perfect genre to get me moving and keep me motivated throughout my run. Here are some of the top pop hits for cardio workouts:

1. "Blinding Lights" by The Weeknd

This upbeat song has a tempo of 171 beats per minute, making it the perfect pace for a high-intensity workout. The Weeknd's smooth vocals and catchy beat create a fun and energetic environment for your exercise routine.

2. "Don't Start Now" by Dua Lipa

This song has a tempo of 124 beats per minute, making it a great choice for a warm-up or cool-down. The catchy chorus and disco-inspired beat will keep you moving throughout your workout.

3. "The Bones" by Maren Morris

This country-pop crossover hit has a tempo of 96 beats per minute, making it perfect for a low-impact workout or stretching session. Morris' soulful vocals and relaxing melody create a calming atmosphere to help you unwind and recover after a tough workout.

Classic Rock Anthems for Cardio Workouts

For those who prefer a more classic vibe, rock music can provide the perfect motivation for your cardio workout. From AC/DC to ZZ Top, here are some classic rock anthems to add to your playlist:

1. "Eye of the Tiger" by Survivor

This iconic song from the Rocky III soundtrack has a tempo of 109 beats per minute, making it a great choice for a high-intensity workout. The motivational lyrics and upbeat rhythm will keep you pushing through your toughest workouts.

2. "Livin' on a Prayer" by Bon Jovi

This 80s classic has a tempo of 123 beats per minute, making it a great choice for a warm-up or cool-down. The upbeat melody and sing-along chorus will help you power through your workouts with a smile on your face.

3. "Don't Stop Believin'" by Journey

This timeless song has a tempo of 119 beats per minute, making it a great choice for a moderate-intensity workout. The uplifting message and catchy melody will keep you motivated and inspired to reach your fitness goals.

Creating Your Own Cardio Playlist

While these songs are a great starting point, creating your own playlist can be a fun and rewarding experience. Here are some tips for making the perfect cardio playlist:

1. Choose songs with a tempo that matches your workout intensity.

2. Mix up the genres to keep things interesting.

3. Include songs that have motivational lyrics to keep you inspired.

4. Don't be afraid to add guilty pleasure songs that make you happy.

How to Find the Best Cardio Music 2020

If you're struggling to find the best cardio music 2020, there are plenty of resources available to help you. Here are some options:

1. Streaming services like Spotify and Apple Music have curated playlists specifically for workouts.

2. Fitness blogs and websites often provide recommendations for workout music.

3. Ask friends or social media followers for their favorite workout songs.

4. Experiment with different genres and songs to find what works best for you.

Conclusion of Best Cardio Music 2020

In conclusion, incorporating music into your cardio workout can be a game-changer for your fitness routine. By choosing the right songs, you can increase endurance, improve mood, and distract from discomfort during exercise. The best cardio music 2020 includes a variety of genres and tempos to appeal to a wide range of tastes. Whether you choose to use our recommendations or create your own playlist, we hope this guide has helped you find the perfect music to power through your next workout.
