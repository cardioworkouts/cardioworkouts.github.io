Best Personal Training Apps For Iphone

Written by Aprilia Jul 06, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking for a way to get fit without leaving your house? Do you want to have a personal trainer, but can't afford one? Then a personal training app for iPhone might be just what you need!

Personal training apps for iPhone are designed to help you get fit and stay motivated. They offer a variety of workouts, meal plans, and tracking features to help you reach your fitness goals. With so many options available, it can be tough to know which app to choose.

In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best personal training apps for iPhone and help you find the one that's right for you. Here are our top picks:

1. Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club is a free app that offers a variety of workouts designed by world-class trainers. You can choose from over 190 workouts and customize them to fit your fitness level and goals. The app also offers personalized meal plans and allows you to track your progress over time.

One user shared, "I have been using the Nike Training Club app for months now and it has helped me stay motivated and accountable. The workouts are challenging but doable, and the meal plans are delicious and easy to follow."

2. Aaptiv

Aaptiv is a paid app that offers audio-guided workouts in a variety of categories, including running, strength training, yoga, and more. The app also offers personalized coaching and allows you to track your progress over time. With over 2,500 workouts available, there's something for everyone.

Another user stated, "I love Aaptiv because the audio-guided workouts keep me motivated and engaged. I don't have to worry about looking at my phone or watching a video – I can just focus on my workout."

3. Fitbit Coach

Fitbit Coach is a free app that offers personalized workouts based on your fitness level and goals. The app also integrates with Fitbit devices, allowing you to track your progress over time. With over 200 workouts available, there's something for everyone.

One user shared, "I love Fitbit Coach because the workouts are tailored to my fitness level and goals. Plus, the integration with my Fitbit device makes it easy to track my progress over time."

4. Daily Burn

Daily Burn is a paid app that offers a variety of workouts, including cardio, strength training, yoga, and more. The app also offers personalized coaching and meal plans to help you reach your fitness goals. With over 800 workouts available, there's something for everyone.

Another user stated, "I love Daily Burn because the workouts are challenging but doable. The personalized coaching and meal plans have helped me reach my fitness goals."

Why Personal Training Apps for iPhone are Useful?

Personal training apps for iPhone are useful because they provide a convenient and affordable way to get fit. With the ability to customize workouts and track progress, these apps make it easy to stay motivated and on track. Plus, they offer a variety of workouts and meal plans to fit your individual needs and goals.

How to Choose the Best Personal Training App for iPhone?

When choosing the best personal training app for iPhone, consider your fitness level, goals, and budget. Look for an app that offers a variety of workouts that fit your individual needs and preferences. Consider whether you want a free or paid app, and whether you want personalized coaching or meal plans.

5. 7 Minute Workout

7 Minute Workout is a free app that offers quick and effective workouts that can be done anywhere, anytime. The app features 12 high-intensity bodyweight exercises that can be completed in just 7 minutes. The app also allows you to customize your workouts and track your progress over time.

One user shared, "I love 7 Minute Workout because it's quick and effective. I can do these workouts anywhere, anytime, and still get a great workout."

Question and Answer

Q: Are personal training apps for iPhone only for people who are already fit?

A: No, personal training apps for iPhone are designed for people of all fitness levels. Many apps offer workouts that can be customized to fit your individual needs and goals.

Q: Do I need any equipment to use a personal training app for iPhone?

A: It depends on the app. Some apps offer workouts that require little or no equipment, while others require gym equipment or weights. Look for an app that fits your individual needs and preferences.

Q: Are personal training apps for iPhone as effective as working with a personal trainer?

A: Personal training apps for iPhone can be just as effective as working with a personal trainer, as long as you use them consistently and follow the workouts and meal plans provided. However, if you have specific health concerns or injuries, it's always best to consult with a medical professional before starting a new fitness program.

Q: Can personal training apps for iPhone help me lose weight?

A: Yes, many personal training apps for iPhone offer meal plans and workouts designed specifically to help you lose weight. However, weight loss is a complex process that requires a combination of proper nutrition, exercise, and lifestyle changes.

Conclusion of Best Personal Training Apps for iPhone

In conclusion, personal training apps for iPhone are a convenient and affordable way to get fit and stay motivated. With a variety of workouts, meal plans, and tracking features available, there's something for everyone. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned athlete, there's an app out there that can help you reach your fitness goals.
