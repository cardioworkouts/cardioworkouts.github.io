60 Tips Best Cardio Workouts On Youtube For Beginners At Home

Written by Eveline Jul 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Get your heart pumping and your blood flowing with the best cardio workouts on YouTube for beginners! If you're new to the world of fitness, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. But don't worry, we've got you covered.

Starting a new workout routine can be challenging, especially when it comes to cardio. You might be worried about not being fit enough or not knowing the right exercises to do. But the truth is, everyone starts somewhere, and there are plenty of resources available to help you get started.

The target of this article is to introduce you to the best cardio workouts on YouTube for beginners. We'll cover a range of exercises that are perfect for those just starting out, and we'll also provide tips on how to stay motivated and make the most of your workout.

In summary, we'll cover the best cardio workouts on YouTube for beginners and related keywords. We'll provide personal experiences, tips, and tricks to help you get started on your fitness journey.

1. Dance Workouts

Dancing is a fun and effective way to get your heart rate up and burn calories. There are plenty of dance workouts available on YouTube, ranging from hip hop to Zumba to Bollywood. You don't need any experience to get started, and you can do it from the comfort of your own home.

I personally love doing dance workouts because they're so much fun. I don't even feel like I'm exercising because I'm having such a good time. Some of my favorite YouTube channels for dance workouts include The Fitness Marshall and Dance Fitness with Jessica.

2. Jump Rope Workouts

Jumping rope is another great cardio workout that you can do anywhere. All you need is a jump rope and a little bit of space. Jump rope workouts are great because they're low-impact, so they're easy on your joints. They're also a great way to improve your coordination and balance.

I started doing jump rope workouts a few months ago, and I've noticed a huge improvement in my endurance. At first, I could only jump for a minute or two at a time, but now I can go for 10-15 minutes without stopping. Some of my favorite YouTube channels for jump rope workouts include Jump Rope Dudes and Jump Rope Fitness.

3. HIIT Workouts

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) is a popular cardio workout that involves short bursts of intense exercise followed by periods of rest. HIIT workouts are great because they're efficient - you can get a great workout in just 20-30 minutes. They're also effective for burning fat and improving cardiovascular health.

I love incorporating HIIT workouts into my routine because they're so challenging. I feel like I've really accomplished something when I finish a tough HIIT workout. Some of my favorite YouTube channels for HIIT workouts include FitnessBlender and PopSugar Fitness.

4. Walking Workouts

Walking might not seem like a very intense cardio workout, but it's actually a great way to get moving and improve your health. Walking is low-impact, so it's easy on your joints, and it's a great way to get outside and enjoy some fresh air.

I like to do walking workouts on days when I don't feel like doing anything too intense. I'll put on a podcast or some music and just walk around my neighborhood. Some of my favorite YouTube channels for walking workouts include Leslie Sansone's Walk at Home and Jessica Smith TV.

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I do cardio workouts?

A: It's recommended that adults get at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise per week. You can break this up however you like - for example, you could do 30 minutes of cardio five days a week. Just make sure to give yourself rest days so your body can recover.

Q: What should I wear for a cardio workout?

A: Wear comfortable, breathable clothing that allows you to move freely. You'll also want to wear supportive sneakers that are appropriate for your workout - for example, if you're doing a dance workout, you might want to wear sneakers with a pivot point on the sole.

Q: What if I don't have any workout equipment?

A: You don't need any fancy equipment to get a good cardio workout in. Bodyweight exercises like jumping jacks, burpees, and mountain climbers are all great ways to get your heart rate up without any equipment. Walking, jogging, and dancing are also great options.

Q: How can I stay motivated to do cardio workouts?

A: Find a workout buddy to keep you accountable, or join an online community to connect with other people who are also working towards their fitness goals. Set small, achievable goals for yourself, and celebrate your progress along the way.

Conclusion of Best Cardio Workouts on YouTube for Beginners

Getting started with cardio workouts can be intimidating, but it doesn't have to be. With the help of YouTube, you can access a wide range of workouts that are perfect for beginners. Whether you prefer dancing, jumping rope, or HIIT workouts, there's something for everyone.

Remember to start slow and listen to your body. It's okay to take breaks and modify exercises as needed. And most importantly, have fun! Working out should be something you enjoy, not something you dread.
