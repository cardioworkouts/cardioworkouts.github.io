Best Free At Home Workout Apps For Iphone

Written by Aprilia Jun 21, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of paying for expensive gym memberships or not having enough time to go to the gym? Do you want to have a workout routine that you can do anytime and anywhere? If yes, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we will talk about the best free at home workout apps for iPhone that can help you achieve your fitness goals without leaving the comfort of your own home.

Working out at home can be challenging, especially if you're not sure where to start or what to do. It can also be difficult to stay motivated without the guidance of a personal trainer or the support of a workout buddy. However, with the help of at home workout apps, you can have access to a variety of workouts, personalized plans, and tracking features that can make your fitness journey easier and more enjoyable.

The target of this article is to provide you with the best free at home workout apps for iPhone that you can download and use right away. These apps offer a range of exercises, from yoga to HIIT, and can be customized to your fitness level and goals. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced fitness enthusiast, these apps can help you stay on track and achieve your desired results.

In summary, the best free at home workout apps for iPhone can help you stay fit and healthy without leaving your home. These apps offer a range of workouts, personalized plans, and tracking features that can make your fitness journey easier and more enjoyable.

1. Nike Training Club

One of the best free at home workout apps for iPhone is the Nike Training Club. This app offers over 190 free workouts that are designed by Nike trainers and athletes. The workouts range from strength training to yoga and can be customized based on your fitness level and goals. The app also offers personalized training plans and allows you to track your progress and set reminders.

Personal experience: I have been using Nike Training Club for a year now, and I love it. The app offers a variety of workouts that are challenging and fun. I also like the fact that I can customize my plan and track my progress.

2. FitOn

FitOn is another great free at home workout app for iPhone. This app offers a range of workouts, from cardio to Pilates, that can be done at home with little to no equipment. The app also offers personalized plans, allows you to track your progress, and offers a community feature where you can connect with other users and trainers.

Personal experience: I recently started using FitOn, and I'm impressed with the variety of workouts and trainers available. The app is easy to use, and the personalized plans are helpful in keeping me on track.

3. Daily Burn

Daily Burn is a free at home workout app for iPhone that offers a range of workouts, from yoga to HIIT. The app also offers personalized plans, allows you to track your progress, and offers a community feature where you can connect with other users and trainers. The app also has a premium version that offers more features and personalized coaching.

Personal experience: I have used Daily Burn in the past, and I like the variety of workouts and personalized plans. The app also offers challenges that can help you stay motivated and on track.

4. 7 Minute Workout

7 Minute Workout is a free at home workout app for iPhone that offers quick and effective workouts that can be done in just seven minutes. The app offers a range of exercises, from jumping jacks to squats, that are designed to get your heart rate up and burn calories. The app also offers personalized plans and tracking features.

Personal experience: I like using 7 Minute Workout when I'm short on time but still want to get a quick workout in. The app is easy to use, and the workouts are challenging but doable.

Question and Answer

Q: Are these apps suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, these apps offer workouts that are suitable for beginners and can be customized based on your fitness level.

Q: Do I need any equipment to use these apps?

A: Some workouts may require equipment, but many of them can be done with little to no equipment.

Q: Are these apps available for Android users?

A: Yes, most of these apps are available for Android users as well.

Q: Can I use these apps for weight loss?

A: Yes, these apps offer workouts that can help you lose weight and achieve your fitness goals.

Conclusion of Best Free At Home Workout Apps for iPhone

The best free at home workout apps for iPhone can help you stay fit and healthy without leaving your home. These apps offer a range of workouts, personalized plans, and tracking features that can make your fitness journey easier and more enjoyable. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced fitness enthusiast, these apps can help you stay on track and achieve your desired results.
