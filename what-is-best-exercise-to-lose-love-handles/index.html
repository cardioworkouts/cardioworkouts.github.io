What Is The Best Exercise To Lose Love Handles

Written by Aprilia Oct 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you feel self-conscious about the excess fat on your hips and waist? Are you struggling to find the best exercise to lose love handles? You're not alone. Love handles, also known as muffin tops, are a common problem area for many people. They can be stubborn to get rid of, even with a proper diet and exercise routine.

The good news is that there are exercises that target your love handles and help you lose fat in that area. But first, it's important to understand that you can't spot-reduce fat. This means that doing only ab exercises won't necessarily lead to a reduction in love handles. You need to combine strength training and cardio to burn overall body fat and reveal your toned abs.

The best exercise to lose love handles is a combination of strength training and cardio. Strength training helps build muscle, which increases your metabolism and burns more calories at rest. Cardio helps burn calories and fat, which leads to a reduction in overall body fat. By combining both, you'll see faster and more effective results.

In summary, the best exercise to lose love handles is a combination of strength training and cardio. You can't spot-reduce fat, so it's important to focus on overall body fat loss to reveal your toned abs.

Strength Training Exercises for Love Handles

One of the best strength training exercises for love handles is the side plank. To do a side plank:

1. Lie on your side with your legs straight and your elbow directly beneath your shoulder.

2. Lift your hips off the ground, creating a straight line from your head to your feet.

3. Hold this position for 30-60 seconds, then switch sides.

Another effective exercise is the Russian twist. To do a Russian twist:

1. Sit on the ground with your knees bent and your feet flat on the floor.

2. Lean back slightly and lift your feet off the ground, balancing on your sit bones.

3. Hold a weight or medicine ball with both hands and twist your torso to the right, then to the left.

4. Repeat for 10-15 repetitions.

Cardio Exercises for Love Handles

Cardio exercises are also important for burning overall body fat, including love handles. Some effective cardio exercises include:

1. Running or jogging

2. Cycling

3. Swimming

4. High-intensity interval training (HIIT)

Choose the cardio exercise that you enjoy the most and can do consistently. Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio per week or 75 minutes of high-intensity cardio per week.

Additional Tips for Losing Love Handles

In addition to strength training and cardio, there are some other tips that can help you lose love handles:

1. Eat a healthy diet with plenty of protein, fiber, and healthy fats.

2. Limit your intake of processed and sugary foods.

3. Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.

4. Get enough sleep to aid in muscle recovery and fat loss.

How to Incorporate These Exercises into Your Routine

Try incorporating these exercises into your routine two to three times per week. Start with one set of each exercise and gradually increase to three sets. As you get stronger, you can also increase the weight or intensity of the exercises.

Conclusion of What is the Best Exercise to Lose Love Handles

The best exercise to lose love handles is a combination of strength training and cardio. By incorporating these exercises into your routine and following a healthy diet, you can effectively reduce overall body fat and reveal your toned abs. Remember to be patient and consistent, as it may take time to see results. With dedication and hard work, you can achieve your fitness goals and lose those stubborn love handles.

Question and Answer:

Q: Can I only do ab exercises to get rid of love handles?

A: No, you need to combine strength training and cardio to burn overall body fat and reveal your toned abs.

Q: What are some effective cardio exercises for love handles?

A: Running, cycling, swimming, and high-intensity interval training (HIIT) are all effective cardio exercises for burning overall body fat.

Q: How often should I do these exercises?

A: Try incorporating these exercises into your routine two to three times per week.

Q: Can I spot-reduce fat in my love handles?

A: No, you can't spot-reduce fat. You need to focus on overall body fat loss to reveal your toned abs.
