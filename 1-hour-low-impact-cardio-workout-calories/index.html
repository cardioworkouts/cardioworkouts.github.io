1 Hour Low Impact Cardio Workout Calories

Written by Christine Oct 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Looking for a low impact cardio workout that you can do in just an hour? Not sure how many calories you can burn during a 1 hour low impact cardio workout? Keep reading to find out!

Have you been searching for a low impact cardio workout that won't put too much strain on your joints? Maybe you're recovering from an injury, or just want to take it easy on your body. Whatever your reason, low impact cardio workouts can be a great way to get your heart rate up without risking injury or discomfort.

A 1 hour low impact cardio workout can burn anywhere from 300-500 calories, depending on factors such as your weight, age, and fitness level. Some popular low impact cardio workouts include walking, cycling, swimming, and dancing.

In summary, a 1 hour low impact cardio workout is a great way to get your heart rate up and burn some calories without putting too much strain on your body. Popular workouts include walking, cycling, swimming, and dancing, and you can expect to burn anywhere from 300-500 calories during your hour-long workout.

Walking for 1 Hour: How Many Calories Can You Burn?

Walking is a great low impact cardio workout that you can do for an hour or more. Personally, I love walking outdoors in nature, but if the weather is bad, I'll do a walking workout on my treadmill. During a 1 hour walk, you can expect to burn around 300-400 calories, depending on your weight and walking speed. To increase the intensity of your walk, try adding some hills or intervals.

Cycling for 1 Hour: How Many Calories Can You Burn?

Cycling is another great low impact cardio workout that you can do for an hour or more. Whether you prefer cycling outdoors or using a stationary bike, you can expect to burn around 400-500 calories during a 1 hour cycling workout, depending on your weight and cycling speed. To increase the intensity of your cycling workout, try adjusting the resistance or adding some sprints.

Swimming for 1 Hour: How Many Calories Can You Burn?

Swimming is a fantastic low impact cardio workout that is gentle on your joints. During a 1 hour swim, you can expect to burn around 400-500 calories, depending on your weight and swimming stroke. To increase the intensity of your swim, try adding some laps or using different strokes.

Dancing for 1 Hour: How Many Calories Can You Burn?

Dancing is a fun and exciting low impact cardio workout that doesn't feel like exercise. Whether you prefer ballet, salsa, or hip hop, dancing can burn around 300-400 calories during a 1 hour dance workout, depending on your weight and dance style. To increase the intensity of your dance workout, try adding some jumps or faster movements.

Conclusion of 1 Hour Low Impact Cardio Workout Calories

Overall, a 1 hour low impact cardio workout can be a great way to get your heart rate up and burn some calories without putting too much strain on your body. Whether you prefer walking, cycling, swimming, or dancing, there are plenty of low impact cardio workouts to choose from. So put on your workout clothes, grab some water, and get ready to sweat!
