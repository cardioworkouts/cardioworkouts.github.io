Target: High-Intensity Cardio

When it comes to high-intensity cardio, the right music can make all the difference. Personally, I love to listen to upbeat electronic music with a fast BPM (beats per minute) to keep me moving. Some of my favorite artists to listen to during a high-intensity cardio workout are The Chainsmokers, Calvin Harris, and David Guetta.

Other popular genres for high-intensity cardio include hip hop, pop, and rock. The key is to find music that matches the intensity of your workout and keeps you motivated to push harder.

Target: Yoga and Stretching

For yoga and stretching, the best workout music 2021 Soundcloud has to offer is calming and relaxing. I personally enjoy listening to instrumental music with soft melodies and nature sounds in the background. Some popular artists for yoga and stretching playlists include Ludovico Einaudi, Max Richter, and Yiruma.

Other popular genres for yoga and stretching include ambient, new age, and classical music. The key is to find music that helps you relax and focus on your breath and movements.

Target: Strength Training

When it comes to strength training, the best workout music 2021 Soundcloud has to offer is energetic and empowering. Personally, I love to listen to rap and hip hop music with lyrics that motivate me to push harder and lift heavier. Some of my favorite artists to listen to during a strength training workout are Eminem, Drake, and Kendrick Lamar.

Other popular genres for strength training include heavy metal, hard rock, and electronic dance music. The key is to find music that pumps you up and gives you the confidence to tackle your workout.
