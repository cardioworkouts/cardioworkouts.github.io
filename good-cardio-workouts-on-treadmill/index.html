Good Cardio Workouts On Treadmill A Beginner s Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 02, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for an effective way to improve your cardiovascular health? Treadmill workout is one of the easiest ways to get started with cardio exercises. However, if you're new to treadmill workouts, it can be challenging to know where to start. In this guide, we'll show you some of the best cardio workouts on a treadmill that can help you achieve your fitness goals.

Many people struggle with finding good cardio workouts on a treadmill that are both challenging and enjoyable. It can be frustrating to spend hours on a treadmill without seeing any results. The key to an effective cardio workout on a treadmill is to find a routine that works for you.

Interval Training

Interval training is an excellent cardio workout on a treadmill that can help you burn calories and build endurance. It involves alternating between high-intensity exercise and low-intensity recovery periods. This type of training is perfect for people who want to challenge themselves and see results quickly.

One of the most popular interval training workouts is the "Tabata" method. This involves doing 20 seconds of intense exercise followed by 10 seconds of rest for a total of eight rounds. You can do this with running, jogging, or walking on a treadmill.

Walking Uphill

Walking uphill on a treadmill is an excellent cardio workout that is low-impact and easy on your joints. It's also a great way to tone your legs and glutes. Start by setting the incline to a moderate level and walking for about 10 minutes. Then, increase the incline every couple of minutes until you reach a challenging level.

You can also try adding weights to your workout to increase the intensity. Hold a pair of dumbbells while walking uphill to challenge your upper body muscles and burn more calories.

Running at an Incline

Running at an incline is an excellent cardio workout that can help you burn calories and build endurance. It's a great way to simulate outdoor running while staying indoors. Start by setting the incline to a moderate level and running for about 10 minutes. Then, increase the incline every couple of minutes until you reach a challenging level.

Running at an incline can be challenging, so it's essential to listen to your body and take breaks when needed. You can also try alternating between running and walking to build endurance gradually.

HIIT Workout

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) is a popular cardio workout that involves short bursts of high-intensity exercise followed by low-intensity recovery periods. It's an excellent way to burn calories and improve your cardiovascular health. You can do this workout on a treadmill by alternating between running and walking at a high intensity.

Start by warming up for about five minutes, then run at a high intensity for 30 seconds, followed by 90 seconds of walking at a moderate pace. Repeat this cycle for 15-20 minutes, then cool down for five minutes.

Conclusion of Good Cardio Workouts on Treadmill

There are many good cardio workouts on a treadmill that can help you achieve your fitness goals. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced runner, there is a routine out there that is perfect for you.
