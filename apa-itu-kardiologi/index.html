Coronary Artery Disease

Coronary artery disease occurs when the blood vessels that supply blood to the heart become narrow or blocked. This can lead to chest pain, shortness of breath, and other symptoms. Treatment for coronary artery disease may include lifestyle changes, medication, or surgery.

Heart Attacks

A heart attack occurs when blood flow to the heart is blocked, causing damage to the heart muscle. This can lead to chest pain, shortness of breath, and other symptoms. Treatment for a heart attack may include medication, surgery, or other interventions.
