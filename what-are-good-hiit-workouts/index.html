What Are Good Hiit Workouts A Beginner s Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your usual workout routine and looking for a way to spice things up? Have you heard about HIIT workouts but don't know where to start? If so, you're in the right place! In this blog post, we'll explore what HIIT workouts are, why they're effective, and provide some examples of good HIIT workouts you can try.

Working out can be challenging, especially when you feel like you're not seeing any progress. It's easy to get stuck in a rut and lose motivation. That's where HIIT workouts come in. By incorporating high-intensity intervals into your routine, you can increase your heart rate, burn more calories, and see results faster. However, it can be overwhelming to know where to begin or which workouts are the most effective.

What Are Good HIIT Workouts?

HIIT, or high-intensity interval training, is a type of workout that alternates between periods of intense activity and brief rest periods. The goal is to get your heart rate up and keep it there for a short amount of time. The beauty of HIIT workouts is that they can be done with just your body weight or minimal equipment, making them accessible to anyone, anywhere.

So, what are good HIIT workouts? Here are some examples:

1. Tabata

Tabata is a type of HIIT workout that involves 20 seconds of intense exercise followed by 10 seconds of rest, repeated for a total of four minutes. You can choose any exercise you like, such as squats, lunges, or push-ups. The key is to push yourself as hard as you can during the 20-second interval.

2. Sprint Intervals

If you're a fan of running, sprint intervals are a great HIIT workout. Simply alternate between sprinting and walking (or jogging) for a set amount of time, such as 30 seconds on, 30 seconds off. You can do this on a treadmill, a track, or in a park.

3. Bodyweight Circuits

A bodyweight circuit involves doing a series of exercises back-to-back with little to no rest in between. You can choose any exercises you like, such as burpees, mountain climbers, or jumping jacks. The key is to keep moving and challenge yourself.

4. Jump Rope

Jumping rope is a great cardio workout that can easily be turned into a HIIT workout. Simply jump as fast as you can for a set amount of time, such as 30 seconds, followed by a brief rest period. Repeat for several rounds.

How to Get Started with HIIT Workouts

If you're new to HIIT workouts, it's important to start slow and gradually increase the intensity. Begin with just a few intervals and work your way up. It's also important to listen to your body and take breaks when needed. Remember, HIIT workouts are supposed to be challenging, but they shouldn't be so difficult that you can't complete them safely.

Benefits of HIIT Workouts

There are many benefits to incorporating HIIT workouts into your routine, including:

  • Burns more calories in less time
  • Increases cardiovascular endurance
  • Improves insulin sensitivity
  • Boosts metabolism
  • Can be done anywhere, anytime

Mistakes to Avoid

When it comes to HIIT workouts, there are a few common mistakes to avoid:

  • Not warming up properly
  • Pushing too hard too soon
  • Skipping the rest periods
  • Doing the same workout every time

FAQs

Q: How often should I do HIIT workouts?

A: It's recommended to do HIIT workouts 2-3 times per week, with rest days in between. This will give your body time to recover and prevent injury.

Q: Do I need any equipment for HIIT workouts?

A: No, you don't need any equipment. Many HIIT workouts can be done with just your body weight. However, some workouts may require equipment such as dumbbells or a jump rope.

Q: How long should a HIIT workout be?

A: A typical HIIT workout can be anywhere from 10-30 minutes, depending on your fitness level and the exercises you choose. The key is to keep the intensity high and the rest periods short.

Q: Can anyone do HIIT workouts?

A: Yes, anyone can do HIIT workouts. However, if you have any underlying health conditions or injuries, it's important to consult with your doctor first.

Conclusion

Now that you know what are good HIIT workouts and how to get started, it's time to put these tips into action. Remember, HIIT workouts are a great way to add variety to your routine, burn more calories, and see results faster. Just be sure to start slow, listen to your body, and have fun!
