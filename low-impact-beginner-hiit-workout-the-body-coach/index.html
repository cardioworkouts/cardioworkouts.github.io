How to Do Low Impact Beginner HIIT Workout: The Body Coach?

The Low Impact Beginner HIIT Workout by The Body Coach is a 20-minute workout that includes six exercises that are performed for 30 seconds each, followed by a 30-second rest. The workout requires no equipment and can be done in the comfort of your own home.

Here is how to do the Low Impact Beginner HIIT Workout by The Body Coach:

1. Knee Tap

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and your arms by your sides. Lift your right knee up and tap it with your left hand. Lower your leg and repeat on the other side.

2. Lunge Tap

Stand with your feet hip-width apart and your arms by your sides. Take a step forward with your right foot and lower your body into a lunge. Tap your left hand on the ground beside your right foot. Return to the starting position and repeat on the other side.

3. Knee Drive

Stand with your feet hip-width apart and your arms by your sides. Lift your right knee up and drive it towards your chest. Lower your leg and repeat on the other side.

4. Step Tap

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and your arms by your sides. Take a step to the right with your right foot and tap your left foot beside it. Return to the starting position and repeat on the other side.

5. Squat Tap

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and your arms by your sides. Lower your body into a squat and tap your left hand on the ground. Return to the starting position and repeat on the other side.

6. Marching on the Spot

Stand with your feet hip-width apart and your arms by your sides. Lift your right knee up and lower it. Repeat on the other side, alternating legs.

Repeat these exercises for 20 minutes, performing each exercise for 30 seconds and taking a 30-second rest between each exercise.
