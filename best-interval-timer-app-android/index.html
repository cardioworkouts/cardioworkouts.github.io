Free Best Interval Timer App Android For Advanced Weight Training

Written by Oliver Aug 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Looking for the best interval timer app for Android? Look no further! We've compiled a list of the top interval timer apps for Android to help you find the perfect one for your needs.

Pain Points of Interval Timer Apps

Interval training is a popular way to work out because it can help you burn more calories and improve your cardiovascular fitness. But it can be difficult to keep track of your intervals without a timer. Traditional timers can be unreliable, and using your phone's built-in timer can be a hassle. That's where interval timer apps come in. With the right app, you can easily set up your intervals and focus on your workout without worrying about the clock.

What is the Best Interval Timer App for Android?

The best interval timer app for Android depends on your needs. Some apps are designed specifically for high-intensity interval training (HIIT), while others are more customizable. Here are some of the top interval timer apps for Android:

1. Seconds Pro

Seconds Pro is a popular interval timer app for Android that allows you to fully customize your intervals. You can set up intervals for any type of workout, including Tabata, HIIT, and circuit training. The app also has a built-in music player, so you can listen to your favorite workout tunes while you exercise.

2. Interval Timer

Interval Timer is a simple but effective interval timer app for Android. It allows you to set up intervals of any length and customize the number of rounds. You can also save your favorite intervals for future use.

3. Tabata Timer

Tabata Timer is a popular interval timer app for Android that is specifically designed for Tabata workouts. The app allows you to set up Tabata intervals of 20 seconds of work and 10 seconds of rest. You can also customize the number of rounds and rest periods.

4. GymNext Flex Timer

GymNext Flex Timer is a versatile interval timer app for Android that allows you to create custom timers for any type of workout. You can set up intervals for Tabata, HIIT, circuit training, and more. The app also has a built-in voice coach to guide you through your workout.

My Personal Experience with Interval Timer Apps

As someone who enjoys HIIT workouts, I've tried several interval timer apps for Android. My favorite is Seconds Pro because of its customization options. I can set up intervals for any type of workout and save my favorite intervals for future use. The built-in music player is also a nice touch.

How to Use an Interval Timer App

Using an interval timer app is easy. Simply download the app of your choice from the Google Play Store, set up your intervals, and start your workout. Most interval timer apps allow you to save your favorite intervals for future use, so you don't have to set them up every time you work out.

Here are some tips for using an interval timer app:

- Choose an app that allows you to fully customize your intervals
- Save your favorite intervals for future use
- Use the built-in music player to keep yourself motivated
- Set up a rest period between rounds to give yourself time to recover
- Use the app's voice coach to guide you through your workout

FAQs About Interval Timer Apps

1. Can I use an interval timer app for any type of workout?

Yes, most interval timer apps allow you to fully customize your intervals for any type of workout.

2. Can I save my favorite intervals for future use?

Yes, most interval timer apps allow you to save your favorite intervals for future use.

3. Do I need to use a voice coach?

No, you don't have to use a voice coach, but it can be helpful for keeping you motivated and on track.

4. Can I use an interval timer app for Tabata workouts?

Yes, there are several interval timer apps specifically designed for Tabata workouts.

Conclusion of Best Interval Timer App for Android

Interval timer apps can make your workouts more effective and enjoyable. Whether you're doing HIIT, Tabata, or circuit training, there's an app out there that can help you stay on track. With the right app, you can focus on your workout and let the timer do the rest.
