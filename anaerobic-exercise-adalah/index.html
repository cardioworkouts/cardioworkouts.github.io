What is Anaerobic Exercise Adalah?

Anaerobic exercise adalah a type of physical activity that requires high levels of energy and intensity. It is characterized by short bursts of activity that are performed at maximum effort, typically lasting no more than two minutes. This type of exercise does not rely on oxygen to sustain energy, which is why it is called "anaerobic."

Examples of anaerobic exercise include weight lifting, sprinting, and high-intensity interval training (HIIT). These activities require a lot of energy and can help you build muscle, increase your metabolism, and improve your overall fitness level.
