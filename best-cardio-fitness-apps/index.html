Best Cardio Fitness Apps For Burning Calories And Staying Fit

Written by Christine Aug 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you struggle with finding the motivation to hit the gym or go for a run? Are you tired of the same old boring cardio routines? Look no further than your smartphone for a variety of cardio fitness apps to spice up your workouts and keep you on track towards your fitness goals.

Whether you are a beginner or an experienced athlete, cardio fitness apps can help you stay motivated, track your progress, and challenge yourself in new ways. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the best cardio fitness app for your needs.

Here, we have rounded up the top cardio fitness apps to help you find the perfect fit for your fitness journey.

1. Nike Training Club

Target: Best cardio fitness app for at-home workouts and beginners.

As a beginner, it can be difficult to know where to start with a fitness routine. Nike Training Club offers a variety of guided workouts for all levels, including cardio, strength training, and yoga. The app also provides personalized workout plans based on your fitness goals and tracks your progress over time. Plus, it's free!

2. Aaptiv

Target: Best cardio fitness app for audio-based workouts.

If you prefer audio-guided workouts, Aaptiv might be the perfect cardio fitness app for you. With over 2,500 on-demand workouts led by certified personal trainers, Aaptiv offers a variety of cardio workouts, including running, cycling, and elliptical. The app also allows you to filter workouts by duration, difficulty level, and music genre to keep your workouts fresh and exciting.

3. MyFitnessPal

Target: Best cardio fitness app for tracking your nutrition and workouts.

While not specifically a cardio fitness app, MyFitnessPal is a great tool for tracking your nutrition and workouts. By logging your meals and exercises, you can stay accountable and see how your diet and exercise habits are affecting your progress. You can also connect with friends and join challenges for added motivation.

4. Strava

Target: Best cardio fitness app for runners and cyclists.

Strava is a popular app among runners and cyclists for tracking their workouts and connecting with other athletes. The app uses GPS to track your route, distance, and pace, and provides detailed analytics to help you improve your performance. You can also join challenges and compete with other athletes to push yourself to the next level.

5. Zombies, Run!

Target: Best cardio fitness app for adding fun and excitement to your workouts.

If you struggle with finding the motivation to run, Zombies, Run! might be the perfect cardio fitness app for you. The app adds a fun twist to your runs by immersing you in a zombie apocalypse story. As you run, you collect supplies and complete missions to help your virtual community survive. The app also includes interval training workouts to help you improve your speed and endurance.

Question and Answer:

Q: Are these apps only for cardio workouts?

A: No, these apps offer a variety of workouts including cardio, strength training, and yoga.

Q: Are these apps free?

A: Some of the apps offer a free version, but others require a subscription fee for access to all features.

Q: Can I connect with friends on these apps?

A: Yes, many of the apps allow you to connect with friends for added motivation and accountability.

Q: Can I use these apps if I don't have a gym membership?

A: Yes, all of these apps offer at-home workouts that do not require gym equipment.

Conclusion of Best Cardio Fitness Apps

With so many cardio fitness apps available, there is something for everyone to help them achieve their fitness goals. Whether you prefer audio-guided workouts, tracking your nutrition, or adding a fun twist to your runs, these apps can help you stay motivated, track your progress, and challenge yourself in new ways. Try out a few of these apps to find the perfect fit for your fitness journey.
