Cardio Exercises: Target and Personal Experience

Cardio exercises are any type of exercise that raises your heart rate and breathing rate, such as jogging, cycling, or swimming. The target of cardio exercises is to improve your cardiovascular health by strengthening your heart and lungs. Personally, I enjoy going for a run in the morning to get my heart pumping and start my day off on the right foot.

Strength Exercises: Target and Personal Experience

Strength exercises are any type of exercise that uses resistance, such as weights or resistance bands, to build and maintain muscle mass and bone density. The target of strength exercises is to improve your overall strength and endurance. Personally, I enjoy doing squats and lunges with weights to build up my leg muscles.

Flexibility Exercises: Target and Explanation

Flexibility exercises are any type of exercise that helps improve your range of motion and reduce the risk of injury, such as stretching or yoga. The target of flexibility exercises is to improve your flexibility and mobility. By incorporating stretching into your routine, you can improve your posture, reduce muscle tension, and prevent injuries.
