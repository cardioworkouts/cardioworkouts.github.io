What To Eat Before Bodybuilding Competition A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a bodybuilder gearing up for a competition? Do you want to make sure you're fueling your body with the right foods to perform at your best? Look no further. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about what to eat before bodybuilding competition.

Why Is What You Eat Before a Bodybuilding Competition Important?

As a bodybuilder, you know that what you eat plays a huge role in your performance. It's essential to fuel your body with the right nutrients to ensure you have the energy and strength to perform at your best on stage. Eating the right foods before a competition can also help reduce muscle fatigue and soreness, improve your focus and concentration, and enhance your overall performance.

What to Eat Before a Bodybuilding Competition

When it comes to what to eat before bodybuilding competition, it's essential to focus on foods that are high in protein, healthy fats, and complex carbohydrates. Here are some examples of foods to include in your pre-competition meal:

Protein:

Protein is essential for building and repairing muscles, making it a crucial nutrient for bodybuilders. Some good sources of protein to include in your pre-competition meal include:

  • Chicken breast
  • Salmon
  • Egg whites
  • Lean beef
  • Whey protein powder

Healthy Fats:

Healthy fats are essential for providing your body with sustained energy and helping to reduce inflammation. Some good sources of healthy fats to include in your pre-competition meal include:

  • Avocado
  • Nuts and seeds
  • Olive oil
  • Coconut oil
  • Fatty fish, such as salmon or tuna

Complex Carbohydrates:

Complex carbohydrates are an excellent source of energy and can help keep you feeling full and satisfied throughout your competition. Some good sources of complex carbohydrates to include in your pre-competition meal include:

  • Brown rice
  • Quinoa
  • Sweet potatoes
  • Oatmeal
  • Whole-grain bread

Personal Experience: What I Eat Before a Bodybuilding Competition

As a bodybuilder, I've found that eating a balanced meal that includes protein, healthy fats, and complex carbohydrates is essential for performing at my best on stage. For my pre-competition meal, I usually have a grilled chicken breast with a side of sweet potato and some steamed broccoli. I also like to have a small green salad with some olive oil and vinegar dressing on the side.

Hydration Before a Bodybuilding Competition

Staying hydrated is just as important as eating the right foods before a bodybuilding competition. Make sure to drink plenty of water in the days leading up to your competition to ensure your body is hydrated. On the day of your competition, continue to drink water, but be careful not to drink too much right before you go on stage, as this can cause you to feel bloated.

Supplements to Consider Before a Bodybuilding Competition

While it's essential to focus on eating whole, nutrient-dense foods before a bodybuilding competition, there are some supplements you may want to consider to help enhance your performance. Some supplements that may be beneficial include:

  • Creatine
  • Beta-alanine
  • Caffeine
  • Branched-chain amino acids (BCAAs)

Question and Answer

Q: Should I eat a lot of carbs before a bodybuilding competition?

A: Yes, it's essential to include complex carbohydrates in your pre-competition meal to ensure you have sustained energy throughout your performance.

Q: Can I drink coffee before a bodybuilding competition?

A: Yes, caffeine can help improve your focus and performance, but be careful not to drink too much, as this can cause jitters or anxiety.

Q: How much water should I drink before a bodybuilding competition?

A: It's essential to stay hydrated in the days leading up to your competition, but be careful not to drink too much right before you go on stage, as this can cause you to feel bloated. Aim to drink 8-10 glasses of water per day leading up to your competition.

Q: Should I eat a lot of protein before a bodybuilding competition?

A: Yes, protein is essential for building and repairing muscles, making it a crucial nutrient for bodybuilders. Aim to include a good source of protein in your pre-competition meal.

Conclusion of What to Eat Before Bodybuilding Competition

What you eat before a bodybuilding competition is crucial for ensuring you have the energy, strength, and focus to perform at your best on stage. Be sure to include a balance of protein, healthy fats, and complex carbohydrates in your pre-competition meal, and stay hydrated in the days leading up to your competition. By following these tips, you'll be well on your way to crushing your next bodybuilding competition.
