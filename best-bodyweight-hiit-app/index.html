The Best Bodyweight Hiit App Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Sep 14, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking for an effective workout routine that doesn't involve any equipment? Do you want to burn fat and build muscle in a short amount of time? If so, you need the best bodyweight HIIT app.

When it comes to working out, everyone has their own preferences. Some people prefer lifting weights, while others prefer running. However, bodyweight HIIT workouts have become increasingly popular in recent years, as they offer numerous benefits, including increased cardiovascular endurance, improved metabolism, and decreased risk of injury. But with so many apps available, how do you choose the best one for your needs?

Before we dive into the best bodyweight HIIT app options, let's first define what HIIT is and what it can do for you. HIIT stands for High-Intensity Interval Training, which involves short bursts of intense exercise followed by a period of rest or low-intensity exercise. This type of training has been shown to be effective in burning fat, improving cardiovascular health, and boosting metabolism. By choosing a bodyweight HIIT app, you can enjoy all these benefits from the comfort of your own home.

In this article, we will share the best bodyweight HIIT app available in the market and explore their features, pros, and cons. We will also provide you with tips on how to choose the right app for your goals and fitness level.

The Best Bodyweight HIIT App: 7 Minute Workout

As the name suggests, 7 Minute Workout is an app that provides quick, high-intensity workouts that can be done in just seven minutes. The app offers a range of bodyweight exercises that target different muscle groups, including push-ups, squats, and lunges. The app also allows you to customize your workout to suit your fitness level and goals.

I have been using 7 Minute Workout for the past six months, and I have seen significant improvements in my fitness level. The app is user-friendly, and the workouts are challenging but not overwhelming. I also appreciate the fact that I can do the workouts anywhere, anytime, without any equipment.

Runtastic Results

Runtastic Results is another popular bodyweight HIIT app that offers personalized workout plans based on your fitness level and goals. The app features a variety of exercises, including bodyweight strength training, high-intensity cardio, and stretching. The app also provides video demonstrations and audio cues to guide you through each exercise.

I started using Runtastic Results a few months ago, and I have been impressed with the app's features and functionality. The workouts are challenging, but the app provides modifications for beginners. I also appreciate the fact that the app tracks my progress and adjusts my workouts accordingly.

Freeletics

Freeletics is a bodyweight HIIT app that offers personalized workout plans that can be done anywhere, anytime. The app features a range of exercises, including bodyweight strength training, cardio, and stretching. The app also provides personalized coaching and nutrition advice.

Although I have not personally used Freeletics, I have heard positive feedback from friends who have used the app. They appreciate the app's personalized approach and the fact that the app provides a range of exercises to target different muscle groups.

How to Choose the Right Bodyweight HIIT App for You

Choosing the right bodyweight HIIT app can be overwhelming, but there are a few things you can consider to make the process easier. First, think about your fitness level and goals. If you are a beginner, look for an app that provides modifications and progressions. If you are more advanced, look for an app that provides challenging workouts.

Second, consider the app's features and functionality. Look for an app that provides video demonstrations, audio cues, and tracking features. Also, consider the app's user interface and ease of use.

Finally, read reviews and ask for recommendations from friends or fitness professionals. This will give you an idea of the app's effectiveness and suitability for your goals.

Conclusion of Best Bodyweight HIIT App

In conclusion, a bodyweight HIIT app can be an effective and convenient way to get in shape and improve your overall health. The best bodyweight HIIT app for you will depend on your fitness level, goals, and personal preferences. By considering the features and functionality of each app and reading reviews and recommendations, you can find the right app to help you achieve your fitness goals.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I do bodyweight HIIT workouts every day?

A: It's not recommended to do high-intensity workouts every day, as your body needs time to recover. Aim to do bodyweight HIIT workouts two to three times a week, and incorporate other types of exercise on the other days.

Q: Can bodyweight HIIT workouts help me lose weight?

A: Yes, bodyweight HIIT workouts can be effective in burning fat and helping you lose weight. However, it's important to combine exercise with a healthy diet for optimal results.

Q: Do I need any equipment for bodyweight HIIT workouts?

A: No, bodyweight HIIT workouts can be done without any equipment. However, some apps may recommend using resistance bands or dumbbells for added resistance.

Q: How long should a bodyweight HIIT workout be?

A: A bodyweight HIIT workout can be as short as seven minutes or as long as 30 minutes, depending on your fitness level and goals. Aim to do at least 20 minutes of high-intensity exercise to reap the benefits of HIIT.
