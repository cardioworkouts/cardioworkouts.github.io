Best Free Cardio Apps For Iphone A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 19, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking for the best free cardio apps for iPhone that can help you stay fit and healthy? Do you want to burn calories and improve your overall health, but don't have the time or money to join a gym? If so, then you're in luck - there are plenty of free cardio apps available for iPhone that can help you achieve your fitness goals.

Cardio exercises are essential for maintaining a healthy heart and lungs, burning calories, and improving overall fitness. However, it can be challenging to find the right cardio workout that suits your needs and fitness level. This is where cardio apps come in handy. These apps offer a variety of workouts that cater to different fitness levels and interests, making it easier for you to find the right workout that suits your needs.

In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best free cardio apps for iPhone that you can use to stay fit and healthy. We've tested and reviewed several apps, and we've narrowed down the list to the top five that we believe are the best.

Here are our top picks for the best free cardio apps for iPhone:

1. Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club is a free app that offers a variety of cardio workouts, including running, cycling, and high-intensity interval training (HIIT). The app also offers personalized training plans that cater to your fitness level and goals. It features audio and video guidance from Nike trainers and allows you to track your progress and share your achievements with friends.

I personally use Nike Training Club and find it to be an excellent app for cardio workouts. The app is user-friendly, and the workouts are challenging but doable. I love the fact that it offers personalized training plans that cater to my fitness level and goals.

2. Runtastic

Runtastic is a free app that offers a variety of cardio workouts, including running, cycling, and walking. It features audio coaching, GPS tracking, and personalized music playlists. The app also allows you to track your progress and share your achievements with friends.

I've used Runtastic in the past and found it to be an excellent app for running and cycling workouts. The audio coaching is a nice touch, and I love the fact that it allows you to create personalized music playlists.

3. FitOn

FitOn is a free app that offers a variety of cardio workouts, including dance, HIIT, and yoga. The app features audio and video guidance from celebrity trainers and allows you to track your progress and share your achievements with friends. It also offers personalized training plans that cater to your fitness level and goals.

I've used FitOn for dance and yoga workouts and found it to be an excellent app for beginners. The trainers are engaging, and the workouts are challenging but doable.

4. 7 Minute Workout

7 Minute Workout is a free app that offers quick and effective cardio workouts that can be done in just seven minutes. The app features audio and video guidance and allows you to track your progress and share your achievements with friends.

I've used 7 Minute Workout for days when I don't have much time to exercise, and I found the workouts to be quick but effective. The app is user-friendly, and the audio and video guidance is helpful.

5. Daily Burn

Daily Burn is a free app that offers a variety of cardio workouts, including running, cycling, and yoga. The app features audio and video guidance from certified trainers and allows you to track your progress and share your achievements with friends. It also offers personalized training plans that cater to your fitness level and goals.

I've used Daily Burn for yoga workouts and found the app to be an excellent resource for beginners. The trainers are knowledgeable, and the workouts are challenging but doable.

Conclusion of Best Free Cardio Apps for iPhone

In conclusion, there are many excellent free cardio apps available for iPhone that can help you stay fit and healthy. Whether you're looking for running, cycling, dance, yoga, or HIIT workouts, there's an app out there that can cater to your needs and fitness level. So, download one of the apps mentioned above, and start your cardio journey today!

Question and Answer

Q1. Are all cardio apps for iPhone free?

A1. No, not all cardio apps for iPhone are free. Some apps offer a free version with limited features, while others require a paid subscription to access all features.

Q2. Can cardio apps for iPhone track my progress?

A2. Yes, most cardio apps for iPhone allow you to track your progress and share your achievements with friends.

Q3. Are cardio apps for iPhone suitable for beginners?

A3. Yes, most cardio apps for iPhone offer workouts that cater to beginners. Some apps even offer personalized training plans that cater to your fitness level and goals.

Q4. Can I use cardio apps for iPhone without internet?

A4. It depends on the app. Some apps require an internet connection to access workouts, while others allow you to download workouts for offline use.
