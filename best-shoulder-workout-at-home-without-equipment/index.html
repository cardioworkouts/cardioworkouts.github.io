Best Shoulder Workout At Home Without Equipment

Written by Robby Oct 30, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for the best shoulder workout that you can do at home without any equipment? Do you want to build strong and toned shoulders but don't have access to a gym or weights? Look no further because we have got you covered!

Having strong and healthy shoulders is essential for overall upper body strength and posture. However, not everyone has the time or resources to hit the gym regularly. But don't worry, you can still achieve your fitness goals with these effective shoulder exercises that you can do at home without any equipment.

The best shoulder workout at home without equipment includes exercises that target your shoulders, upper back, and arms. These exercises not only help you build shoulder strength and stability but also improve your posture and reduce the risk of injury.

In this article, we will discuss the best shoulder workout at home without equipment and related keywords to help you achieve your fitness goals.

Shoulder Taps

Shoulder taps are a great exercise that targets your shoulders and also engages your core muscles. To perform this exercise, start in a high plank position with your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart. Keeping your core engaged, lift your right hand and tap your left shoulder. Return to the starting position and repeat with your left hand tapping your right shoulder. This completes one rep. Do 3 sets of 10 reps.

Pike Push-Ups

Pike push-ups are another great exercise that targets your shoulders and also engages your triceps and chest muscles. To perform this exercise, start in a downward dog position with your hands and feet on the ground and your hips lifted. Lower your head towards the ground by bending your elbows and then push back up to the starting position. Do 3 sets of 10 reps.

Plank Shoulder Taps

Plank shoulder taps are a variation of shoulder taps that add more challenge to your core muscles. To perform this exercise, start in a high plank position with your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart. Keeping your core engaged, lift your right hand and tap your left shoulder. Return to the starting position and repeat with your left hand tapping your right shoulder. This completes one rep. Do 3 sets of 10 reps.

Handstand Push-Ups

If you are looking for a more challenging exercise that targets your shoulders, then handstand push-ups are perfect for you. To perform this exercise, start in a handstand position against a wall. Lower your head towards the ground by bending your elbows and then push back up to the starting position. Do 3 sets of 10 reps.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I do these exercises every day?

A: It is recommended to give your muscles time to rest and recover. You can do these exercises every other day.

Q: Can I modify these exercises if I am a beginner?

A: Yes, you can modify these exercises by doing them on your knees or against a wall until you build enough strength to perform the full exercise.

Q: How long should I hold the plank position?

A: You can hold the plank position for 30 seconds to 1 minute, depending on your fitness level.

Q: Can these exercises help me reduce shoulder pain?

A: These exercises can help improve shoulder stability and reduce the risk of injury but if you have any existing shoulder pain, it is recommended to consult a doctor before performing any exercises.

Conclusion of Best Shoulder Workout at Home Without Equipment

Building strong and healthy shoulders is essential for overall upper body strength and posture. With these effective shoulder exercises that you can do at home without any equipment, you can achieve your fitness goals and improve your overall health and wellbeing. Remember to start with modifications if needed and always listen to your body. Happy exercising!
