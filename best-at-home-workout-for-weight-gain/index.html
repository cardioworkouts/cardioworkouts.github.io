Best At Home Workout For Weight Gain

Written by Frank Oct 07, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of being underweight and want to gain some muscle mass? Do you want to avoid going to the gym and prefer to workout in the comfort of your own home? If so, you are in the right place. In this article, we will discuss the best at home workout for weight gain that will help you achieve your fitness goals.

Being underweight can be a frustrating experience. It can lead to a lack of confidence, low self-esteem, and even health problems. Many people struggle to gain weight and build muscle, especially without access to a gym. However, with the right exercises and workout routine, you can achieve your weight gain goals from the comfort of your own home.

The best at home workout for weight gain includes a combination of strength training exercises and a balanced diet. You will need to focus on exercises that target multiple muscle groups and increase your overall muscle mass. Additionally, you will need to consume more calories than you burn to gain weight and build muscle.

In summary, the best at home workout for weight gain involves a combination of strength training exercises and a balanced diet. The key is to focus on exercises that target multiple muscle groups and consume more calories than you burn to gain weight and build muscle.

Bodyweight Exercises

Bodyweight exercises are a great way to gain muscle mass and increase strength without the need for equipment. These exercises use your own body weight as resistance to build muscle and can be done anywhere, anytime. One of the best bodyweight exercises for weight gain is the push-up. Push-ups target your chest, shoulders, and triceps, and can be modified to make them more challenging.

Start with a standard push-up and gradually increase the number of reps and sets. Once you can do 3 sets of 10 reps, try a more challenging variation such as a decline push-up or a diamond push-up. You can also add weight to your push-ups by wearing a weighted vest or using resistance bands.

Dumbbell Exercises

Dumbbells are a great piece of equipment to have at home for weight gain. They are versatile and can be used for a variety of exercises that target multiple muscle groups. One of the best dumbbell exercises for weight gain is the dumbbell bench press. The bench press targets your chest, shoulders, and triceps, and can be done on a flat bench or an incline bench.

Start with a weight that you can comfortably lift for 3 sets of 10 reps. Gradually increase the weight as you get stronger. You can also try other dumbbell exercises such as bicep curls, tricep extensions, and shoulder presses.

Resistance Band Exercises

Resistance bands are another great piece of equipment to have at home for weight gain. They are lightweight, portable, and can be used for a variety of exercises that target multiple muscle groups. One of the best resistance band exercises for weight gain is the resistance band squat. The squat targets your legs, glutes, and core, and can be modified to make it more challenging.

Start with a resistance band that provides enough resistance for you to feel the burn after 3 sets of 10 reps. Gradually increase the resistance as you get stronger. You can also try other resistance band exercises such as bicep curls, tricep extensions, and shoulder presses.

Calisthenics Exercises

Calisthenics exercises are bodyweight exercises that focus on using your own bodyweight as resistance to build muscle. They are a great way to gain muscle mass and increase strength without the need for equipment. One of the best calisthenics exercises for weight gain is the pull-up. Pull-ups target your back, biceps, and forearms, and can be modified to make them more challenging.

Start with a standard pull-up and gradually increase the number of reps and sets. Once you can do 3 sets of 10 reps, try a more challenging variation such as a wide grip pull-up or a muscle-up. You can also add weight to your pull-ups by wearing a weighted vest or using a dip belt.

Question and Answer

Q: How many times a week should I do these exercises?

A: Aim to do these exercises at least 3 times a week, with a rest day in between each workout.

Q: Do I need to lift heavy weights to gain muscle mass?

A: No, you do not necessarily need to lift heavy weights to gain muscle mass. Bodyweight exercises, dumbbell exercises, resistance band exercises, and calisthenics exercises can all be effective for gaining muscle mass.

Q: What should I eat to support weight gain?

A: To support weight gain, you should focus on consuming more calories than you burn. This means eating a balanced diet that includes protein, carbohydrates, and healthy fats. Aim to eat at least 3 meals a day, and consider adding snacks in between meals.

Q: How long will it take to see results?

A: The amount of time it takes to see results will vary depending on your starting point and how consistent you are with your workouts and diet. Stick with it and be patient, and you will eventually see results.

Conclusion of Best At Home Workout for Weight Gain

The best at home workout for weight gain involves a combination of strength training exercises and a balanced diet. Bodyweight exercises, dumbbell exercises, resistance band exercises, and calisthenics exercises are all effective for gaining muscle mass. Remember to focus on exercises that target multiple muscle groups and consume more calories than you burn to gain weight and build muscle. With consistency and dedication, you can achieve your weight gain goals from the comfort of your own home.
