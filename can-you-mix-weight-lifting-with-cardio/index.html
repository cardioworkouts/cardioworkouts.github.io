Can You Mix Weight Lifting With Cardio A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jun 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a fitness enthusiast? Do you want to know if you can combine weight lifting with cardio? If yes, then you are in the right place. Here, we are going to discuss everything related to mixing weight lifting with cardio.

Many people have this misconception that weight lifting and cardio should be performed separately. They believe that weight lifting is for building muscle, and cardio is for burning fat. However, the truth is that you can mix weight lifting with cardio, and it can help you achieve your fitness goals faster.

The answer to the question, "Can you mix weight lifting with cardio?" is yes. In fact, mixing weight lifting with cardio is highly recommended by fitness experts as it provides numerous benefits to your body.

Combining weight lifting with cardio helps you burn more calories, lose fat, and build muscle simultaneously. Moreover, it increases your endurance, improves your heart health, and reduces the risk of various diseases.

My Personal Experience with Mixing Weight Lifting with Cardio

Personally, I have been combining weight lifting with cardio for a few years now, and I have seen significant changes in my body. I used to perform cardio separately, but I felt like I was not making much progress. However, since I started mixing cardio with weight lifting, I have seen great results.

I have lost fat, gained muscle, and improved my overall fitness level. Moreover, I feel more energetic and motivated to work out. Mixing weight lifting with cardio has made my workouts more challenging and fun.

The Benefits of Mixing Weight Lifting with Cardio

There are several benefits of mixing weight lifting with cardio. Here are some of them:

Burn More Calories

Combining weight lifting with cardio helps you burn more calories than performing them separately. Cardio burns calories during the workout, while weight lifting burns calories even after the workout. Therefore, mixing weight lifting with cardio helps you burn more calories overall.

Build Muscle and Lose Fat Simultaneously

Weight lifting helps you build muscle, while cardio helps you burn fat. By combining both, you can build muscle and lose fat simultaneously. This is because weight lifting helps you retain muscle while losing fat.

Improve Endurance

Mixing weight lifting with cardio helps you improve your endurance. Cardio improves your cardiovascular endurance, while weight lifting improves your muscular endurance. Therefore, combining both helps you improve your overall endurance.

Reduce the Risk of Diseases

Studies have shown that mixing weight lifting with cardio can reduce the risk of various diseases such as heart disease, diabetes, and obesity. It also helps you improve your overall health and well-being.

Question and Answer

Q. Can you mix weight lifting with cardio every day?

A. Yes, you can mix weight lifting with cardio every day. However, it is essential to give your body proper rest and recovery time to avoid injuries and fatigue.

Q. Should you do weight lifting or cardio first?

A. There is no specific order in which you should perform weight lifting and cardio. However, it is recommended to perform weight lifting first as it requires more energy and focus.

Q. How long should you do weight lifting and cardio?

A. The duration of weight lifting and cardio depends on your fitness level and goals. However, it is recommended to perform at least 30 minutes of cardio and 30 minutes of weight lifting for optimal results.

Q. Can you mix weight lifting with cardio if you are a beginner?

A. Yes, you can mix weight lifting with cardio even if you are a beginner. However, it is recommended to start slowly and gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts.

Conclusion of Can You Mix Weight Lifting with Cardio

Mixing weight lifting with cardio is an excellent way to improve your overall fitness level. It helps you burn more calories, lose fat, build muscle, and improve your endurance. Moreover, it reduces the risk of various diseases and improves your overall health and well-being. Therefore, if you want to achieve your fitness goals faster, then start mixing weight lifting with cardio today.
