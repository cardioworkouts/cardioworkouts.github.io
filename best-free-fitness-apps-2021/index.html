Best Free Fitness Apps 2021 Your Ultimate Guide To Staying Fit And Healthy

Written by Petter Oct 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you struggle to find the motivation to exercise? Are you looking for a way to track your progress and stay on track with your fitness goals? If so, then you're in luck because there are plenty of free fitness apps available in 2021 to help you achieve your health and fitness goals.

The target of this article is to provide you with an ultimate guide to the best free fitness apps 2021. We'll cover everything from the top-rated apps to the features they offer, and how to use them to your advantage.

In summary, the main points we'll discuss in this article include:

  • The best free fitness apps 2021
  • The target audience for each app
  • The features of each app
  • How to use the apps to stay fit and healthy

MyFitnessPal: The Ultimate Fitness App for Diet and Nutrition

MyFitnessPal is a popular fitness app that focuses on diet and nutrition. It has a large database of over 6 million foods, making it easy to track your calorie intake and macronutrient ratios. The app also allows you to set goals, track your progress, and connect with friends for added motivation.

Personally, I've been using MyFitnessPal for several years now, and I've found it to be an invaluable tool in my fitness journey. It's helped me to stay on track with my nutrition goals and has provided me with the motivation to keep going even when things get tough.

Fitbit: The Ultimate Fitness App for Tracking Activity and Sleep

Fitbit is a popular fitness app that focuses on tracking activity and sleep. It connects with Fitbit wearable devices to track steps, distance, calories burned, and sleep patterns. The app also allows you to set goals, track your progress, and compete with friends for added motivation.

Personally, I've been using Fitbit for several years now, and I've found it to be an excellent tool for tracking my daily activity and sleep patterns. It's helped me to stay accountable for my fitness goals and has motivated me to push myself to new levels.

Nike Training Club: The Ultimate Fitness App for Home Workouts

Nike Training Club is a popular fitness app that focuses on home workouts. It offers a wide variety of workouts, ranging from bodyweight exercises to yoga and HIIT. The app also allows you to set goals, track your progress, and connect with friends for added motivation.

Personally, I've found Nike Training Club to be an excellent tool for staying fit and healthy, even when I can't make it to the gym. The workouts are challenging but manageable, and the app provides me with the motivation to keep going.

C25K: The Ultimate Fitness App for Running

C25K is a popular fitness app that focuses on running. It's designed to help beginners go from couch to 5K in just eight weeks. The app provides audio cues to let you know when to run and when to walk, making it easy to follow along with the program.

Personally, I've found C25K to be an excellent tool for getting back into running after a long break. The app provides a structured program that takes the guesswork out of running, and the audio cues keep me on track.

Question and Answer Section

Q1: Are these fitness apps free to use?
A: Yes, all of the apps mentioned in this article are free to download and use. However, some of them offer premium features that require a subscription.

Q2: Can I use these fitness apps on my phone or tablet?
A: Yes, all of the apps mentioned in this article are available for both iOS and Android devices.

Q3: Do I need any special equipment to use these fitness apps?
A: No, most of the apps mentioned in this article can be used with little to no equipment. However, some of the apps may require specific equipment, such as a Fitbit wearable device.

Q4: Can I connect with friends on these fitness apps?
A: Yes, most of the apps mentioned in this article allow you to connect with friends for added motivation and support.

Conclusion of Best Free Fitness Apps 2021

Staying fit and healthy has never been easier, thanks to the wide variety of free fitness apps available in 2021. Whether you're looking to track your nutrition, activity, or sleep, there's an app out there that can help you achieve your goals. So why not give one (or all) of these apps a try and see how they can transform your fitness journey?
