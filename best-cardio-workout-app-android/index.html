Benefits of Cardio Exercises

Cardiovascular exercise is any activity that increases your heart rate and breathing rate. This type of exercise is great for improving your overall health and fitness, and can also help you lose weight and reduce your risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease and diabetes.

Cardio exercises also help to improve your mood and reduce stress levels. They release endorphins, which are natural mood-boosters, and can help to combat anxiety and depression.
