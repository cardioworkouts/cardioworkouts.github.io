Which Apple Watch Workout Burns The Most Calories A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for an effective workout that can help you burn the most calories? If you own an Apple Watch, you're in luck! The device comes with a range of workout options that can help you achieve your fitness goals.

However, with so many choices available, it can be overwhelming to decide which one to choose. In this post, we'll explore which Apple Watch workout burns the most calories and help you make an informed decision.

What is the Target of Which Apple Watch Workout Burns the Most Calories?

The target of this post is to help you identify the Apple Watch workout that can help you burn the most calories. We will provide you with an in-depth analysis of each workout option and its calorie-burning potential, so you can choose the one that aligns with your fitness goals.

In addition, we will also cover related keywords such as "Apple Watch workout," "calorie burn," and "fitness goals" to help improve the post's SEO.

My Personal Experience with Which Apple Watch Workout Burns the Most Calories

As a fitness enthusiast, I've tried several Apple Watch workouts to help me achieve my fitness goals. However, I've found that the High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) workout burns the most calories for me.

HIIT involves short bursts of intense exercise followed by a period of rest. This workout is perfect for people who have a busy schedule and want to maximize their calorie burn in a short amount of time. I usually do a 30-minute session, and I can burn up to 300 calories!

The Most Effective Apple Watch Workout for Burning Calories

The most effective Apple Watch workout for burning calories is the HIIT workout. This workout has been proven to burn more calories than any other workout option available on the Apple Watch.

A study conducted by the American Council on Exercise found that HIIT burns an average of 15-20 calories per minute, compared to running, which burns 10-11 calories per minute.

Moreover, HIIT can help you burn calories even after your workout is over. This is known as the "afterburn effect," where your body continues to burn calories for up to 24 hours after your workout.

The Benefits of HIIT

Aside from its calorie-burning potential, HIIT offers several other benefits, including:

  • Improved cardiovascular health
  • Increase in muscle mass
  • Boosted metabolism
  • Reduced insulin resistance

How to Perform a HIIT Workout

Performing a HIIT workout on your Apple Watch is straightforward. Here's how to do it:

  1. Open the "Workout" app on your Apple Watch
  2. Select "High-Intensity Interval Training"
  3. Choose your time and hit "Start"
  4. Follow the prompts and perform the exercises as instructed
  5. After the workout, review your results and track your progress

Other Apple Watch Workouts That Burn Calories

While HIIT is the most effective Apple Watch workout for burning calories, there are other options that can also help you achieve your fitness goals. Here are some other workouts that burn calories:

  • Running
  • Swimming
  • Cycling
  • Rowing

These workouts offer a range of benefits and can help you burn calories while improving your overall fitness level.

Question and Answer

Q: How many calories can I burn during a HIIT workout on my Apple Watch?

A: On average, you can burn 15-20 calories per minute during a HIIT workout on your Apple Watch.

Q: Is HIIT suitable for beginners?

A: HIIT can be challenging for beginners, but it can be modified to suit your fitness level. Start with shorter intervals and build your way up to longer and more intense sessions.

Q: Can I track my calorie burn during other workouts on my Apple Watch?

A: Yes, you can track your calorie burn during other workouts on your Apple Watch using the "Workout" app. Simply select the workout option that aligns with your fitness goals and hit "Start."

Q: Can I combine different workouts on my Apple Watch to burn more calories?

A: Yes, you can combine different workouts on your Apple Watch to burn more calories. The device allows you to create a custom workout that combines different exercises to suit your fitness goals.

Conclusion of Which Apple Watch Workout Burns the Most Calories

Choosing the right Apple Watch workout can help you burn the most calories and achieve your fitness goals. While there are several options available, the High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) workout is the most effective for burning calories.

However, it's essential to choose a workout that aligns with your fitness level and goals. Don't be afraid to try different workouts and find the one that works best for you.
