Low Impact Cardio Exercises

My favorite low impact cardio exercise is walking. Walking is a great way to burn calories and fat, and it's gentle on your joints. You can start with a 10-minute walk and gradually increase your time and distance. Other low impact cardio exercises that you can try are cycling, swimming, and dancing.

Strength Training Exercises

Strength training exercises are great for building muscle, improving your metabolism, and burning fat. You can start with bodyweight exercises such as squats, lunges, push-ups, and planks. You can also use resistance bands or dumbbells for added resistance.

Yoga

Yoga is a low impact exercise that helps to improve your flexibility, balance, and strength. It also helps to reduce stress and improve your overall well-being. You can try different yoga poses such as downward-facing dog, warrior I and II, and tree pose.

Pilates

Pilates is a low impact exercise that focuses on core strength, stability, and balance. It's great for toning your muscles and improving your posture. You can try different Pilates exercises such as the hundred, roll-up, and spine stretch.
