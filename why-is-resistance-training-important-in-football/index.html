Why Is Resistance Training Important In Football

Written by Bardi Aug 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Football is a sport that requires a combination of strength, speed, agility, and endurance. To be successful on the field, players need to have a strong foundation of physical fitness. Resistance training is an essential component of any football player's workout routine, as it helps to improve strength, power, and muscular endurance.

What are the Pain Points of Football Players?

Football players are at risk of various injuries, such as sprains, strains, and fractures, due to the high-impact nature of the sport. They also need to be able to perform explosive movements, such as jumping, sprinting, and tackling, while maintaining their balance and coordination. Moreover, they need to have the stamina to keep up with the pace of the game, which can last up to 90 minutes.

Why is Resistance Training Important in Football?

Resistance training involves using weights, resistance bands, or bodyweight exercises to create resistance against the muscles. This type of training helps to stimulate muscle growth and improve neuromuscular coordination, which is essential for football players. Here are some of the benefits of resistance training in football:

  • Increased strength: Resistance training helps to build muscle strength, which can improve a player's ability to perform explosive movements, such as jumping, sprinting, and tackling.
  • Improved power: Power is the ability to exert force quickly, and resistance training helps to improve power by increasing the rate of force development.
  • Enhanced muscular endurance: Football players need to have the stamina to perform at a high level throughout the game, and resistance training can help to improve muscular endurance by increasing the number of repetitions a player can do before fatigue sets in.
  • Reduced risk of injury: Resistance training can help to strengthen the muscles and connective tissues, which can reduce the risk of injury from high-impact movements.

My Personal Experience with Resistance Training in Football

As a former football player, I can attest to the importance of resistance training in improving my performance on the field. I used to incorporate exercises such as squats, deadlifts, lunges, and bench press into my workout routine, which helped me to build strength and power. I also did plyometric exercises, such as box jumps and hurdle jumps, to improve my explosiveness. Overall, resistance training helped me to become a better football player by enhancing my physical abilities.

How to Incorporate Resistance Training into Your Football Workout

If you're a football player looking to incorporate resistance training into your workout routine, here are some tips:

  • Start with the basics: Focus on compound exercises, such as squats, deadlifts, and bench press, which work multiple muscle groups at once.
  • Gradually increase weight: Start with a weight that you can comfortably lift for 10-12 repetitions, and gradually increase the weight as you get stronger.
  • Incorporate plyometrics: Plyometric exercises, such as box jumps and hurdle jumps, can help to improve your explosiveness on the field.
  • Don't neglect cardiovascular exercise: While resistance training is important, you also need to have good cardiovascular fitness to perform well on the field. Incorporate activities such as running, cycling, or swimming into your workout routine.

FAQs About Why is Resistance Training Important in Football

Q: How often should I incorporate resistance training into my football workout?

A: It's recommended to do resistance training 2-3 times per week, with at least one day of rest between sessions.

Q: Do I need to lift heavy weights to see results from resistance training?

A: No, you can see results from resistance training even with light weights or bodyweight exercises, as long as you're challenging your muscles and gradually increasing the resistance over time.

Q: Can resistance training help me to improve my speed on the field?

A: Yes, resistance training can help to improve your power and explosiveness, which can translate to faster sprinting and agility on the field.

Q: Is resistance training safe for young football players?

A: Yes, resistance training can be safe for young football players as long as they are supervised by a qualified coach or trainer and use proper form and technique.

Conclusion of Why is Resistance Training Important in Football

Resistance training is an essential component of any football player's workout routine, as it helps to improve strength, power, and muscular endurance. By incorporating resistance training into your workout, you can reduce your risk of injury and enhance your performance on the field. Remember to start with the basics, gradually increase weight, incorporate plyometrics, and don't neglect cardiovascular exercise for a well-rounded workout routine.
