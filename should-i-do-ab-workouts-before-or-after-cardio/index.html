Should I Do Ab Workouts Before Or After Cardio

Written by William Jul 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you an avid gym-goer looking to tone your abs? Or are you a beginner trying to figure out the best workout routine for your fitness goals? One of the most common questions in the fitness world is whether one should do ab workouts before or after cardio. Read on to find out the answer.

The order in which you do ab workouts and cardio can have an impact on your overall fitness results. This decision can be a pain point for many fitness enthusiasts who are unsure of which order is best to achieve their desired outcomes.

The short answer is that it depends on your fitness goals. If your main goal is to build and tone your abs, then it is recommended to do your ab workouts first before moving on to cardio. On the other hand, if your primary goal is to improve your cardiovascular health or lose weight, then it is better to start with cardio before proceeding with ab workouts.

In conclusion, the order of your workout routine depends on what you want to achieve. However, if you are unsure, it is always best to consult with a fitness professional who can guide you in creating a personalized workout plan.

Benefits of Doing Ab Workouts Before Cardio

Personally, I prefer doing my ab workouts before cardio because it helps me to focus on my core muscles without being too tired from cardio. Doing ab workouts first also helps to activate your core muscles, which can help to improve your performance during cardio exercises.

When you do cardio first, your body is already in a fatigued state, which can impact your form and reduce the effectiveness of your ab workout. Therefore, starting with ab workouts can help to maintain proper form and maximize the benefits of your workout.

Benefits of Doing Cardio Before Ab Workouts

On the other hand, doing cardio before ab workouts can also have its benefits. Starting with cardio can help to raise your heart rate and warm up your body, which can help to prevent injury during your ab workout.

Additionally, doing cardio first can help you to burn more calories during your workout, which can be beneficial if your primary goal is weight loss.

How to Incorporate Both Ab Workouts and Cardio in Your Routine

If you want to incorporate both ab workouts and cardio in your routine, it is recommended to alternate between the two. For example, you can start with ab workouts on Monday and Wednesday, and then do cardio on Tuesday, Thursday, and Friday. This allows your body to rest and recover in between workouts.

Tips for Maximizing Your Ab Workout

Whether you decide to do ab workouts before or after cardio, there are some tips you can follow to maximize your ab workout:

  • Focus on quality over quantity: It's better to do a few reps with proper form than many reps with bad form.
  • Incorporate a variety of exercises: Don't just stick to one type of ab exercise, mix it up to target different areas of your core.
  • Engage your core: Make sure to engage your core muscles throughout the entire exercise to maximize the benefits.
  • Be consistent: Consistency is key when it comes to seeing results. Make sure to incorporate ab workouts into your routine at least 2-3 times a week.

Question and Answer

Q: How long should I do ab workouts?

A: It is recommended to do ab workouts for at least 10-15 minutes, 2-3 times a week.

Q: Can I do cardio and ab workouts on the same day?

A: Yes, you can do both on the same day. Just make sure to alternate between the two and give your body time to rest and recover in between workouts.

Q: What are some effective ab exercises?

A: Some effective ab exercises include crunches, planks, bicycle crunches, and Russian twists.

Q: How long does it take to see results from ab workouts?

A: It can take anywhere from 4-12 weeks to see visible results from ab workouts, depending on your fitness level and consistency.

Conclusion

Ultimately, whether you do ab workouts before or after cardio depends on your fitness goals. However, it's important to keep in mind that your form and consistency are key factors in achieving your desired results. By incorporating a variety of exercises, engaging your core muscles, and being consistent, you can maximize the benefits of your ab workout, regardless of the order in which you choose to do it.
