Best Free Workout App For Overweight Beginners

Written by Aprilia Oct 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you an overweight beginner looking for a workout app that can help you get started on your fitness journey? Do you find it difficult to find the motivation to exercise or the time to go to a gym? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will introduce you to the best free workout app for overweight beginners that can help you achieve your fitness goals.

Starting a fitness journey is not easy, especially when you are overweight. You may feel self-conscious about your body or lack confidence in your ability to exercise. Additionally, you may not have the budget or time to hire a personal trainer, or join a gym. These are some of the pain points that overweight beginners face when trying to start their fitness journey.

The best free workout app for overweight beginners is MyFitnessPal. This app is designed to help you track your food intake, exercise, and progress towards your fitness goals. It is available for free on both Android and iOS devices. With this app, you can set your fitness goals, track your progress, and get personalized recommendations for workouts and meals that are tailored to your specific needs.

In summary, the best free workout app for overweight beginners is MyFitnessPal. This app can help you overcome the pain points of starting a fitness journey, and provide you with the tools you need to achieve your goals.

What is MyFitnessPal?

MyFitnessPal is a free app that helps you track your food intake, exercise, and progress towards your fitness goals. It has over 6 million foods in its database, making it easy to track what you eat. You can also connect with friends, join challenges, and get support from the MyFitnessPal community.

When I first started my fitness journey, I was overwhelmed by the number of apps available. However, after trying a few, I found that MyFitnessPal was the best fit for me. It was easy to use, had a lot of features, and helped me stay on track.

How Can MyFitnessPal Help Overweight Beginners?

MyFitnessPal can help overweight beginners in several ways. Firstly, it can help you track your food intake, which is essential for weight loss. Secondly, it can provide you with personalized recommendations for workouts and meals that are tailored to your specific needs. Thirdly, it can help you stay motivated by tracking your progress and celebrating your achievements.

As an overweight beginner, I found it difficult to know what foods and exercises were best for me. However, with MyFitnessPal, I was able to get personalized recommendations that helped me achieve my goals.

How Does MyFitnessPal Track Your Food Intake?

MyFitnessPal has a database of over 6 million foods. To track your food intake, you simply search for the food you ate and add it to your diary. The app will then calculate the calories, macros, and other nutritional information for you. You can also scan barcodes or create custom foods to make tracking easier.

One of the things I love about MyFitnessPal is how easy it is to track my food intake. I can easily see how many calories I have consumed, and how many I have left for the day.

How Can MyFitnessPal Help You Stay Motivated?

MyFitnessPal can help you stay motivated by tracking your progress and celebrating your achievements. You can set goals, such as losing a certain amount of weight or running a certain distance, and the app will track your progress towards those goals. You can also earn badges for achievements, such as logging your meals for a certain number of days in a row.

Personally, I found the badges to be a great way to stay motivated. It was fun to see how many I could earn, and they provided a sense of accomplishment.

Conclusion of Best Free Workout App for Overweight Beginners

In conclusion, if you are an overweight beginner looking for a free workout app, then MyFitnessPal is the best option for you. It can help you track your food intake, provide you with personalized recommendations for workouts and meals, and help you stay motivated. With the help of MyFitnessPal, you can achieve your fitness goals and lead a healthier life.

Question and Answer

Q: Is MyFitnessPal free?

A: Yes, MyFitnessPal is completely free to use.

Q: Can you track your exercise with MyFitnessPal?

A: Yes, MyFitnessPal has a built-in exercise tracker that allows you to log your workouts and track your progress.

Q: How accurate is the calorie tracking on MyFitnessPal?

A: The calorie tracking on MyFitnessPal is generally very accurate, as it has a large database of foods and uses algorithms to estimate the nutritional content of foods that are not in its database.

Q: Can you connect with friends on MyFitnessPal?

A: Yes, you can connect with friends on MyFitnessPal and share your progress with them. You can also join challenges and compete with other users.
