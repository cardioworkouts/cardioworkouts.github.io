One of my favorite exercises from the Fitness Blender Total Body Cardio Workout is the squat jack. This exercise targets the legs, glutes, and cardiovascular system. To do a squat jack, start in a standing position with your feet together. Jump your feet out to the sides while simultaneously lowering your body into a squat. Jump your feet back together while standing up. Repeat this exercise for 30 seconds.
