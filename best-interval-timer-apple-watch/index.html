How to Customize Intervals and Rest Periods with the Best Interval Timer Apple Watch

To customize your intervals and rest periods with the best interval timer Apple Watch, follow these steps:

  1. Open the best interval timer Apple Watch app on your watch.
  2. Select "New Workout."
  3. Select the type of exercise you'll be doing (e.g., running, cycling, weightlifting).
  4. Choose the duration of your workout.
  5. Customize your intervals and rest periods by selecting the "+" button.
  6. Set the duration of your interval and rest period.
  7. Repeat steps 5 and 6 until you've created all your intervals.
  8. Select "Start" to begin your workout.
