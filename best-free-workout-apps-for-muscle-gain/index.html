Best Free Workout Apps For Muscle Gain

Written by William Jun 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to build muscle but don't want to spend money on a personal trainer or gym membership? Luckily, there are many free workout apps available that can help you achieve your muscle gain goals from the comfort of your own home. In this post, we will explore the best free workout apps for muscle gain and how they can help you reach your fitness goals.

Building muscle can be a challenging and time-consuming process, especially if you don't have the right guidance and tools. Pain points such as lack of motivation, not knowing what exercises to do, and not having access to equipment can make it difficult to stay on track. However, with the best free workout apps for muscle gain, you can overcome these obstacles and achieve your desired results.

What are the Best Free Workout Apps for Muscle Gain?

There are many free workout apps available, but not all of them are created equal. Here are some of the best free workout apps for muscle gain:

1. Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club offers a wide variety of workouts for all fitness levels, including strength training, cardio, and yoga. The app provides personalized recommendations based on your fitness goals and offers guidance from professional trainers. With over 190 free workouts available, there is something for everyone.

2. Fitbod

Fitbod is a weightlifting app that creates personalized workout plans based on your fitness goals, equipment, and available time. The app tracks your progress and adjusts your workouts accordingly, ensuring that you are constantly challenged and making progress.

3. JEFIT

JEFIT offers over 1,300 exercises and 60 workout routines, making it a great app for those who want variety in their workouts. The app also tracks your progress and provides detailed analytics, allowing you to see your improvements over time.

4. Bodyweight Training: You Are Your Own Gym

This app offers a variety of bodyweight exercises that can be done anywhere, without the need for equipment. The app includes over 200 exercises and 4 different workout levels, making it a great option for beginners and advanced users alike.

My Personal Experience with the Best Free Workout Apps for Muscle Gain

I have personally used the Nike Training Club app for several months and have found it to be a great tool for building muscle. The app offers a wide variety of workouts, and the personalized recommendations have helped me stay motivated and on track. I also appreciate the guidance from professional trainers, as it ensures that I am performing exercises correctly and safely.

Additionally, I have recently started using the Fitbod app and have been impressed with its personalized workout plans. The app takes into account my fitness goals, available equipment, and time constraints, and creates a workout plan that is challenging but achievable. The tracking and analytics features are also helpful for monitoring progress and staying motivated.

How Do These Apps Help with Muscle Gain?

The best free workout apps for muscle gain provide a variety of exercises that target different muscle groups, ensuring that you are getting a well-rounded workout. Additionally, many of these apps offer personalized recommendations and guidance from professional trainers, ensuring that you are performing exercises correctly and safely. The tracking and analytics features also allow you to monitor your progress and make adjustments to your workouts as needed, ensuring that you are constantly challenging yourself and making progress.

How Often Should I Use These Apps?

It is recommended to use these apps at least 3-4 times per week to see significant muscle gain results. However, it's important to listen to your body and not overdo it, as rest and recovery are also important for muscle growth.

What if I Don't Have Access to Equipment?

Apps such as Bodyweight Training: You Are Your Own Gym offer exercises that can be done without the need for equipment. Additionally, many of the other apps offer workouts that can be done with minimal equipment, such as dumbbells or resistance bands.

Conclusion of Best Free Workout Apps for Muscle Gain

Building muscle doesn't have to cost a fortune. The best free workout apps for muscle gain offer a variety of exercises, personalized recommendations, and tracking features that can help you achieve your fitness goals. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced lifter, these apps can provide the guidance and motivation you need to build muscle and achieve your desired results.
