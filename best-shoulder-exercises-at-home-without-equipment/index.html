Best Shoulder Exercises At Home Without Equipment

Written by Eveline Nov 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you want to strengthen your shoulders but don't have access to a gym or equipment? Don't worry, you can still get a great shoulder workout at home without any equipment. In this tutorial, we will show you the best shoulder exercises that you can do at home without any equipment.

Many people often struggle with shoulder pain and discomfort, which can limit their range of motion and affect their daily activities. Strengthening your shoulder muscles can help improve your posture and reduce the risk of injury. However, finding the right exercises that target the shoulders without equipment can be a challenge.

The target of this tutorial is to provide you with the best shoulder exercises that you can do at home without any equipment. These exercises will help you build strong and stable shoulders, improve your posture, and reduce the risk of shoulder injuries.

In summary, this tutorial will cover the best shoulder exercises at home without equipment and provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to perform each exercise. These exercises include shoulder push-ups, shoulder taps, pike push-ups, and plank shoulder taps.

Shoulder Push-Ups

Shoulder push-ups are a great exercise that targets your shoulders, chest, and triceps. To perform this exercise, start in a push-up position with your hands shoulder-width apart. Lower your body towards the ground while keeping your elbows close to your body. Push back up to the starting position and repeat for 3 sets of 10 reps.

I personally love doing shoulder push-ups because they are very effective in strengthening my shoulders, and I can feel the burn after just a few reps. They are also a great exercise to do at home because you don't need any equipment.

Shoulder Taps

Shoulder taps are another great exercise that targets your shoulders, core, and arms. Start in a plank position with your hands shoulder-width apart. Lift one hand off the ground and tap your opposite shoulder. Return to the starting position and repeat with the other hand. Do 3 sets of 10 reps on each side.

I find shoulder taps to be challenging but effective in building shoulder stability and core strength. They are also a great exercise to do at home because they don't require any equipment.

Pike Push-Ups

Pike push-ups are a more challenging exercise that targets your shoulders and triceps. Start in a downward-facing dog position with your hands and feet shoulder-width apart. Lower your head towards the ground while keeping your elbows close to your body. Push back up to the starting position and repeat for 3 sets of 10 reps.

I love doing pike push-ups because they are a great way to challenge myself and build upper body strength. They are also a great exercise to do at home because they don't require any equipment.

Plank Shoulder Taps

Plank shoulder taps are a variation of the shoulder tap exercise that targets your shoulders, core, and arms. Start in a plank position with your hands shoulder-width apart. Lift one hand off the ground and tap your opposite shoulder. Return to the starting position and repeat with the other hand. Do 3 sets of 10 reps on each side.

I find plank shoulder taps to be a great exercise for building shoulder stability and core strength. They are also a great exercise to do at home because they don't require any equipment.

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I do these exercises?

A: You should aim to do these exercises 2-3 times per week for best results.

Q: Can I do these exercises if I have shoulder pain?

A: If you have shoulder pain, it's best to consult a healthcare professional before doing any exercises. They can help diagnose the cause of your pain and recommend the best exercises for you.

Q: Do I need any equipment for these exercises?

A: No, you don't need any equipment for these exercises. They can all be done using your own body weight.

Q: Can I modify these exercises if they are too difficult?

A: Yes, you can modify these exercises by doing fewer reps or doing a less intense variation of the exercise. For example, you can start with knee push-ups instead of full push-ups.

Conclusion of Best Shoulder Exercises at Home Without Equipment

Strengthening your shoulders is important for improving your posture and reducing the risk of injury. These best shoulder exercises at home without equipment are a great way to build strong and stable shoulders without needing a gym or any equipment. By incorporating these exercises into your workout routine, you can achieve stronger and healthier shoulders in no time.
