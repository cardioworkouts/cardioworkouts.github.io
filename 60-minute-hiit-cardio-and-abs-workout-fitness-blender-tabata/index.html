What is the 60 Minute HIIT Cardio and Abs Workout Fitness Blender Tabata?

The 60 Minute HIIT Cardio and Abs Workout Fitness Blender Tabata is a full-body workout that combines cardio and abdominal exercises in a high-intensity interval training format. The workout is led by Fitness Blender, a popular online fitness community that offers free workout videos and fitness tips.

The workout consists of various exercises, including jumping jacks, mountain climbers, burpees, squat jumps, and planks, all designed to get your heart rate up and burn calories. Each exercise is done in a tabata style, which means you perform each exercise for 20 seconds, followed by a 10-second rest, for a total of four minutes per exercise. The workout is broken down into four rounds, with a two-minute rest period in between each round.
