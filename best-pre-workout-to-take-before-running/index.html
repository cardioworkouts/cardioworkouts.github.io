Best Pre Workout To Take Before Running A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 19, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for the best pre workout to take before running? Do you want to maximize your performance and get the most out of your workout? If so, you've come to the right place. In this guide, we'll explore the top pre workout supplements for runners and provide you with everything you need to know to make an informed decision.

Understanding the Pain Points of Running

Running is a physically demanding activity that requires a lot of energy and endurance. For many runners, fatigue and muscle soreness can be major obstacles to achieving their fitness goals. Additionally, running can also be mentally challenging, requiring focus and determination to push through the discomfort.

One of the best ways to overcome these pain points is by incorporating a pre workout supplement into your routine. A pre workout can help you increase your energy levels, reduce fatigue, and improve your mental focus, allowing you to push through even the toughest runs.

What is the Best Pre Workout to Take Before Running?

When it comes to choosing the best pre workout for running, there are several factors to consider. Some of the most important factors include:

  • Energy Boosting
  • Endurance Enhancing
  • Muscle Recovery
  • Mental Focus

Based on these factors, some of the best pre workout supplements for runners include:

  • Caffeine
  • Beta-Alanine
  • Citrulline Malate
  • Creatine
  • Branched Chain Amino Acids (BCAAs)

Each of these supplements has unique benefits that can help improve your running performance. For example, caffeine is a powerful stimulant that can boost energy levels and improve mental focus, while beta-alanine can help reduce muscle fatigue and improve endurance.

Personal Experience with Caffeine as Pre Workout

One of the most popular pre workout supplements for runners is caffeine. As someone who regularly runs long distances, I've found that caffeine can be a game-changer when it comes to improving my performance. By taking a caffeine supplement before my runs, I'm able to increase my energy levels and mental focus, allowing me to push through even the toughest runs.

Additionally, caffeine can also help improve my endurance by reducing muscle fatigue and allowing me to run for longer periods of time. Overall, I highly recommend using caffeine as a pre workout supplement for running.

Personal Experience with Beta-Alanine as Pre Workout

Another pre workout supplement that I've had great success with is beta-alanine. Beta-alanine is an amino acid that can help reduce muscle fatigue and improve endurance, making it an excellent choice for runners.

When I take beta-alanine before my runs, I've noticed that I'm able to run for longer periods of time without feeling as fatigued. Additionally, I've also noticed that my muscles feel less sore after my runs, allowing me to recover more quickly and get back to training sooner.

The Benefits of Creatine for Runners

Creatine is another popular pre workout supplement that can be beneficial for runners. Creatine is a naturally occurring compound that is found in muscle cells, and it can help improve muscle strength and endurance.

For runners, creatine can be particularly helpful for improving sprint performance and reducing muscle fatigue. Additionally, creatine can also help improve muscle recovery, allowing runners to get back to training more quickly.

How to Take Pre Workout Supplements for Running

If you're interested in taking pre workout supplements for running, it's important to follow the recommended dosage instructions carefully. Additionally, you should also make sure to drink plenty of water before and during your runs to stay hydrated.

It's also a good idea to start with a lower dosage of any new supplement and gradually increase the dosage over time. This can help you avoid any potential side effects and ensure that you're getting the maximum benefits from the supplement.

Personal Experience with Citrulline Malate as Pre Workout

Citrulline malate is another pre workout supplement that can be beneficial for runners. Citrulline malate is an amino acid that can help improve blood flow and reduce muscle fatigue, making it an excellent choice for runners.

When I take citrulline malate before my runs, I've noticed that my muscles feel less fatigued and I'm able to run for longer periods of time. Additionally, I've also noticed that my recovery time is shorter, allowing me to get back to training sooner.

Question and Answer

Q: Are pre workout supplements safe for runners?

A: When taken according to the recommended dosage instructions, pre workout supplements are generally safe for runners. However, it's important to talk to your doctor before starting any new supplement regimen.

Q: How long before my run should I take my pre workout supplement?

A: The timing of your pre workout supplement will depend on the specific supplement you're taking. In general, it's a good idea to take your supplement 30-60 minutes before your run to allow enough time for it to take effect.

Q: Can I take multiple pre workout supplements at once?

A: While it's possible to take multiple pre workout supplements at once, it's generally not recommended. Taking too many supplements at once can increase your risk of side effects and may not provide any additional benefits.

Q: Do I need to cycle my pre workout supplements?

A: Some pre workout supplements, such as caffeine, may require cycling to avoid building up a tolerance. However, other supplements, such as creatine, can be taken continuously without cycling.

Conclusion of Best Pre Workout to Take Before Running

When it comes to choosing the best pre workout to take before running, there are several factors to consider. By incorporating a pre workout supplement into your routine, you can increase your energy levels, reduce fatigue, and improve your mental focus, allowing you to push through even the toughest runs.

Whether you choose caffeine, beta-alanine, citrulline malate, creatine, or BCAAs, make sure to follow the recommended dosage instructions carefully and drink plenty of water to stay hydrated. With the right pre workout supplement, you can take your running performance to the next level.
