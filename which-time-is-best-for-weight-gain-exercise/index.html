Which Time Is Best For Weight Gain Exercise

Written by Bardi Nov 12, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you struggling to gain weight despite your efforts to improve your diet and workout routine? One factor you may not have considered is the timing of your workouts. The time of day you exercise can have a significant impact on your ability to gain weight and build muscle. In this post, we will explore which time is best for weight gain exercise and how you can optimize your workouts to achieve your goals.

Many people who struggle with weight gain are unaware that the timing of their workouts may be hindering their progress. Some common pain points include feeling unmotivated to workout at certain times, struggling to fit exercise into a busy schedule, or not knowing which time is best for weight gain exercise.

The short answer to the question of which time is best for weight gain exercise is: there is no one-size-fits-all answer. The optimal time to exercise for weight gain will depend on several factors, including your schedule, lifestyle, and personal preferences. However, there are some general guidelines you can follow to ensure you are maximizing the benefits of your workouts.

To summarize, the timing of your workouts can have a significant impact on your ability to gain weight and build muscle. While there is no one-size-fits-all answer to the question of which time is best for weight gain exercise, there are some general guidelines you can follow to optimize your workouts.

Morning Workouts

Personally, I have found that morning workouts are the best for weight gain. Working out in the morning can help jumpstart your metabolism, increase energy levels, and set a positive tone for the rest of the day. Additionally, morning workouts can help you stay consistent with your exercise routine, as you are less likely to skip a workout if you complete it first thing in the morning.

If you decide to try morning workouts, it is important to ensure you are fueling your body properly before and after your workout. Eat a small snack or meal before your workout to provide your body with the fuel it needs to perform. After your workout, be sure to refuel with a protein-rich meal or snack to aid in muscle recovery and growth.

Afternoon Workouts

Afternoon workouts can be a good option for those who struggle to wake up early or have a busy schedule in the morning. Working out in the afternoon can help you relieve stress and boost energy levels mid-day, which can help you power through the rest of the day. Additionally, afternoon workouts can be a good option for those who enjoy working out with a partner or in a group setting, as many fitness classes and group workouts are held in the afternoon.

If you decide to try afternoon workouts, be sure to plan your meals and snacks accordingly. Eat a light meal or snack before your workout to provide your body with the energy it needs to perform. After your workout, refuel with a balanced meal or snack to aid in muscle recovery and growth.

Evening Workouts

Evening workouts can be a good option for those who have busy mornings and afternoons. Working out in the evening can help you relieve stress and unwind after a long day, and can also help you avoid the post-workout fatigue that can occur after early morning or mid-day workouts. Additionally, evening workouts can be a good option for those who enjoy working out alone, as the gym is typically less crowded in the evening.

If you decide to try evening workouts, be sure to eat a light meal or snack before your workout to provide your body with the energy it needs to perform. After your workout, be sure to refuel with a balanced meal or snack to aid in muscle recovery and growth.

Night Workouts

Personally, I do not recommend night workouts for weight gain. Working out late at night can interfere with your sleep and recovery, which can hinder your ability to build muscle and gain weight. Additionally, working out late at night can cause an increase in cortisol levels, which can lead to muscle breakdown and hinder muscle growth.

If you must workout at night, be sure to eat a light meal or snack before your workout and allow time to wind down and relax before going to bed.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I workout on an empty stomach?

A: It is generally not recommended to workout on an empty stomach, as your body needs fuel to perform. Eat a small snack or meal before your workout to provide your body with the energy it needs to perform.

Q: Should I eat before or after my workout?

A: It is important to fuel your body both before and after your workout. Eat a small snack or meal before your workout to provide your body with the energy it needs to perform, and refuel with a protein-rich meal or snack after your workout to aid in muscle recovery and growth.

Q: How long should I wait to eat after my workout?

A: It is generally recommended to eat within 30 minutes to an hour after your workout to refuel and aid in muscle recovery and growth.

Q: Can I workout at any time of the day?

A: Yes, you can workout at any time of the day that works best for you and your schedule. However, be sure to follow the guidelines outlined above to ensure you are optimizing the benefits of your workouts.

Conclusion of Which Time is Best for Weight Gain Exercise

Ultimately, the best time to workout for weight gain will depend on your schedule, lifestyle, and personal preferences. However, there are some general guidelines you can follow to ensure you are optimizing the benefits of your workouts. Experiment with different times of day and meal timings to find the routine that works best for you and your goals.
