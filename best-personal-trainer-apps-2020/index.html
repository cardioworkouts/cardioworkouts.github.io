Best Personal Trainer Apps 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Aug 05, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of going to the gym and working out without seeing any results? Do you want to hire a personal trainer but can't afford one? If you answered yes to either of these questions, then you're in luck! In today's digital age, there are plenty of personal trainer apps available that can help you achieve your fitness goals. In this post, we'll review some of the best personal trainer apps 2020 has to offer and help you find the perfect one for you.

Working out can be a pain for many people, especially if they don't have a clear idea of what they're doing. Finding a personal trainer can be expensive, and committing to a workout plan can be challenging. Luckily, personal trainer apps can help solve these problems. With features like personalized workout plans, tracking progress, and virtual coaching, these apps can provide you with the guidance and motivation you need to succeed.

The target of best personal trainer apps 2020 is to help people achieve their fitness goals by providing them with personalized workout plans, tracking their progress, and offering virtual coaching. Some of the best personal trainer apps 2020 has to offer are: Nike Training Club, Fitbit Coach, MyFitnessPal, 8fit, and Aaptiv.

In summary, personal trainer apps are a great way to get in shape and stay motivated. They offer personalized workout plans, tracking progress, and virtual coaching to help you achieve your fitness goals. Some of the best personal trainer apps 2020 has to offer are Nike Training Club, Fitbit Coach, MyFitnessPal, 8fit, and Aaptiv.

Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club is a great personal trainer app for those who want a personalized workout plan. The app offers over 185 free workouts, ranging from yoga to strength training, all of which are designed by Nike's Master Trainers. The app's AI technology also creates personalized workout plans based on your fitness level, goals, and available equipment. One of the best things about this app is that it offers both audio and video coaching, making it easy to follow along with the workouts.

Fitbit Coach

Fitbit Coach is another great personal trainer app that offers personalized workout plans. The app creates workout plans based on your fitness level, goals, and available equipment. It also tracks your progress and offers virtual coaching to help keep you motivated. One of the best things about this app is that it integrates with Fitbit's other products, such as the Fitbit tracker, to provide you with a complete fitness solution.

MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is an all-in-one personal trainer app that offers both workout plans and nutrition tracking. The app creates personalized workout plans based on your fitness level, goals, and available equipment. It also tracks your food intake and offers personalized nutrition advice to help you achieve your fitness goals. One of the best things about this app is that it integrates with other fitness apps, such as Fitbit and Apple Health, to provide you with a complete fitness solution.

8fit

8fit is a personal trainer app that offers both workout plans and meal plans. The app creates personalized workout plans based on your fitness level, goals, and available equipment. It also offers personalized meal plans based on your dietary preferences, making it easy to eat healthy and achieve your fitness goals. One of the best things about this app is that it offers both audio and video coaching, making it easy to follow along with the workouts.

Aaptiv

Aaptiv is a personal trainer app that offers audio-based workouts. The app offers over 2,500 workouts, ranging from yoga to strength training, all of which are designed by certified personal trainers. One of the best things about this app is that it offers personalized workout plans based on your fitness level, goals, and available equipment. It also tracks your progress and offers virtual coaching to help keep you motivated.

Question and Answer

Q: Can personal trainer apps really help me achieve my fitness goals?

A: Yes, personal trainer apps can help you achieve your fitness goals by offering personalized workout plans, tracking your progress, and providing virtual coaching.

Q: How do I choose the best personal trainer app for me?

A: To choose the best personal trainer app for you, consider your fitness goals, available equipment, and dietary preferences. You should also read reviews and try out different apps to find the one that works best for you.

Q: Are personal trainer apps expensive?

A: Personal trainer apps can range from free to a few dollars per month. Some apps also offer premium features for an additional cost. However, compared to hiring a personal trainer, personal trainer apps are generally more affordable.

Q: Can I use personal trainer apps if I'm a beginner?

A: Yes, most personal trainer apps offer workouts for all fitness levels, including beginners. The apps also offer personalized workout plans based on your fitness level, making it easy to get started.

Conclusion of Best Personal Trainer Apps 2020

Personal trainer apps are a great way to get in shape and stay motivated. They offer personalized workout plans, tracking progress, and virtual coaching to help you achieve your fitness goals. Some of the best personal trainer apps 2020 has to offer are Nike Training Club, Fitbit Coach, MyFitnessPal, 8fit, and Aaptiv. To choose the best app for you, consider your fitness goals, available equipment, and dietary preferences, and try out different apps to find the one that works best for you.
