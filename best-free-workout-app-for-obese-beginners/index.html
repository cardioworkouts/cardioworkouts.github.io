Best Free Workout App For Obese Beginners

Written by Robby Jul 30, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a way to start exercising but feel overwhelmed by the gym or don't know where to begin? As an obese beginner, it can be challenging to find a workout routine that is both effective and safe. Fortunately, there are many free workout apps available to help you get started on your fitness journey.

Starting a workout routine can be difficult for anyone, but it can be especially challenging for obese beginners. You may feel self-conscious, lack confidence, or have physical limitations. Finding a workout app that is tailored to your needs can make all the difference in staying motivated and reaching your fitness goals.

The best free workout app for obese beginners is one that provides low-impact exercises, step-by-step instructions, and customizable routines. These apps can help you build strength, increase flexibility, and improve your overall health. Here are some of the best free workout apps for obese beginners:

MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is a popular app that not only helps you track your food intake but also provides workout routines. The app offers personalized workout plans based on your fitness level and goals. The app has different types of workouts, including cardio, strength training, and yoga.

The app provides detailed instructions and videos for each exercise, making it easy to follow along. You can also track your progress and set reminders to keep you motivated. MyFitnessPal is available for both iOS and Android devices.

FitOn

FitOn is another great free workout app for obese beginners. The app offers a range of workout routines that are designed for different fitness levels. The app has workouts for cardio, strength training, yoga, and Pilates.

The app provides step-by-step instructions and videos for each exercise, making it easy to follow along. You can also customize your workout plan based on your fitness level and goals. The app is available for both iOS and Android devices.

Additional Features

Both MyFitnessPal and FitOn allow you to track your progress and connect with a community of like-minded individuals. These features can help you stay motivated and on track with your fitness goals. You can also set reminders to help you stay on schedule and make exercise a habit.

How to Get Started

To get started with either MyFitnessPal or FitOn, simply download the app on your device and create an account. From there, you can customize your workout plan and start exercising. It's best to start with shorter workouts and gradually increase the duration and intensity as you build strength and confidence.

Conclusion of Best Free Workout App for Obese Beginners

Starting a workout routine can be challenging for anyone, but it can be especially difficult for obese beginners. Fortunately, there are many free workout apps available that can help you get started on your fitness journey. The best free workout app for obese beginners is one that provides low-impact exercises, step-by-step instructions, and customizable routines. Whether you choose MyFitnessPal or FitOn, these apps can help you build strength, increase flexibility, and improve your overall health.

Question and Answer

Q. Can I use these apps if I have physical limitations?

A. Yes, both MyFitnessPal and FitOn offer low-impact exercises that are suitable for individuals with physical limitations. However, it's always best to consult with your healthcare provider before starting any exercise program.

Q. Do I need any equipment to use these apps?

A. No, both MyFitnessPal and FitOn offer workouts that require little to no equipment. However, some exercises may require basic equipment such as dumbbells or a yoga mat.

Q. Can I use these apps if I have never exercised before?

A. Yes, both MyFitnessPal and FitOn offer workouts that are suitable for beginners. The apps provide step-by-step instructions and videos for each exercise, making it easy to follow along.

Q. Are these apps really free?

A. Yes, both MyFitnessPal and FitOn offer free versions of their apps. However, they also offer premium versions with additional features for a fee.
