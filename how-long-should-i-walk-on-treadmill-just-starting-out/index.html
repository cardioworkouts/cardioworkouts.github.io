How Long Should I Walk On Treadmill Just Starting Out

Written by Oliver Sep 02, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking to start exercising but unsure of how long you should be walking on a treadmill? It can be overwhelming to figure out a routine and stick to it, especially as a beginner. But don't worry, we're here to help!

Starting a new exercise routine can be daunting, especially if you're not sure where to begin. One of the biggest pain points for beginners is figuring out how long they should be walking on a treadmill. It's important to find a balance between pushing yourself and not overdoing it.

So, how long should you walk on a treadmill just starting out? The general rule of thumb is to start with 20 minutes at a moderate pace, 3-4 times a week. As you build up your endurance and strength, you can gradually increase your time and intensity.

To summarize, as a beginner, it's important to start slow and gradually build up your endurance. Aim for 20 minutes at a moderate pace, 3-4 times a week, and increase your time and intensity as you progress.

My Personal Experience:

When I first started walking on a treadmill, I found it difficult to keep up with even 10 minutes at a time. However, I stuck with it and gradually increased my time and intensity. Now, I can easily walk for 45 minutes at a brisk pace without feeling fatigued.

Tips for Walking on a Treadmill:

1. Wear comfortable shoes and clothing
2. Start with a warm-up and end with a cool-down
3. Keep good posture
4. Don't hold onto the handrails
5. Gradually increase your time and intensity

Why is Walking on a Treadmill Beneficial for Beginners?

Walking on a treadmill is a great low-impact exercise for beginners. It's an easy way to get your heart rate up and burn calories without putting too much strain on your joints. Plus, you can easily adjust the speed and incline to challenge yourself as you progress.

How to Make Walking on a Treadmill More Challenging:

If you're looking to increase the intensity of your treadmill workout, there are a few things you can try. First, increase the speed or incline. You can also add intervals of jogging or running to your routine. Lastly, try incorporating weights or resistance bands to your workout.

My Personal Experience:

When I first started walking on a treadmill, I found it difficult to keep up with even 10 minutes at a time. However, I stuck with it and gradually increased my time and intensity. Now, I can easily walk for 45 minutes at a brisk pace without feeling fatigued.

Question and Answer:

Q: Can I walk on a treadmill every day?

A: Yes, but as a beginner, it's important to start slow and gradually build up your endurance. Aim for 3-4 times a week and increase your time and intensity as you progress.

Q: How fast should I walk on a treadmill?

A: Aim for a moderate pace, around 3-4 mph, to start. As you progress, you can increase your speed.

Q: Should I hold onto the handrails while walking on a treadmill?

A: No, it's important to keep good posture and not hold onto the handrails as it can affect your balance and form.

Q: Can I lose weight by walking on a treadmill?

A: Yes, walking on a treadmill can help you burn calories and aid in weight loss. However, it's important to pair it with a healthy diet and other forms of exercise for optimal results.

Conclusion of How Long Should I Walk on Treadmill Just Starting Out:

Starting a new exercise routine can be intimidating, but with the right approach, walking on a treadmill can be an easy and effective way to get started. As a beginner, start slow and gradually increase your time and intensity. And remember, consistency is key!
