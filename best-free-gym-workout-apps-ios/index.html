Best Free Gym Workout Apps For Ios A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 17, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling to find the motivation to head to the gym? Do you feel like you need guidance when it comes to your workout routine? Look no further than the best free gym workout apps for iOS. These apps can help you stay motivated, track your progress, and take your fitness journey to the next level.

Working out can be challenging, especially if you’re new to the gym or unsure of what exercises to do. Even seasoned gym-goers can struggle with staying on track and pushing themselves to reach new goals. This is where the best free gym workout apps for iOS come in handy.

If you’re looking for an app that can guide you through a workout routine, track your progress, and offer personalized recommendations, then you’re in luck. There are several free gym workout apps available for iOS that can help you stay on track and achieve your fitness goals.

In this comprehensive guide, we’ll walk you through the best free gym workout apps for iOS and explain what makes them stand out. We’ll cover everything from workout routines to progress tracking, so you can find the app that’s right for you.

1. Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club is a free app that offers a variety of workout routines, from strength training to yoga. The app is designed to help you reach your fitness goals while offering personalized recommendations based on your fitness level and goals.

Personally, I’ve found Nike Training Club to be a great app for staying motivated and trying new things. The app offers video demonstrations of each exercise, making it easy to follow along and ensure proper form. Plus, the app allows you to track your progress and earn rewards for completing workouts.

2. Fitbod

Fitbod is another free gym workout app for iOS that offers personalized workout recommendations based on your fitness level and goals. The app uses machine learning algorithms to create customized workout plans that are tailored to your body and fitness level.

I’ve found Fitbod to be a great app for those who are new to the gym or looking to switch up their routine. The app offers a variety of exercises and allows you to track your progress over time. Plus, the app offers detailed instructions for each exercise, making it easy to ensure proper form.

3. JEFIT

JEFIT is a free app that offers a variety of workout routines, from strength training to cardio. The app allows you to track your progress, create custom workout plans, and connect with other users for motivation and support.

In my experience, JEFIT is a great app for those who are looking for a community of like-minded individuals. The app allows you to connect with other users and join challenges, which can help you stay motivated and on track with your fitness goals.

4. 7 Minute Workout

7 Minute Workout is a free app that offers quick and effective workout routines that can be completed in just 7 minutes. The app is perfect for those who are short on time but still want to get a good workout in.

In my opinion, 7 Minute Workout is a great app for those who are just starting out on their fitness journey or looking to add some variety to their workout routine. The app offers a variety of exercises and allows you to track your progress over time.

Question and Answer

Q: Do I need any equipment to use these apps?

A: It depends on the app. Some apps, like Nike Training Club and JEFIT, offer workout routines that require equipment. Fitbod and 7 Minute Workout, on the other hand, offer routines that can be completed without equipment.

Q: Can I use these apps if I’m a beginner?

A: Absolutely! These apps are designed to offer personalized recommendations based on your fitness level and goals. Whether you’re a seasoned gym-goer or just starting out, these apps can help you reach your fitness goals.

Q: Are these apps really free?

A: Yes! All of the apps listed in this guide are completely free to download and use. Some apps offer premium features that require a subscription, but the basic features are free.

Q: Can I track my progress with these apps?

A: Yes! All of the apps listed in this guide allow you to track your progress over time. Some apps offer more detailed progress tracking than others, but all of them offer some level of progress tracking.

Conclusion of Best Free Gym Workout Apps for iOS

Whether you’re new to the gym or a seasoned gym-goer, the best free gym workout apps for iOS can help you stay motivated and on track with your fitness goals. From personalized workout recommendations to progress tracking, these apps offer everything you need to take your fitness journey to the next level.
