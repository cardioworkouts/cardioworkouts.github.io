Which Is Best Cardio Before Or After Workout

Written by Daniel Oct 26, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you confused about whether to do cardio before or after your workout? Do you want to know which one is more effective for weight loss and muscle gain? If so, then you are in the right place. In this article, we will discuss which is best cardio before or after workout and related keywords.

Many people struggle with the decision of whether to perform cardio before or after their workout. This is a valid concern, as the timing of your cardio can have a significant impact on your fitness goals. In general, the answer to this question depends on your individual fitness goals and preferences.

If your primary goal is weight loss, then it is recommended to perform cardio after your workout. This is because when you do weight training first, you deplete your glycogen stores, and your body starts to burn fat for fuel during your cardio session. This leads to more fat loss and a better overall calorie burn.

On the other hand, if your primary goal is to improve your cardiovascular fitness, then it is recommended to perform cardio before your workout. This is because when you do cardio first, you warm up your body and get your heart rate up, which prepares you for the more intense weight training session that follows.

My Personal Experience

Personally, I prefer to do cardio after my weight training session. I find that I have more energy for my weight training when I am not already fatigued from cardio. Additionally, I enjoy the feeling of ending my workout with a good cardio session, which leaves me feeling energized and accomplished.

Benefits of Cardio Before Workout

If you choose to do cardio before your workout, there are several benefits to doing so. First, it can help you warm up your body and prepare you for the more intense weight training session that follows. Second, it can help improve your cardiovascular fitness and endurance, which can help you perform better during your weight training session. Lastly, it can help you burn more calories overall, which can be beneficial for weight loss.

Things to Consider When Doing Cardio Before Workout

When doing cardio before your workout, it is important to keep a few things in mind. First, make sure to properly warm up your body before starting your cardio session. This can help prevent injury and improve your performance. Second, try to keep your cardio session relatively short and intense, as you do not want to tire yourself out before your weight training session. Lastly, make sure to properly cool down and stretch after your cardio session to prevent injury and promote recovery.

Benefits of Cardio After Workout

If you choose to do cardio after your workout, there are also several benefits to doing so. First, it can help you burn more fat and calories overall, as your body is already in a fat-burning mode after weight training. Second, it can help improve your recovery time and reduce muscle soreness. Lastly, it can help you increase your overall fitness level and endurance.

Things to Consider When Doing Cardio After Workout

When doing cardio after your workout, it is important to keep a few things in mind. First, make sure to properly fuel your body with carbohydrates and protein after your weight training session, as this will help replenish your glycogen stores and promote muscle recovery. Second, try to keep your cardio session relatively short and intense, as you do not want to overdo it and risk injury. Lastly, make sure to properly cool down and stretch after your cardio session to prevent injury and promote recovery.

FAQs About Which is Best Cardio Before or After Workout

Q: Should I do cardio before or after weight training?

A: If your primary goal is weight loss, it is recommended to do cardio after weight training. If your primary goal is to improve your cardiovascular fitness, it is recommended to do cardio before weight training.

Q: How long should my cardio session be?

A: Your cardio session should be relatively short and intense, lasting no more than 30-45 minutes.

Q: Should I do cardio every day?

A: It is recommended to do cardio at least 3-4 times per week, with at least one day of rest in between sessions.

Q: Can I do both cardio before and after my workout?

A: Yes, you can do both cardio before and after your workout. However, it is important to listen to your body and not overdo it, as this can lead to injury and fatigue.

Conclusion of Which is Best Cardio Before or After Workout

In conclusion, the answer to the question of which is best cardio before or after workout depends on your individual fitness goals and preferences. If your primary goal is weight loss, it is recommended to do cardio after weight training. If your primary goal is to improve your cardiovascular fitness, it is recommended to do cardio before weight training. Regardless of when you decide to do your cardio, it is important to properly warm up, fuel your body, and cool down to prevent injury and promote recovery.
