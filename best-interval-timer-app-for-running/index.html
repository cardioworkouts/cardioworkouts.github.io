The Best Interval Timer App For Running A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of constantly checking your watch during runs to keep track of intervals? Do you struggle with maintaining a consistent pace during timed intervals? Look no further than the best interval timer app for running.

The best interval timer app for running can alleviate the stress of keeping track of intervals during a run. It can also help runners maintain a consistent pace during timed intervals, improving overall performance.

So, what is the best interval timer app for running? The answer may vary depending on personal preference and specific needs, but some of the most popular options include Interval Timer, Seconds Pro, and Tabata Timer.

In summary, the best interval timer app for running can improve performance and alleviate the stress of keeping track of intervals. Popular options include Interval Timer, Seconds Pro, and Tabata Timer.

Interval Timer

Interval Timer is a popular choice for runners looking for a simple and customizable interval timer app. It allows users to set up custom intervals and rest periods, with the option to save and reuse them for future runs.

Personally, I have been using Interval Timer for years and have found it to be extremely user-friendly and effective in improving my interval training. Its customizable features allow me to tailor my runs to specific training needs, whether it be sprint intervals or longer, endurance-based intervals.

Seconds Pro

Seconds Pro is another popular interval timer app for running, especially for those looking for more advanced features. It offers a library of pre-made interval timers and the ability to create custom timers with music and visual cues.

Although I haven't personally used Seconds Pro, I have heard great things from fellow runners who swear by its advanced features and ability to enhance their interval training.

Tabata Timer

Tabata Timer is specifically designed for Tabata-style workouts, which consist of 20 seconds of high-intensity exercise followed by 10 seconds of rest. It is a popular choice for runners looking to incorporate interval training into their routine.

While I haven't used Tabata Timer myself, many runners find it to be an effective tool for improving endurance and overall performance during interval training.

How to Use the Best Interval Timer App for Running

Using the best interval timer app for running is as simple as downloading the app and setting up custom intervals. Many apps, such as Interval Timer and Seconds Pro, offer tutorials and helpful tips for getting started.

Once the intervals are set up, simply hit start and let the app do the work. Some apps, such as Seconds Pro, offer visual and audio cues to help maintain a consistent pace during timed intervals.

Conclusion of Best Interval Timer App for Running

Whether you're a beginner or seasoned runner, incorporating interval training into your routine can improve overall performance. The best interval timer app for running can alleviate the stress of keeping track of intervals and help maintain a consistent pace during timed intervals.

Popular options include Interval Timer, Seconds Pro, and Tabata Timer. Choose the option that best fits your personal preferences and specific training needs, and watch as your interval training improves with every run.
