What is High Intensity Interval Training?

High intensity interval training (HIIT) is a type of workout that involves short bursts of intense exercise followed by brief periods of rest or active recovery. The goal of HIIT is to increase heart rate and improve cardiovascular fitness, while also building strength and endurance. HIIT workouts can be done with or without equipment and are designed to provide maximum results in a short amount of time.
