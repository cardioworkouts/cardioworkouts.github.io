Best Aerobic Exercise Video For Beginners

Written by Frank Aug 12, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking for an easy way to start exercising? Do you want to get in shape, but don't know where to begin? If you're a beginner, the thought of starting an exercise routine can be overwhelming. You may be worried about not knowing what exercises to do or how to do them correctly. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this post, we'll discuss the best aerobic exercise video for beginners that will help you get started on your fitness journey.

If you're a beginner, you may have concerns such as not knowing how to perform the exercises correctly, not having enough time, or not having the motivation to stick with it. These are common pain points that many beginners face, but with the right tools and mindset, you can overcome them.

The target of the best aerobic exercise video for beginners is to provide a low-impact workout that is easy to follow, and requires no equipment. It is perfect for those who are just starting their fitness journey or those who want to add some variety to their existing workout routine. Some of the best aerobic exercise videos for beginners include Leslie Sansone's Walk at Home and Jessica Smith TV.

In summary, the best aerobic exercise video for beginners is a low-impact workout that is easy to follow and requires no equipment. It is perfect for those who are just starting their fitness journey or those who want to add some variety to their existing workout routine.

Leslie Sansone's Walk at Home

Leslie Sansone's Walk at Home is one of the best aerobic exercise videos for beginners. It is a low-impact workout that is easy to follow and requires no equipment. Leslie Sansone is a well-known fitness instructor who has been helping people get in shape for over 30 years. In her Walk at Home series, she combines walking and strength training to provide a full-body workout that is suitable for people of all fitness levels.

Personally, I have been using Leslie Sansone's Walk at Home videos for over a year now, and I have seen great results. The videos are easy to follow, and Leslie is a great motivator. I love that I don't need any equipment, and I can do the workout in the comfort of my own home. The best part is that the workouts are only 30 minutes long, so they fit easily into my busy schedule.

Jessica Smith TV

Jessica Smith TV is another great option for beginners. Jessica Smith is a certified personal trainer who has created a range of workout videos that are suitable for people of all fitness levels. Her videos are easy to follow, and she provides modifications for those who need them. Her workouts are also low-impact, making them perfect for beginners.

I have also used Jessica Smith TV, and I love her workouts. Her videos are fun and engaging, and I always feel like I get a great workout. I appreciate that she provides modifications, so I can adjust the workout to my fitness level. Her videos range from 10 minutes to 60 minutes, so there's something for everyone.

Why are Low-Impact Workouts Suitable for Beginners?

Low-impact workouts are suitable for beginners because they are gentle on the joints and reduce the risk of injury. These workouts involve less jumping and high-impact movements, which can be hard on the knees and ankles. Low-impact workouts still provide a great cardiovascular workout, but without the added stress on the body.

How to Get Started with the Best Aerobic Exercise Video for Beginners?

To get started with the best aerobic exercise video for beginners, all you need is a comfortable pair of shoes and some space to move around. You can find these videos on YouTube or purchase them online. Start with a 10-minute workout and gradually increase the duration as you get more comfortable. Remember to listen to your body and take breaks when you need them.

Conclusion of Best Aerobic Exercise Video for Beginners

The best aerobic exercise video for beginners is one that is low-impact, easy to follow, and requires no equipment. Leslie Sansone's Walk at Home and Jessica Smith TV are two great options to get started on your fitness journey. Remember to start slow, listen to your body, and have fun!

Question and Answer

Q: Can I do these workouts if I have a health condition?
A: It's always best to consult with your doctor before starting a new exercise routine, especially if you have a health condition.

Q: Do I need any equipment for these workouts?
A: No, you don't need any equipment for these workouts. All you need is a comfortable pair of shoes and some space to move around.

Q: How often should I do these workouts?
A: It's recommended to do aerobic exercise for at least 150 minutes per week. You can spread this out over several days or do it all at once.

Q: Can I modify the workouts if I need to?
A: Yes, both Leslie Sansone and Jessica Smith provide modifications for those who need them. Listen to your body and adjust the workout to your fitness level.
