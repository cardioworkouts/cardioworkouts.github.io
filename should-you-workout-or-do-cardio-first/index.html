Should You Workout Or Do Cardio First

Written by Bardi Jul 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you confused about whether you should do cardio or workout first? If you're looking for an answer to this question, you're in the right place. Many fitness enthusiasts have debated over the years about whether cardio or workout should come first during a workout session. In this blog post, we'll dive deep into this topic and give you an answer to this much-debated question.

Pain Points

People who are new to fitness often find it challenging to decide whether they should do cardio or workout first. They are confused and overwhelmed by the conflicting information available on the internet. They also fear that they might not be doing the right thing, which can impact their results.

Answer to Should You Workout or Do Cardio First

It depends on your fitness goals. If you're looking to build muscle or increase strength, you should do your workout first. If you're looking to improve your cardiovascular health or lose weight, you should do cardio first.

Summary of Main Points

The debate about whether you should do cardio or workout first has been ongoing for years. The answer to this question depends on your fitness goals. If you're looking to build muscle or increase strength, you should do your workout first. If you're looking to improve your cardiovascular health or lose weight, you should do cardio first.

Why You Should Do Cardio First

Personally, I prefer doing cardio first before my workout. When you do cardio first, you warm up your body and get your heart rate up. This helps you to burn more calories during your workout. Doing cardio first also helps you to improve your endurance, which can help you to push harder during your workout.

Why You Should Do Workout First

However, if you're looking to build muscle or increase strength, you should do your workout first. When you do your workout first, you have more energy and can lift heavier weights. This helps to build muscle and increase strength more effectively.

Factors to Consider

When deciding whether to do cardio or workout first, there are several factors to consider. These factors include your fitness goals, your current fitness level, and your personal preferences. If you're new to fitness, it's best to consult with a personal trainer or fitness expert to determine the best approach for you.

Tips on How to Do Cardio or Workout First

If you're still unsure whether to do cardio or workout first, you can try both and see what works best for you. You can also split your workout into two sessions, doing cardio in the morning and workout in the evening. This can help you to get the best of both worlds.

Personal Experience

Personally, I prefer doing cardio first, as it helps me to warm up and get into the right mindset for my workout. I also find that doing cardio first helps me to burn more calories and improve my endurance, which can help me to push harder during my workout. However, I know people who prefer doing their workout first, and that works best for them.

Question and Answer

Q: Should I do cardio or workout first if I want to lose weight?

A: You should do cardio first if you want to lose weight. Cardio helps to burn more calories and improve your cardiovascular health, which can help you to achieve your weight loss goals.

Q: Can I do cardio and workout on the same day?

A: Yes, you can do both cardio and workout on the same day. However, it's best to split your workout into two sessions, doing cardio in the morning and workout in the evening.

Q: Should I do cardio or workout first if I want to build muscle?

A: You should do your workout first if you want to build muscle. When you do your workout first, you have more energy and can lift heavier weights, which helps to build muscle more effectively.

Q: How long should I do cardio or workout for?

A: The duration of your cardio or workout session depends on your fitness goals and your current fitness level. It's best to consult with a personal trainer or fitness expert to determine the best approach for you.

Conclusion of Should You Workout or Do Cardio First

To sum up, whether you should do cardio or workout first depends on your fitness goals. If you're looking to build muscle or increase strength, you should do your workout first. If you're looking to improve your cardiovascular health or lose weight, you should do cardio first. It's essential to listen to your body and do what works best for you. Remember, consistency is key to achieving your fitness goals.
