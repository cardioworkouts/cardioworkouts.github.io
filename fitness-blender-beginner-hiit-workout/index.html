Fitness Blender Beginner HIIT Workout: How Does It Work?

The Fitness Blender Beginner HIIT Workout works by combining high-intensity cardio and strength exercises to help you burn fat and build muscle. The workout consists of a series of intervals, with each interval lasting between 20-60 seconds. During each interval, you'll perform a high-intensity exercise, such as jumping jacks or burpees. After each interval, you'll take a short break before starting the next interval.
