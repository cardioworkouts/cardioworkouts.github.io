What To Eat Right Before Bodybuilding Competition

Written by Christine Aug 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you preparing for a bodybuilding competition and wondering what to eat right before the big day? Your diet is crucial for achieving the best results and presenting your best physique on stage. In this article, we will discuss the best foods to eat before competition day.

As a bodybuilder, you may have experienced the discomfort of bloating or feeling too full on stage. It is important to avoid these symptoms by choosing the right foods that will fuel your body and provide the necessary nutrients for optimal performance.

What to Eat Right Before Bodybuilding Competition?

When it comes to competition day, it is important to stick to foods that are easily digestible and won't cause any discomfort. Here are some of the best options:

1. Complex Carbohydrates

Complex carbohydrates such as brown rice, sweet potatoes, and quinoa are excellent choices for providing long-lasting energy. These foods will help fuel your muscles and keep you feeling full without causing bloating or discomfort.

Example:

My personal go-to meal before a competition includes a combination of brown rice, grilled chicken, and steamed vegetables. This provides the perfect balance of complex carbohydrates, protein, and fiber to keep me energized throughout the day.

2. Lean Protein

Lean protein sources such as chicken, fish, and turkey are essential for building and repairing muscle tissue. They are also easily digestible and won't cause any discomfort on stage.

Example:

One great option is grilled salmon with a side of asparagus. This meal provides a healthy dose of omega-3 fatty acids, which help reduce inflammation and improve overall health.

3. Fruits and Vegetables

Fruits and vegetables are packed with essential vitamins and minerals that can help reduce inflammation and support overall health. They are also low in calories and won't cause any bloating or discomfort.

Example:

My favorite pre-competition snack is sliced apples with almond butter. This provides a healthy dose of fiber, protein, and healthy fats to keep me feeling full and satisfied.

Question and Answer:

Q: Can I eat high-fat foods before competition?

A: It is best to avoid high-fat foods before competition as they can be difficult to digest and may cause discomfort on stage.

Q: How much should I eat before competition?

A: It is best to eat small, frequent meals throughout the day to keep your energy levels stable. Aim for a balance of complex carbohydrates, lean protein, and healthy fats.

Q: Should I eat before going on stage?

A: It is important to eat a small meal or snack before going on stage to provide your body with the necessary energy. However, make sure to choose foods that are easily digestible and won't cause any discomfort.

Q: Should I drink water before competition?

A: Staying hydrated is important for optimal performance, but make sure to avoid drinking too much water right before going on stage as it can cause bloating and discomfort.

Conclusion of What to Eat Right Before Bodybuilding Competition

In conclusion, choosing the right foods before a bodybuilding competition is crucial for achieving the best results. Stick to easily digestible foods that provide the necessary nutrients for optimal performance. Remember to stay hydrated and avoid foods that may cause discomfort or bloating.
