Can You Build Muscle While Doing Hiit

Written by Oliver Jul 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you someone who is looking to build muscle but also loves HIIT workouts? If so, you may be wondering if it is possible to build muscle while doing HIIT. It's a common question for fitness enthusiasts who want to maximize their workouts, but aren't quite sure if HIIT is the right choice for building muscle. In this article, we'll explore the answer to the question: Can you build muscle while doing HIIT?

Many people who do HIIT workouts are looking to burn fat and improve their cardiovascular health. However, if you're also looking to build muscle, you may be concerned that HIIT workouts won't be effective for that purpose. This is a common pain point for people who are trying to achieve multiple fitness goals at once.

The short answer to the question of whether you can build muscle while doing HIIT is yes, you can. However, there are some caveats to keep in mind. In order to build muscle, you need to put your muscles under tension, which means lifting heavy weights or doing exercises that require a lot of strength. HIIT workouts typically involve bodyweight exercises or light weights, so you may not be putting your muscles under enough tension to build significant muscle mass.

In summary, it is possible to build muscle while doing HIIT, but it may not be as effective as other types of workouts that focus specifically on strength training. That being said, there are ways to modify your HIIT workouts to make them more effective for building muscle.

Personal Experience with Building Muscle While Doing HIIT

As someone who loves HIIT workouts but also wants to build muscle, I've experimented with different approaches to see what works best for me. Initially, I found that my HIIT workouts were not giving me the muscle gains that I was looking for. However, I started incorporating heavier weights into my workouts and doing more strength-focused exercises, like squats and deadlifts, and I started to see more muscle growth.

How to Modify Your HIIT Workouts for Muscle Building

If you want to build muscle while doing HIIT, there are several modifications you can make to your workouts:

Incorporate Heavier Weights

As mentioned earlier, in order to build muscle you need to put your muscles under tension, and heavier weights are one way to do that. Incorporate dumbbells or barbells into your HIIT workouts to increase the resistance and challenge your muscles.

Focus on Compound Exercises

Compound exercises, like squats, deadlifts, and bench presses, work multiple muscle groups at once and are more effective for building muscle than isolation exercises. Incorporate these exercises into your HIIT workouts to maximize muscle growth.

Personal Experience with Modifying HIIT Workouts for Muscle Building

After incorporating heavier weights and compound exercises into my HIIT workouts, I started to see more muscle growth. I also made sure to eat enough protein and get enough rest to support muscle recovery and growth.

Can You Build Muscle While Doing HIIT? – Question and Answer Section

Q: Is it possible to build muscle with bodyweight HIIT workouts?

A: Yes, it is possible, but you may need to increase the number of reps or sets to put your muscles under enough tension to stimulate muscle growth.

Q: Should I do HIIT or strength training if I want to build muscle?

A: Both HIIT and strength training can be effective for building muscle, but strength training is generally more effective because it allows you to lift heavier weights and put your muscles under more tension.

Q: How often should I do HIIT workouts if I want to build muscle?

A: It's best to incorporate HIIT workouts into your overall fitness routine, but if you're specifically looking to build muscle, aim to do strength training workouts at least 2-3 times per week.

Q: Should I eat more protein if I want to build muscle while doing HIIT?

A: Yes, protein is essential for muscle recovery and growth. Aim to eat a diet that is high in protein, and consider adding a protein supplement if you're not getting enough through food alone.

Conclusion of Can You Build Muscle While Doing HIIT

In conclusion, it is possible to build muscle while doing HIIT, but it may not be as effective as other types of workouts that focus specifically on strength training. However, by incorporating heavier weights and compound exercises into your HIIT workouts, you can maximize your muscle growth potential. Remember to also eat enough protein and get enough rest to support muscle recovery and growth.
