Are you tired of doing the same old strength training routine? Do you want to switch things up and challenge yourself with new exercises? Look no further than the best strength training apps free! These apps offer a variety of workouts that can be done from the comfort of your own home without any equipment.

Strength training can seem intimidating, especially if you're new to it. It takes time, effort, and dedication to build muscle and improve your overall strength. But with the right tools and guidance, anyone can achieve their fitness goals. That's where the best strength training apps free come in.

If you're looking for an app that can help you build muscle, burn fat, and improve your overall fitness, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll discuss the best strength training apps free and their features, so you can choose the one that best fits your needs.

In summary, the best strength training apps free offer a variety of workouts that can be done from home without any equipment. These apps are perfect for anyone who wants to switch up their routine, challenge themselves, and improve their overall strength and fitness.

1. Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club is one of the best strength training apps free that offers a variety of workouts for all fitness levels. The app features over 185 free workouts that can be done from home or at the gym. The workouts range from bodyweight exercises to HIIT workouts, and everything in between.

What sets Nike Training Club apart from other apps is its personalized training programs. The app will create a custom workout plan based on your fitness level, goals, and available equipment. The app also includes audio and visual guidance from Nike Master Trainers to ensure proper form and technique.

Personally, I've been using Nike Training Club for over a year and have seen significant improvements in my strength and endurance. The app is easy to use, and the workouts are challenging but not overwhelming. I highly recommend this app for anyone looking to improve their overall fitness.

2. FitOn

FitOn is another great option for those looking for the best strength training apps free. The app features a variety of workouts, including strength training, cardio, yoga, and Pilates. The app also offers personalized workout plans and challenges to help you achieve your fitness goals.

What I love about FitOn is its community aspect. The app allows you to connect with friends and other users to share your progress and support each other. The app also has a variety of celebrity trainers, including Cassey Ho and Jeanette Jenkins, who lead the workouts and provide motivation.

I've been using FitOn for a few months now and have enjoyed trying out different workouts and challenges. The app is user-friendly and offers a lot of variety, which keeps me motivated and engaged.

3. JEFIT

JEFIT is a strength training app that offers over 1,300 exercises and routines for all fitness levels. The app features a workout planner, progress tracker, and personalized training programs. The app also has a social aspect, allowing you to connect with other users and share your progress.

What sets JEFIT apart from other apps is its focus on weightlifting. The app includes detailed instructions and animations for each exercise to ensure proper form and technique. The app also allows you to track your progress and set goals, making it a great option for those looking to build muscle and improve their lifting technique.

I've used JEFIT in the past and found it to be a great resource for weightlifting workouts. The app is easy to use and offers a lot of variety, which keeps things interesting.

4. Sworkit

Sworkit is a fitness app that offers a variety of workouts, including strength training, cardio, and yoga. The app allows you to customize your workouts based on your fitness level, goals, and available equipment. The app also includes audio and visual guidance to ensure proper form and technique.

What I love about Sworkit is its flexibility. The app allows you to choose the length of your workout, from 5 minutes to an hour, making it easy to fit a workout into your busy schedule. The app also offers a variety of challenges and workout plans to keep you motivated.

I've used Sworkit in the past and found it to be a great option for quick, effective workouts. The app is easy to use and offers a lot of variety, which keeps things interesting.
