60 Tips 1 Month Calisthenics Workout Plan For Advanced Weight Training

Written by Aprilia Jul 09, 2023 Â· 5 min read

In this tutorial, you will learn a comprehensive 1 month calisthenics workout plan in relaxed English language. This workout plan is ideal for beginners who want to build strength, burn fat, and improve their overall fitness level.

Starting a new workout routine can be challenging, especially if you don't know where to begin. The thought of committing to a workout plan for a whole month can seem daunting, but don't worry, we've got you covered.

The target of this 1 month calisthenics workout plan is to help you improve your strength, endurance, and overall fitness level. Calisthenics is a form of exercise that uses your body weight to build muscle and increase flexibility. It is an excellent way to get fit without needing any expensive equipment or gym membership.

In summary, this tutorial will provide a 1 month calisthenics workout plan that is designed for beginners. The workout plan will target your entire body and aims to increase your strength, endurance, and overall fitness level using your body weight.

1 Month Calisthenics Workout Plan

When I first started with calisthenics, I found it challenging to put together a proper workout routine. I was confused about which exercises to do, and how often to do them. Over time, I learned what works and what doesn't, and I want to share my experience with you.

Here is a 1-month workout plan that you can follow:

Week 1: Full-Body Workouts

For the first week, you will be doing full-body workouts. These workouts will target all your major muscle groups and help you build overall strength. Here is a sample workout you can do:

Monday, Wednesday, and Friday:

  • 10 x Push-Ups
  • 10 x Squats
  • 10 x Lunges
  • 10 x Dips
  • 10 x Leg Raises
  • 10 x Plank

Do each exercise for 3 sets with 30 seconds rest in between each set. As you progress, you can add more sets or reps to each exercise.

Week 2: Upper Body and Core Workouts

For the second week, you will be focusing on your upper body and core. These workouts will help you build upper body strength and improve your posture. Here is a sample workout you can do:

Monday, Wednesday, and Friday:

  • 10 x Push-Ups
  • 10 x Pull-Ups or Inverted Rows
  • 10 x Dips
  • 10 x Plank
  • 10 x Leg Raises
  • 10 x Russian Twists

Do each exercise for 3 sets with 30 seconds rest in between each set. As you progress, you can add more sets or reps to each exercise.

Week 3: Lower Body and Cardio Workouts

For the third week, you will be focusing on your lower body and cardio. These workouts will help you build leg strength and improve your cardiovascular health. Here is a sample workout you can do:

Monday, Wednesday, and Friday:

  • 10 x Squats
  • 10 x Lunges
  • 10 x Calf Raises
  • 10 x Jumping Jacks
  • 10 x Burpees
  • 10 x High Knees

Do each exercise for 3 sets with 30 seconds rest in between each set. As you progress, you can add more sets or reps to each exercise.

Week 4: Full-Body Workouts

For the final week, you will be doing full-body workouts again. This time, you can add more intensity and variation to your workouts. Here is a sample workout you can do:

Monday, Wednesday, and Friday:

  • 10 x Push-Ups
  • 10 x Pull-Ups or Inverted Rows
  • 10 x Squats
  • 10 x Lunges
  • 10 x Dips
  • 10 x Leg Raises
  • 10 x Plank
  • 10 x Burpees

Do each exercise for 3 sets with 30 seconds rest in between each set. As you progress, you can add more sets or reps to each exercise.

Benefits of Calisthenics

Calisthenics has many benefits, including:

  • Improving strength and endurance
  • Burning fat and building muscle
  • Improving flexibility and range of motion
  • Requiring no expensive equipment or gym membership
  • Can be done anywhere, anytime

How to Progress with Calisthenics

If you're looking to progress with calisthenics, there are a few things you can do:

  • Add more reps or sets to each exercise
  • Try more challenging variations of each exercise
  • Take shorter rests in between sets
  • Add weights to your exercises

Tips for a Successful 1 Month Calisthenics Workout Plan

Here are some tips to help you succeed with your 1 month calisthenics workout plan:

  • Stay consistent with your workouts
  • Track your progress and celebrate your wins
  • Eat a healthy diet to fuel your workouts
  • Rest and recover properly
  • Stay hydrated

Question and Answer

Q: What if I can't do some of the exercises?

A: That's okay! You can modify the exercises or do easier variations until you build up enough strength to do the full exercise.

Q: How often should I do the 1 month calisthenics workout plan?

A: It depends on your fitness level and goals. You can do the workouts 3-5 times per week, with at least one rest day in between.

Q: Can I add cardio to my 1 month calisthenics workout plan?

A: Yes, you can! Cardio is an excellent way to burn fat and improve your cardiovascular health. You can add cardio on your rest days or do it after your workouts.

Q: How long will it take to see results?

A: It depends on your fitness level, diet, and consistency. With proper nutrition and consistent workouts, you should start seeing results within a few weeks.

Conclusion of 1 Month Calisthenics Workout Plan

The 1 month calisthenics workout plan is an excellent way to improve your strength, endurance, and overall fitness level using your body weight. With consistency and dedication, you can achieve your fitness goals and see results in a short period. Remember to take rest days, eat a healthy diet, and stay hydrated to maximize your results. Good luck on your fitness journey!
