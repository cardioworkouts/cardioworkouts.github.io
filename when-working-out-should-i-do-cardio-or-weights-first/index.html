When Working Out Should I Do Cardio Or Weights First

Written by Oliver Aug 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you someone who is confused about whether to start with cardio or weights during your workout session? Well, you are not alone. Many people cannot decide which one to do first as both cardio and weightlifting are crucial for a fit and healthy body. In this post, we will guide you on when working out should i do cardio or weights first and related keywords.

It can be frustrating to see no progress despite putting in hours at the gym. Choosing the wrong order of exercises can be a reason for this. It is essential to understand that both cardio and weightlifting have their benefits and are necessary for a complete workout. However, the order in which you perform them can affect your fitness goals.

The answer to when working out should i do cardio or weights first is that it depends on your fitness goals. If your goal is to build strength and gain muscle mass, then you should start with weightlifting. On the other hand, if you aim to lose weight and burn calories, then you should start with cardio.

In summary, when working out should i do cardio or weights first, the order depends on your fitness goals. Starting with weights helps build muscles, and starting with cardio helps in burning calories. It is essential to have a clear idea of your fitness goals before deciding on the order.

Starting with Weights

If your goal is to get stronger and gain muscle mass, then you should start with weightlifting. When you start with weights, your body has more energy to perform heavy lifts. Also, lifting weights first can help activate the central nervous system, which enhances muscle contraction and leads to better results.

Starting with Cardio

Starting your workout with cardio can help you burn more calories and lose weight. When you do cardio, your heart rate increases, and your body starts burning calories. Starting with cardio can also help you warm up, making it easier for you to lift weights later.

Combining Cardio and Weights

Combining cardio and weights is an excellent way to achieve overall fitness. However, the order in which you perform them is crucial. If your goal is to build muscle and strength, then you should lift weights first and then do cardio. If your goal is to lose weight and burn calories, then you should do cardio first and then lift weights.

How to Incorporate Cardio and Weights in Your Workout Routine

The best way to incorporate cardio and weights in your workout routine is to alternate between them. You can do weightlifting on one day and cardio on the other. If you want to combine them, then you can perform strength training and cardio on alternate days. It is essential to give your body enough time to recover between workouts.

Personal Experience

As a fitness enthusiast, I have tried both starting with cardio and weights. When I started lifting weights first, I noticed that I could perform heavy lifts with more ease. I also saw that my muscle mass increased. On the other hand, when I started with cardio, I felt more energized and burned more calories during my workout.

Question and Answer

Q. Does the order of exercises affect the number of calories burned?

A. Yes, the order of exercises can affect the number of calories burned. Starting with cardio can help you burn more calories during your workout, while starting with weights can help you burn more calories after your workout.

Q. Can starting with weights help improve cardio performance?

A. Yes, starting with weights can help improve cardio performance. When you lift weights, you increase muscle mass, which can help you perform cardio exercises with more ease.

Q. How long should I wait between cardio and weightlifting?

A. It is recommended to wait for at least 30 minutes between cardio and weightlifting. This will help your body recover and get ready for the next workout.

Q. Can I do cardio and weights on the same day?

A. Yes, you can do cardio and weights on the same day. However, make sure to give your body enough time to recover between workouts.

Conclusion of When Working Out Should I Do Cardio or Weights First

When working out, it is essential to understand your fitness goals and choose the right order of exercises. Starting with weights can help build muscle mass, while starting with cardio can help burn calories. Combining both is an excellent way to achieve overall fitness. It is recommended to alternate between them and give your body enough time to recover between workouts.
