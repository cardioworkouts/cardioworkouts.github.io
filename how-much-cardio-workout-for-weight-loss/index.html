How Much Cardio Workout For Weight Loss

Written by Frank Sep 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling to lose weight despite your regular workouts? Do you feel like you’re not doing enough cardio to achieve your weight loss goals? You’re not alone. Many people are unsure about how much cardio they should be doing to see significant weight loss results.

Cardio is an essential part of any weight loss program. It helps to burn calories, boost your metabolism, and improve your cardiovascular health. However, the amount of cardio you need to do to lose weight depends on several factors, including your age, weight, fitness level, and weight loss goals.

So, how much cardio workout for weight loss is optimal? Let’s find out.

Firstly, it is important to note that the American Heart Association recommends at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio per week for overall cardiovascular health. But for weight loss, you may need to increase this amount. As a general rule of thumb, aim for 250-300 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio per week to see significant weight loss results. This can include activities such as brisk walking, jogging, cycling, swimming, or dancing.

In addition to the duration of your cardio workouts, the intensity also plays a role in weight loss. High-intensity cardio workouts are more effective for weight loss than low-intensity workouts. This is because high-intensity workouts burn more calories in a shorter amount of time and increase your metabolism post-workout. So, if you’re short on time, consider doing high-intensity interval training (HIIT) for maximum weight loss results.

My Personal Experience

When I first started my weight loss journey, I was doing 30 minutes of cardio per day, five days a week. However, after a few weeks, I noticed that I wasn’t seeing significant weight loss results. I decided to increase my cardio to 60 minutes per day, six days a week, and I started to see a difference in my weight loss progress. I also incorporated HIIT workouts into my routine, which helped to boost my weight loss even further.

How Much Cardio for Beginners?

If you’re a beginner, it is best to start slow and gradually increase your cardio workout over time. Aim for 30 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio three times a week, and gradually increase the duration and frequency as you become fitter. Remember to listen to your body and take rest days when needed.

Factors to Consider

Other factors to consider when determining how much cardio workout for weight loss include your diet, sleep, stress levels, and strength training routine. A balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, fiber, and healthy fats is essential for weight loss. Aim for at least seven hours of sleep per night, as lack of sleep can lead to weight gain. High-stress levels can also hinder weight loss progress, so try to incorporate stress-reducing activities into your routine, such as yoga or meditation. Lastly, strength training is important for building muscle, which can increase your metabolism and help you burn more calories at rest.

How to Incorporate Cardio into Your Routine?

If you’re struggling to incorporate cardio into your routine, try to make it more enjoyable by doing activities that you enjoy, such as dancing or hiking. You can also try to break up your cardio workouts into smaller chunks throughout the day, such as doing 10 minutes of jumping jacks in the morning and 10 minutes of cycling in the evening. Lastly, try to find a workout buddy or join a fitness class to keep you motivated and accountable.

FAQs

Q: Can I do too much cardio?

A: Yes, doing too much cardio can lead to overtraining, which can cause injury and hinder weight loss progress. It is important to listen to your body and take rest days when needed.

Q: Is cardio or strength training better for weight loss?

A: Both cardio and strength training are important for weight loss. Cardio helps to burn calories and improve cardiovascular health, while strength training helps to build muscle and increase metabolism.

Q: How often should I do cardio for weight loss?

A: Aim for 250-300 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio per week for weight loss.

Q: Can I lose weight with just cardio?

A: While cardio is important for weight loss, it is not enough on its own. A balanced diet and strength training are also essential for significant weight loss results.

Conclusion

In conclusion, how much cardio workout for weight loss depends on several factors, including your age, weight, fitness level, and weight loss goals. Aim for at least 250-300 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio per week, and gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts over time. Remember to also incorporate strength training, eat a balanced diet, and take care of your sleep and stress levels for optimal weight loss results.
