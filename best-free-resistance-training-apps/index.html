Best Free Resistance Training Apps A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Sep 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to get into shape but don't want to spend a fortune on a personal trainer or gym membership? Look no further than the best free resistance training apps! These apps can help you create a personalized workout plan, track your progress, and stay motivated to reach your fitness goals. In this guide, we'll explore the top free resistance training apps and how they can help you achieve your fitness goals.

When it comes to fitness, one of the biggest pain points is finding a workout plan that fits your needs and schedule. Many people struggle to find the time or motivation to go to the gym regularly, while others may not know where to start when it comes to creating a workout plan. That's where the best free resistance training apps come in - they provide an easy and accessible way to create a personalized workout plan and track your progress.

If you're looking for the best free resistance training apps, we recommend starting with the following:

1. Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club is a free app that offers a wide variety of workouts, ranging from bodyweight exercises to full gym workouts. The app features personalized training plans based on your fitness level and goals, as well as video demonstrations of each exercise. You can also track your progress and share your workouts with friends for added motivation.

2. JEFIT

JEFIT is another popular free resistance training app that offers a library of exercises and workout plans. The app features personalized workout plans based on your fitness level and goals, as well as the ability to track your progress and connect with a community of users for added motivation.

3. Fitbod

Fitbod is a free app that creates personalized workout plans based on your fitness level and goals. The app features a library of over 300 exercises and uses machine learning to adjust your workout plan based on your progress. Fitbod also offers the ability to track your progress and connect with a community of users for added motivation.

4. MyFitnessPal

While not specifically a resistance training app, MyFitnessPal is a free app that can help you track your nutrition and fitness goals. The app features a database of over 6 million foods, as well as the ability to track your workouts and connect with a community of users for added motivation.

Overall, the best free resistance training apps can help you create a personalized workout plan, track your progress, and stay motivated to reach your fitness goals. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced fitness enthusiast, these apps can help you achieve your fitness goals without breaking the bank.

What are the benefits of using free resistance training apps?

Using free resistance training apps can offer a variety of benefits, including:

  • Creating a personalized workout plan
  • Tracking your progress
  • Staying motivated with reminders and notifications
  • Connecting with a community of users for added support and motivation

Can free resistance training apps replace a personal trainer?

While free resistance training apps can offer personalized workout plans and track your progress, they cannot replace the expertise and guidance of a personal trainer. If you're new to resistance training or have specific fitness goals, it may be beneficial to work with a personal trainer to ensure proper form and technique.

How often should I use free resistance training apps?

The frequency at which you use free resistance training apps will depend on your fitness goals and schedule. However, most experts recommend resistance training at least 2-3 times per week for optimal results.

What if I don't have access to gym equipment?

Many free resistance training apps offer bodyweight exercises that require little to no equipment. If you don't have access to weights or gym equipment, consider trying a bodyweight workout or investing in a set of resistance bands.

Conclusion of Best Free Resistance Training Apps

Overall, the best free resistance training apps can help you create a personalized workout plan, track your progress, and stay motivated to reach your fitness goals. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced fitness enthusiast, these apps offer an easy and accessible way to achieve your fitness goals without breaking the bank.
