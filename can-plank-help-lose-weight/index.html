Can Plank Help Lose Weight

Written by Petter Sep 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Losing weight can be a challenging and overwhelming task. There are countless diets, exercises, and weight loss products out there that claim to help you shed those extra pounds. However, not all of them are effective, and some can even be harmful to your health. One popular exercise that has been gaining attention lately is the plank. But can plank help lose weight?

People who struggle with weight loss often experience various pain points. These can include feeling self-conscious about their appearance, low self-esteem, difficulty finding clothes that fit properly, and health complications such as high blood pressure, diabetes, and heart disease.

The short answer is yes, plank can help lose weight. Plank is a full-body exercise that targets multiple muscle groups simultaneously, including the core, shoulders, arms, and legs. This means that you burn more calories in a shorter amount of time, making it an efficient way to lose weight. Additionally, plank helps improve your posture and balance, strengthens your back muscles, and reduces the risk of injury.

In summary, plank is an effective exercise for weight loss. It targets multiple muscle groups, burns more calories in less time, and has numerous other health benefits. However, it's important to note that plank alone is not enough to lose weight. To achieve your weight loss goals, you need to combine exercise with a healthy diet and lifestyle.

How Does Plank Help Lose Weight?

As mentioned earlier, plank is a full-body exercise that targets multiple muscle groups simultaneously. When you hold a plank position, your entire body is engaged, and you're using your own body weight as resistance. This means that you burn more calories than you would with isolated exercises that target only one muscle group.

Personally, I've been incorporating plank into my workout routine for several months now, and I've noticed a significant difference in my body composition. My core is stronger, my posture is better, and I've lost a few inches around my waistline. However, it's important to note that I also follow a healthy diet and lifestyle, which includes eating whole foods, drinking plenty of water, and getting enough sleep.

How Often Should You Do Plank?

How often you should do plank depends on your fitness level and goals. If you're just starting, it's recommended to start with 30-second holds and gradually work your way up to 60 seconds or more. Aim to do plank at least 3-4 times per week, and incorporate other exercises that target different muscle groups for a well-rounded workout routine.

Tips for Doing Plank Correctly

To get the most out of your plank exercise, it's important to do it correctly. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Keep your elbows directly under your shoulders
  • Maintain a straight line from your head to your heels
  • Engage your core muscles and squeeze your glutes
  • Breathe deeply and evenly throughout the exercise

How to Make Plank More Challenging

If you find that plank is becoming too easy, there are several ways to make it more challenging. Here are some options:

  • Try different variations, such as side plank or plank with leg lifts
  • Incorporate props, such as a stability ball or resistance band
  • Hold the plank position for longer periods of time

Can Plank Help Lose Weight? - Q&A

Q: Is plank suitable for beginners?
A: Yes, plank can be modified to suit different fitness levels. It's important to start with shorter holds and work your way up to longer ones as you build strength and endurance.

Q: Can you lose weight with plank alone?
A: While plank is an effective exercise for weight loss, it's important to combine it with a healthy diet and lifestyle to achieve your weight loss goals.

Q: How long should you hold a plank?
A: It's recommended to start with 30-second holds and gradually work your way up to 60 seconds or more.

Q: How many times per week should you do plank?
A: Aim to do plank at least 3-4 times per week, and incorporate other exercises that target different muscle groups for a well-rounded workout routine.

Conclusion of Can Plank Help Lose Weight

In conclusion, plank is an effective exercise for weight loss. It targets multiple muscle groups, burns more calories in less time, and has numerous other health benefits. However, it's important to combine plank with a healthy diet and lifestyle to achieve your weight loss goals. By incorporating plank into your workout routine and making healthy choices, you can reach your desired weight and improve your overall health and wellbeing.
