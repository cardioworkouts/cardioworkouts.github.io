How Often Should You Do Cardio Exercise A Week A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you wondering how often you should do cardio exercise a week? You're not alone. There are so many different opinions out there that it can be hard to figure out what's right for you. But don't worry, we're here to help.

The American Heart Association recommends that adults get at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic exercise or 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity aerobic exercise per week. This can be spread out over the course of the week however you like. For example, you could do 30 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise five days a week, or you could do 25 minutes of vigorous-intensity exercise three days a week.

In addition to the recommendations from the American Heart Association, it's important to listen to your body. If you're just starting out with exercise, you may need to start slow and gradually increase your intensity and duration over time. If you're an experienced exerciser, you may be able to handle more than the recommended amount of exercise.

To summarize, the general guideline is to aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic exercise or 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity aerobic exercise per week, but it's important to listen to your body and adjust as needed.

My Experience with Cardio Exercise

Personally, I aim for 30 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise five days a week. I find that this is a manageable amount for me and helps me feel energized throughout the day. I also mix up my workouts so that I'm not doing the same thing every day. Some days I'll go for a run, other days I'll do a cardio workout video, and other days I'll go for a long walk.

The Benefits of Cardio Exercise

Cardio exercise has numerous benefits, both for your physical health and your mental health. Here are just a few:

-Improved heart health

-Lowered risk of chronic diseases such as diabetes and high blood pressure

-Improved mood and decreased symptoms of depression and anxiety

-Increased energy and stamina

-Improved sleep quality

Tips for Incorporating Cardio Exercise into Your Routine

If you're new to cardio exercise, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. Here are a few tips:

-Start slow and gradually increase your intensity and duration

-Find an activity you enjoy so that you're more likely to stick with it

-Mix up your workouts to keep things interesting

-Schedule your workouts at a time that works for you and stick to that schedule as much as possible

How to Make Cardio Exercise a Habit

It can be hard to make exercise a habit, but here are a few tips that have worked for me:

-Schedule your workouts into your calendar

-Find an accountability partner

-Start small and gradually increase your frequency and duration

-Reward yourself after a workout

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, the general guideline for how often you should do cardio exercise a week is at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic exercise or 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity aerobic exercise per week. However, it's important to listen to your body and adjust as needed. Incorporating cardio exercise into your routine can have numerous physical and mental health benefits, and with a few simple tips, you can make it a habit that sticks.

Question and Answer

Q: Can you do too much cardio exercise?

A: Yes, it is possible to do too much cardio exercise. Overdoing it can lead to injuries, burnout, and other health issues. It's important to listen to your body and not push yourself too hard.

Q: How many days a week should I do cardio exercise?

A: Aim for at least three days a week, but ideally, you should aim for five to six days a week.

Q: Is it better to do cardio exercise in the morning or evening?

A: There's no one-size-fits-all answer to this question. It really depends on what works for you and your schedule. Some people prefer to exercise in the morning to jumpstart their day, while others prefer to exercise in the evening to relieve stress after a long day.

Q: How long should a cardio workout be?

A: Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic exercise per workout. If you're doing vigorous-intensity aerobic exercise, you can aim for 20-25 minutes per workout.

Conclusion of How Often Should You Do Cardio Exercise a Week

Cardio exercise is an important part of a healthy lifestyle. By aiming for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic exercise or 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity aerobic exercise per week and listening to your body, you can reap the numerous physical and mental health benefits of cardio exercise. With a few simple tips, you can make cardio exercise a habit that sticks and enjoy the benefits for years to come.
