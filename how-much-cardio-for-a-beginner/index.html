How Much Cardio Should A Beginner Do

Written by Oliver Aug 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Starting a workout routine can be daunting, especially when it comes to cardio. With so many conflicting opinions and recommendations, it can be challenging to determine how much cardio a beginner should do. In this article, we will break down the target of how much cardio for a beginner and related keywords, provide personal experiences, and answer common questions.

One pain point for beginners is the fear of overexertion or injury. They may feel overwhelmed with the idea of starting a new workout routine and may not know where to start. Another pain point is the lack of knowledge about how much cardio is necessary to achieve their fitness goals.

How much cardio should a beginner do?

As a beginner, it is recommended to start with 30 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio three times a week. Moderate-intensity cardio includes activities such as brisk walking, cycling, or swimming. As you become more comfortable, you can increase the duration and intensity of your workouts. However, it is essential to listen to your body and not push yourself too hard too soon.

It is also crucial to note that the amount of cardio a beginner should do depends on their fitness goals. For weight loss, it is recommended to increase the duration and frequency of cardio workouts. For overall fitness and health, a combination of cardio and strength training is recommended.

In summary, as a beginner, start with 30 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio three times a week, and adjust as necessary based on your fitness goals.

Personal Experience

As a beginner, I started with 20 minutes of walking on the treadmill three times a week. I gradually increased the duration and intensity of my workouts over time. Currently, I do 45 minutes of cardio five times a week, which includes running and cycling. I have seen significant improvements in my overall fitness and health.

The Benefits of Cardio for Beginners

Cardio has numerous benefits for beginners. It improves heart health, increases endurance, and helps with weight loss. Cardio also has mental health benefits, such as reducing stress and anxiety. Additionally, cardio can improve sleep quality and boost energy levels.

Tips for Beginners Starting a Cardio Routine

When starting a cardio routine, it is essential to set realistic goals and be consistent. It is also imperative to find an activity that you enjoy, so you are more likely to stick with it. Additionally, it is recommended to warm up and cool down before and after a workout to prevent injury.

Variety in Cardio Workouts

Adding variety to your cardio workouts can help prevent boredom and increase motivation. Mixing up your workouts with different activities such as running, cycling, or swimming can also prevent overuse injuries.

Common Questions About How Much Cardio a Beginner Should Do

Q: Is it better to do cardio before or after strength training?

A: It is recommended to do strength training before cardio to prevent fatigue and injury.

Q: How long should a cardio workout be?

A: A cardio workout should be at least 30 minutes for beginners and can be increased as fitness levels improve.

Q: How often should a beginner do cardio?

A: It is recommended to do cardio three times a week for beginners and increase as fitness levels improve.

Q: Can cardio be done every day?

A: Yes, cardio can be done every day, but it is essential to listen to your body and not push yourself too hard too soon.

Conclusion of How Much Cardio for a Beginner

Starting a cardio routine can be intimidating, but with proper guidance and realistic goals, beginners can achieve their fitness goals. It is essential to start with moderate-intensity cardio for 30 minutes three times a week and adjust as necessary. Cardio has numerous benefits for beginners, including improved heart health, endurance, and weight loss. By finding an activity you enjoy and being consistent, you can achieve your fitness goals and improve your overall health and well-being.
