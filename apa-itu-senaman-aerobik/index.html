What is the Target of Senaman Aerobik?

Senaman aerobik, also known as aerobic exercise, is a type of physical activity that involves continuous movement of large muscle groups over an extended period of time. This can include activities such as walking, running, cycling, swimming, dancing, or using cardio machines like treadmills or ellipticals. The goal of senaman aerobik is to increase your heart rate and breathing rate, which helps to improve your cardiovascular fitness and burn calories.

Research has shown that regular aerobic exercise can have a range of health benefits, including:

  • Improving your mood and reducing symptoms of depression and anxiety
  • Boosting your immune system and improving your overall health
  • Helping you to maintain a healthy weight and body composition

If you're interested in trying senaman aerobik for the first time, there are a few things that you should keep in mind. Q: How often should I do senaman aerobik?

A: It's recommended that adults get at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic exercise or 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity aerobic exercise per week, spread out over at least three days.
