The Best Home Cardio Workout Videos To Get Your Heart Pumping

Written by Petter Aug 23, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking for a way to get in shape without leaving the house? Do you want to burn calories and work up a sweat in the comfort of your own living room? If so, you're not alone. Many people are searching for the best home cardio workout videos to help them meet their fitness goals. Here's what you need to know.

The target of the best home cardio workout video is to provide a high-intensity, full-body workout that gets your heart rate up and burns calories. These videos are designed to simulate the experience of a fitness class or personal training session, but in the privacy of your own home. They offer a range of exercises, from traditional cardio moves like jumping jacks and burpees to strength training exercises like squats and lunges.

So, what are some of the best home cardio workout videos on the market today? Here are a few top picks:

1. Beachbody's Insanity

Beachbody's Insanity is a high-intensity interval training (HIIT) workout that promises to transform your body in just 60 days. The program consists of 10 DVDs, each with a different workout, as well as a nutrition guide and workout calendar. The workouts are led by Shaun T, a personal trainer and fitness expert, and are designed to push you to your limits.

When I first tried Beachbody's Insanity, I was skeptical. I had tried other home workout videos before, but I always felt like I wasn't getting a real workout. But with Insanity, I was sweating buckets within the first few minutes. The workouts are challenging, but the results are worth it.

2. Jillian Michaels' 30 Day Shred

Jillian Michaels' 30 Day Shred is a 20-minute workout that promises to help you lose up to 20 pounds in just 30 days. The program consists of three levels, each with a different workout, and is designed to be done for 30 days straight. The workouts include a mix of cardio and strength training exercises.

I've been a fan of Jillian Michaels for years, and her 30 Day Shred is one of my favorite home workout videos. The workouts are short but intense, and I always feel like I've gotten a full-body workout when I'm done.

3. P90X

P90X is a 90-day program that promises to transform your body with a combination of cardio, strength training, and yoga. The program consists of 12 DVDs, each with a different workout, as well as a nutrition guide and workout calendar. The workouts are led by Tony Horton, a personal trainer and fitness expert.

I've never tried P90X myself, but I've heard great things about it from friends who have. The workouts are challenging but effective, and the program is designed to keep you motivated for the full 90 days.

4. Zumba Fitness

Zumba Fitness is a dance-based workout that combines Latin rhythms with cardio exercise. The program consists of DVDs, as well as live classes at gyms and fitness studios around the world. The workouts are led by licensed Zumba instructors and are designed to be fun and easy to follow.

Zumba Fitness is a great option if you're looking for a cardio workout that doesn't feel like exercise. The music is upbeat and the dance moves are easy to pick up, so you'll be having fun while getting in shape.

5. Cardio Kickboxing

Cardio kickboxing is a high-energy workout that combines martial arts with cardio exercise. The program is available on DVD and is led by personal trainers and fitness experts. The workouts include a mix of punches, kicks, and other martial arts moves, as well as cardio exercises like jumping jacks and burpees.

Cardio kickboxing is a great way to get in shape while learning some self-defense moves. The workouts are challenging but fun, and you'll feel like a badass when you're done.

How to Get the Most Out of Your Home Cardio Workout

Now that you know some of the best home cardio workout videos, how can you get the most out of your workouts? Here are a few tips:

- Set aside a dedicated workout space. Whether it's a corner of your living room or a spare bedroom, having a dedicated space for your workouts can help you stay motivated and focused.

- Schedule your workouts. Treat your home cardio workouts like any other appointment and put them on your calendar. This will help you stay accountable and make sure you don't skip workouts.

- Mix it up. Don't do the same workout every day. Mix it up with different videos or types of exercise to keep things interesting and challenge your body.

- Stay hydrated. Make sure you drink plenty of water before, during, and after your workouts to stay hydrated.

Conclusion of Best Home Cardio Workout Video

At the end of the day, the best home cardio workout video is the one that works for you. Whether you prefer dance-based workouts, martial arts-inspired workouts, or high-intensity interval training, there's a video out there that will help you meet your fitness goals. So, pick one (or a few!) and get ready to sweat.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I do these workouts if I'm a beginner?

A: Yes! All of the workouts listed above offer modifications for beginners, so you can start at your own pace.

Q: Do I need any special equipment?

A: Some of the workouts require dumbbells or resistance bands, but most can be done with just your bodyweight.

Q: How often should I do these workouts?

A: Aim for at least three days a week, but listen to your body and don't push yourself too hard.

Q: Can I combine these workouts with other forms of exercise?

A: Absolutely! These workouts can be a great addition to your existing exercise routine.
