Question and Answer

Q: Do I need an expensive exercise bike to use an exercise bike app?

A: No, you don't need an expensive exercise bike to use an exercise bike app. You can use any stationary bike or even a regular bike with a bike trainer.

Q: Can I use an exercise bike app without a heart rate monitor?

A: Yes, you can use an exercise bike app without a heart rate monitor. However, a heart rate monitor can provide more accurate information about your workout and help you stay within your target heart rate zone.

Q: Are free exercise bike apps as effective as paid ones?

A: Yes, free exercise bike apps can be as effective as paid ones. It all depends on the features and quality of the app.
