How to Create Your Own Best Spotify EDM Workout Playlist

If none of the pre-made playlists on Spotify suit your needs, don't worry! You can easily create your own custom playlist that is tailored specifically to your workout style and preferences. Here's how:

1. Start by selecting your favorite EDM tracks on Spotify. You can either search for specific songs or browse through pre-made playlists to find the perfect tracks for your workout.

2. Once you have selected your tracks, create a new playlist and add your chosen tracks to it.

3. Arrange your tracks in an order that makes sense for your workout. For example, you may want to start with slower tracks for your warm-up and gradually increase the tempo as you get into your workout.

4. Give your playlist a name and save it for future use.

With these simple steps, you can create a custom playlist that is perfect for your workout needs.
