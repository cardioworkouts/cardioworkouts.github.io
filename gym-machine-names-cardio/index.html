Treadmill

The treadmill is perhaps the most well-known gym machine names cardio. It's a stationary machine with a conveyor belt that allows you to walk or run in place. Stationary Bike

The stationary bike is another popular gym machine names cardio. It's a stationary machine that mimics the movement of a bicycle. You can adjust the resistance level, speed, and incline to customize your workout. I like the stationary bike because it's low-impact, which means it's gentle on your joints. Elliptical Machine

The elliptical machine is a low-impact gym machine names cardio that provides a full-body workout. I like the elliptical machine because it works my arms, legs, and core simultaneously.

The stair climber is a challenging gym machine names cardio that simulates the movement of climbing stairs. You can adjust the speed and resistance level to make your workout more difficult. The stair climber is great for toning your legs and glutes, and improving your cardiovascular endurance. I usually use the stair climber when I want to push myself to the limit.
