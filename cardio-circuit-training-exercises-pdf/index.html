The Target of Cardio Circuit Training Exercises PDF

Cardio circuit training exercises PDF is a form of high-intensity interval training (HIIT) that includes a series of exercises performed in a specific order with little to no rest in between. The goal is to keep your heart rate elevated throughout the workout to burn calories and improve cardiovascular health.

Cardio circuit training exercises PDF typically consists of five to ten exercises that work different muscle groups, such as squats, lunges, push-ups, and burpees. The exercises are performed for a set amount of time, usually between 30 seconds to a minute, before moving on to the next exercise. The entire circuit is repeated two to three times, with little to no rest in between.
