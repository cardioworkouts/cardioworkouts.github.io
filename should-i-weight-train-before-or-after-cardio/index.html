Should I Weight Train Before Or After Cardio

Written by Eveline Sep 19, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you someone who loves to work out but often finds yourself confused about whether you should do weight training or cardio first? If yes, then you are not alone. Many fitness enthusiasts face this dilemma every day.

Some people believe that it is better to do cardio before weight training, while others argue that weight training should come first. However, the truth is that there is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question. The answer depends on your fitness goals, the type of workout you are doing, and your overall fitness level.

There are many factors to consider when deciding whether to weight train before or after cardio. In this article, we will discuss the pros and cons of each option, and help you decide which is best for you.

Weight training and cardio are both essential components of a workout routine. Weight training helps build muscle, increases strength, and boosts metabolism, while cardio improves cardiovascular health, helps burn calories, and promotes weight loss. Therefore, it is important to understand how each type of exercise affects your body before deciding which one to do first.

My Personal Experience

Before we dive into the details, let me share my personal experience. When I first started my fitness journey, I would do cardio before weight training. However, I noticed that I was not able to lift as heavy weights as I wanted to, which was hindering my muscle growth. I switched to weight training before cardio, and I noticed a significant improvement in my strength and muscle gain.

Weight Training before Cardio

If your goal is to build muscle and increase strength, it is recommended to do weight training before cardio. This is because weight training requires a lot of energy and focus, and doing cardio first can tire you out, making it difficult to perform weight training exercises with proper form and intensity. Additionally, weight training helps increase your metabolism, which means that you will burn more calories even after your workout is over.

Cardio before Weight Training

On the other hand, if your goal is to lose weight and improve cardiovascular health, it is better to do cardio before weight training. This is because cardio helps warm up your muscles, increases your heart rate, and prepares your body for the more intense weight training exercises. Additionally, doing cardio first can help you burn more calories during your workout.

Factors to Consider

When deciding whether to weight train before or after cardio, there are several factors to consider:

1. Fitness goals: Your fitness goals play a crucial role in deciding which exercise to do first. If your goal is to build muscle, weight training should come first. If your goal is to lose weight, cardio should be your first priority.

2. Time: If you are short on time, it is better to do a combination of weight training and cardio in the same workout session. However, if you have more time, it is recommended to do weight training and cardio on different days.

3. Fitness level: If you are a beginner, it is best to start with weight training before cardio. This will help you build a solid foundation of strength and proper form. However, if you are more advanced, you can switch things up and experiment with different workout combinations.

4. Type of workout: The type of workout you are doing also plays a role in deciding which exercise to do first. For example, if you are doing a full-body workout, it is recommended to do weight training before cardio. However, if you are doing a HIIT (high-intensity interval training) workout, it is better to do cardio first.

How to Make the Decision

Now that you know the pros and cons of weight training before or after cardio, you may be wondering how to make the decision. The answer is simple - listen to your body. Your body knows best what it needs. If you feel more energized and focused after weight training, then do weight training first. If you feel more invigorated and ready to take on the world after cardio, then do cardio first.

Conclusion of Should I Weight Train Before or After Cardio?

Ultimately, there is no right or wrong answer to the question of should I weight train before or after cardio. It depends on your fitness goals, the type of workout you are doing, and your overall fitness level. The key is to listen to your body and do what feels right for you.

Question and Answer

Q1. Which exercise should I do first if my goal is to build muscle?

A1. If your goal is to build muscle, it is recommended to do weight training before cardio.

Q2. Can I do weight training and cardio in the same workout session?

A2. Yes, you can do a combination of weight training and cardio in the same workout session if you are short on time.

Q3. Should I always do weight training before cardio?

A3. No, it depends on your fitness goals, the type of workout you are doing, and your overall fitness level.

Q4. What is the best way to decide whether to weight train before or after cardio?

A4. The best way to decide is to listen to your body and do what feels right for you.
