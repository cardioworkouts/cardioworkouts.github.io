Which Is The Best Time To Do Exercise For Weight Gain

Written by Luffy Oct 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling to gain weight despite consuming a high-calorie diet and lifting weights? Have you ever wondered if the timing of your workouts affects your weight gain progress? If so, you're not alone. Many people are confused about which is the best time to do exercise for weight gain. In this article, we'll explore the topic and provide you with insights that can help you achieve your weight gain goals.

When it comes to weight gain, the most important thing is to consume more calories than you burn. However, exercise can play a crucial role in building muscle mass and improving overall health. Therefore, it's important to find the optimal time to work out for maximum benefits.

The best time to do exercise for weight gain is in the morning or early afternoon. Studies have shown that working out in the morning can boost your metabolism and energy levels throughout the day, while afternoon workouts can help regulate your appetite and reduce the risk of overeating later on.

In summary, the best time to do exercise for weight gain is in the morning or early afternoon. However, it's important to find a time that works best for your schedule and lifestyle. Consistency is key when it comes to achieving your weight gain goals, so make sure to stick to a regular workout routine.

Personal Experience

As someone who struggled with gaining weight for years, I found that working out in the morning was the most effective for me. Not only did it boost my energy levels and productivity throughout the day, but it also helped me regulate my appetite and make better food choices. However, everyone is different, so it's important to experiment with different workout times to find what works best for you.

Benefits of Morning Workouts

There are several benefits to working out in the morning, especially if you're trying to gain weight. Here are some of the main advantages:

Boosts Metabolism

Working out in the morning can boost your metabolism and help you burn more calories throughout the day. This is because exercise increases your metabolic rate and keeps it elevated for hours after your workout.

How to:

Start your day with a quick workout routine that includes cardio and strength training exercises. This will help you kickstart your metabolism and keep it elevated throughout the day. Some great morning workout options include running, cycling, or a quick HIIT session.

Benefits of Afternoon Workouts

While morning workouts might be more effective for some people, afternoon workouts also have their benefits. Here are some of the main advantages:

Regulates Appetite

Working out in the afternoon can help regulate your appetite and reduce the risk of overeating later on. This is because exercise stimulates the release of hormones that suppress appetite and promote satiety.

How to:

Plan your workout for the early afternoon, preferably after lunch. This will help you avoid the midday slump and keep you energized throughout the day. Some great afternoon workout options include yoga, Pilates, or a brisk walk.

Question and Answer

Q: Can working out at night affect weight gain?

A: While working out at night can be beneficial for some people, it's generally not recommended. This is because exercise can stimulate your body and make it harder to fall asleep, which can negatively affect your weight gain progress.

Q: How often should I work out to gain weight?

A: It's recommended to work out at least 3-4 times a week to gain weight. However, the frequency and intensity of your workouts should be adjusted based on your goals, fitness level, and schedule.

Q: Should I eat before or after my workout?

A: It's recommended to eat a small meal or snack before your workout to fuel your body and improve your performance. After your workout, it's important to consume a high-protein meal or shake to help your muscles recover and grow.

Q: Can cardio affect weight gain progress?

A: While cardio can be beneficial for overall health, it's generally not recommended for weight gain. This is because cardio burns calories and can make it harder to achieve a calorie surplus, which is necessary for weight gain.

Conclusion of which is the Best Time to Do Exercise for Weight Gain

In conclusion, the best time to do exercise for weight gain is in the morning or early afternoon. However, it's important to find a time that works best for your schedule and lifestyle. Consistency and dedication are key when it comes to achieving your weight gain goals. Remember to fuel your body with a high-calorie diet and consume enough protein to support muscle growth.
