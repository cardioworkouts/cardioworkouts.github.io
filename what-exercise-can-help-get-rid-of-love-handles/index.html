Get Rid Of Love Handles With These Exercises

Written by Frank Aug 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having excess fat around your waistline that won't seem to go away no matter how hard you try? Love handles can be frustrating and make it hard to feel confident in your own skin. But don't worry, there are exercises that can help target this problem area.

The struggle to lose love handles can be a daunting task for many people. It can be frustrating to see little to no results after putting in so much effort. But with the right exercises, you can get rid of love handles and achieve the body you desire.

The best exercises to get rid of love handles are those that target the oblique muscles, which are located on the sides of your waistline. These muscles are responsible for twisting and rotating your torso, so exercises that involve side-to-side movements are especially effective.

To get rid of love handles, you should focus on exercises such as side bends, Russian twists, bicycle crunches, and side plank dips. These exercises will help strengthen and tone your oblique muscles, which will in turn reduce the appearance of love handles.

Side Bends

One exercise that has been particularly effective for targeting love handles is the side bend. To perform this exercise, stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold a weight in one hand. Slowly lean to the side, bending at the waist, and then return to the starting position. Repeat on the other side.

Personally, I have found side bends to be a great addition to my workout routine. Not only do they help target my love handles, but they also work my core and improve my overall balance.

Russian Twists

Another exercise that is effective for getting rid of love handles is the Russian twist. To perform this exercise, sit on the floor with your knees bent and your feet flat on the ground. Hold a weight with both hands and lean back slightly, engaging your core. Twist your torso to the right, then to the left, while holding the weight in front of your chest.

I have found that incorporating Russian twists into my routine has helped me strengthen my oblique muscles and improve my posture.

Bicycle Crunches

Bicycle crunches are another great exercise for targeting love handles. To perform this exercise, lie on your back with your hands behind your head and your knees bent. Lift your head and shoulders off the ground, and then alternate bringing your right elbow to your left knee and your left elbow to your right knee.

Bicycle crunches have been a staple in my workout routine for years, and I have seen significant improvement in my core strength and the appearance of my love handles.

Side Plank Dips

Finally, side plank dips are a great exercise for targeting love handles. To perform this exercise, start in a side plank position with your elbow on the ground and your body in a straight line. Dip your hip down towards the ground, and then return to the starting position.

Side plank dips have been challenging for me, but I have noticed a significant improvement in my oblique muscles and overall core strength since incorporating them into my routine.

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I do these exercises to see results?

A: Aim to do these exercises at least 2-3 times per week for best results.

Q: Will doing these exercises alone get rid of my love handles?

A: While these exercises are effective for targeting love handles, it is important to also maintain a healthy diet and incorporate cardio into your routine to see the best results.

Q: Can I do these exercises at home without any equipment?

A: Yes, all of these exercises can be done at home without any equipment.

Q: How long will it take to see results?

A: Results may vary, but with consistent effort, you should begin to see results within 4-6 weeks.

Conclusion of What Exercise Can Help Get Rid of Love Handles

If you're looking to get rid of love handles, incorporating exercises that target your oblique muscles is key. Side bends, Russian twists, bicycle crunches, and side plank dips are all effective exercises that can help you achieve your goals. Remember to also maintain a healthy diet and incorporate cardio into your routine for best results. With consistent effort, you can say goodbye to love handles and hello to a stronger, healthier body.
