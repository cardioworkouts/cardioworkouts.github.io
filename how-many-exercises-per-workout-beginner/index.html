How Many Exercises Per Workout Beginner A Guide To Getting Started

Written by Frank Jul 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a beginner wondering how many exercises you should do per workout? It can be overwhelming to start a new exercise routine, especially when you don't know where to begin. In this guide, we'll explore the ideal number of exercises for beginners to incorporate into their workouts, and how to make the most out of each exercise.

Starting a new workout routine can be tough, especially if you're not sure how many exercises you should do per workout. It's important to find a balance between pushing yourself and avoiding injury, which can be a challenge for beginners. Additionally, not knowing how many exercises to do can make you feel lost and unsure of what to do next.

How many exercises per workout beginner?

As a beginner, it's recommended that you aim for 8-10 exercises per workout. This may seem like a lot, but it's important to work all of the major muscle groups in your body to see results. Doing too few exercises can limit your progress, while doing too many can lead to burnout or injury.

When deciding which exercises to incorporate into your workout, focus on compound exercises that work multiple muscle groups at once. These include exercises like squats, lunges, push-ups, and rows. They are great for building strength and burning fat, and can be modified to suit your fitness level.

My personal experience

When I first started working out, I was unsure of how many exercises to do per workout. I would often try to do as many exercises as possible, which left me feeling tired and unmotivated. It wasn't until I started focusing on compound exercises that I saw real progress in my fitness journey. Now, I aim for 8-10 exercises per workout, and make sure to include a mix of strength and cardio exercises to keep things interesting.

How to make the most out of your exercises

When starting a new exercise routine, it's important to take things slow and listen to your body. Start with lighter weights or bodyweight exercises, and gradually increase the intensity as your fitness level improves. Additionally, make sure to focus on proper form to avoid injury and get the most out of each exercise.

One way to make the most out of your exercises is to incorporate interval training into your routine. This involves alternating between high-intensity exercises and rest periods, which can help you burn more calories and build endurance. Additionally, consider mixing up your routine by trying new exercises or adding in resistance bands or weights to challenge your muscles.

Tips for beginners

Here are some additional tips for beginners looking to start a new exercise routine:

  • Start with lighter weights or bodyweight exercises
  • Focus on compound exercises that work multiple muscle groups
  • Listen to your body and avoid pushing yourself too hard
  • Make sure to warm up and stretch before and after your workout
  • Mix up your routine to keep things interesting

How to track your progress

Tracking your progress is an important part of any fitness journey. Consider keeping a workout journal to track your exercises, weights, and reps. This can help you identify areas of improvement and stay motivated. Additionally, take progress photos every few weeks to see the changes in your body over time.

Final thoughts

Starting a new exercise routine can be overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. By aiming for 8-10 exercises per workout and focusing on compound exercises, you can see real progress in your fitness journey. Remember to take things slow, listen to your body, and have fun!

Question and Answer

Q: Should I do cardio or strength training?

A: Both cardio and strength training are important for overall fitness. Aim to do a mix of both in your workout routine.

Q: How long should I rest between exercises?

A: Rest for 30-60 seconds between sets to allow your muscles to recover.

Q: Can I do the same workout every day?

A: It's important to mix up your routine to challenge your muscles and avoid burnout. Consider alternating between different workouts or incorporating new exercises into your routine.

Q: How many days a week should I work out?

A: Aim to work out at least 3-4 days per week, and give your muscles time to rest and recover in between workouts.

Conclusion of How Many Exercises Per Workout Beginner

As a beginner, it's important to find the right balance between pushing yourself and avoiding injury. By aiming for 8-10 exercises per workout and focusing on compound exercises, you can see real progress in your fitness journey. Remember to take things slow, listen to your body, and have fun!
