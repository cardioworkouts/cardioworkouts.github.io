Cardio Workouts At Gym With Weights A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Nov 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a way to spice up your cardio routine at the gym? Incorporating weights into your workouts can be a great way to add variety and challenge yourself. In this guide, we'll explore the benefits of cardio workouts at gym with weights, provide tips for getting started, and offer some sample workouts to try.

Why Do Cardio Workouts at Gym with Weights?

Cardio workouts at gym with weights are an effective way to build strength, burn calories, and improve cardiovascular health. Adding weights to your cardio routine can also help to prevent injury, as it strengthens your muscles and improves your overall fitness level. By combining cardio and weights, you'll get a full-body workout that targets multiple muscle groups at once.

Here are some of the benefits of cardio workouts at gym with weights:

  • Improved cardiovascular health
  • Increased muscular strength and endurance
  • Burns more calories compared to traditional cardio workouts
  • Reduced risk of injury
  • Improved overall fitness level

How to Get Started with Cardio Workouts at Gym with Weights

If you're new to cardio workouts at gym with weights, it's important to start slow and gradually increase the intensity of your workouts. Before starting any exercise program, it's a good idea to consult with a personal trainer or fitness professional to ensure that you're using proper form and technique.

Here are some tips for getting started with cardio workouts at gym with weights:

  • Start with light weights and gradually increase the weight as you get stronger
  • Incorporate weights into your existing cardio routine, such as adding lunges or squats with dumbbells to your treadmill workout
  • Try new exercises that incorporate both cardio and weights, such as kettlebell swings or jump squats
  • Alternate between cardio and weight exercises to keep your heart rate up and challenge your muscles in different ways

Sample Cardio Workouts at Gym with Weights

Here are some sample cardio workouts at gym with weights to try:

Workout 1: Treadmill Intervals with Dumbbells

Warm up for 5 minutes on the treadmill, then perform the following:

  • 1 minute of walking lunges with dumbbells
  • 1 minute of running on the treadmill
  • 1 minute of squats with dumbbells
  • 1 minute of running on the treadmill
  • Repeat for 20-30 minutes

Workout 2: Kettlebell Circuit

Perform the following exercises as a circuit, resting for 30 seconds in between each exercise:

  • Kettlebell swings - 15 reps
  • Jumping jacks - 30 seconds
  • Goblet squats - 15 reps
  • Mountain climbers - 30 seconds
  • Repeat for 20-30 minutes

Workout 3: Weighted Stair Climbing

Using a weighted vest or carrying dumbbells, climb up and down a flight of stairs for 20-30 minutes. Alternate between running up the stairs and walking down, and try to increase your speed and weight as you get stronger.

Question and Answer

Q: Can cardio workouts at gym with weights help me lose weight?

A: Yes! Cardio workouts at gym with weights are a great way to burn calories and lose weight. By incorporating weights into your cardio routine, you'll also build muscle, which can help to boost your metabolism and burn more calories throughout the day.

Q: How often should I do cardio workouts at gym with weights?

A: It's recommended to aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio per week, which can include cardio workouts at gym with weights. It's also important to give your muscles time to rest and recover, so try to space out your workouts throughout the week.

Q: What kind of weights should I use for cardio workouts at gym with weights?

A: You can use a variety of weights for cardio workouts at gym with weights, including dumbbells, kettlebells, and weighted vests. Start with a weight that feels comfortable and gradually increase the weight as you get stronger.

Q: Can cardio workouts at gym with weights help to prevent injury?

A: Yes! By strengthening your muscles and improving your overall fitness level, cardio workouts at gym with weights can help to prevent injury and improve your range of motion.

Conclusion of Cardio Workouts at Gym with Weights

Cardio workouts at gym with weights are a great way to improve your cardiovascular health, build strength, and burn calories. By incorporating weights into your cardio routine, you'll challenge your muscles in new ways and reduce the risk of injury. Try some of the sample workouts above, and don't be afraid to mix and match exercises to create your own custom routine.
