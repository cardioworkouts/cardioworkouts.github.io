How to Get Started with Exercise

If you're new to exercise, getting started can be intimidating. Here are some tips to help you get started:

Exercise doesn't have to be boring. Find an activity that you enjoy, whether it's dancing, hiking, or playing sports. This will make it easier to stick to your routine and stay motivated.
