What are Pilates Machines?

Pilates machines are specialized equipment that are used in Pilates workouts. They are designed to help users build core strength, improve flexibility, and increase balance. There are several different types of Pilates machines, including the Pilates Reformer, Cadillac, Wunda Chair, and Barrel. These machines use resistance and spring-based mechanisms to provide low-impact workouts that can help users achieve their fitness goals.
