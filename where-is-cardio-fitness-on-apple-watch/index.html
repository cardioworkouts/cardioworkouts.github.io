Where Is Cardio Fitness On Apple Watch

Written by Daniel Oct 31, 2023 · 4 min read

If you are an Apple Watch user, you might be wondering where exactly you can find the Cardio Fitness feature. This feature has become increasingly popular as people have become more health conscious and want to monitor their fitness levels. However, finding it can be a bit confusing for some users.

The Cardio Fitness feature is designed to track your heart rate during workouts and everyday activities. It also provides you with an overall assessment of your cardio health by measuring your VO2 max, which is the maximum amount of oxygen your body can consume during exercise. This feature can be very beneficial for those who want to improve their fitness levels and stay healthy.

To access the Cardio Fitness feature on your Apple Watch, you need to open the Health app on your iPhone. From there, you can find the "Browse" tab and then select "Heart." You will then see the option for "Cardio Fitness."

In summary, the Cardio Fitness feature on Apple Watch can be found in the Health app on your iPhone under the "Browse" tab and then selecting "Heart" and "Cardio Fitness". This feature is beneficial for those who want to monitor their heart rate during workouts and everyday activities, and improve their overall cardio health.
