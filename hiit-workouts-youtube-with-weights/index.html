Free Hiit Workouts Youtube With Weights At Home

Get ready to sweat with the ultimate HIIT Workouts Youtube with weights!

Are you tired of the same old boring workouts? Do you find yourself struggling to stay motivated during your workout routine? If so, you're not alone. Many people struggle with finding the right workout routine that fits their needs and keeps them engaged.

The solution? High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) workouts with weights. HIIT workouts are short, intense bursts of exercise that can help you burn fat, build muscle, and improve your overall fitness level. Adding weights to your HIIT routine can increase the intensity and help you see faster results.

In this article, we'll explore the benefits of HIIT workouts youtube with weights and provide you with some of the best HIIT workouts to try at home.

What is the target of HIIT Workouts Youtube with weights?

HIIT workouts youtube with weights are designed to provide a full-body workout in a short amount of time. These workouts use a combination of high-intensity exercises and weightlifting to help you burn fat, build muscle, and improve your overall fitness level. The goal is to push your body to its limits and increase your heart rate, so you can burn more calories in less time.

Now, let's dive into some of the best HIIT workouts youtube with weights you can try at home:

Workout 1: Full Body HIIT with Weights

For this workout, you'll need a pair of dumbbells. Start with a warm-up of jumping jacks, high knees, and butt kicks for 30 seconds each. Then, complete the following exercises for 30 seconds each, with a 10-second rest in between:

  • Dumbbell squats
  • Dumbbell lunges
  • Dumbbell push press
  • Dumbbell bent-over rows
  • Dumbbell bicep curls
  • Mountain climbers

Repeat the circuit 3-4 times, with a 1-minute rest in between each circuit.

Workout 2: HIIT Cardio and Weights

For this workout, you'll need a pair of dumbbells and a jump rope. Start with a warm-up of jumping jacks, high knees, and butt kicks for 30 seconds each. Then, complete the following exercises for 30 seconds each, with a 10-second rest in between:

  • Jump rope
  • Dumbbell thrusters
  • Jump rope
  • Dumbbell deadlifts
  • Jump rope
  • Dumbbell renegade rows

Repeat the circuit 3-4 times, with a 1-minute rest in between each circuit.

Why are HIIT workouts youtube with weights so effective?

HIIT workouts with weights are so effective because they combine both cardio and strength training into one workout. This means that you're burning fat and building muscle at the same time. Additionally, the high-intensity nature of these workouts means that you're burning calories long after you've finished your workout.

How can you make HIIT workouts youtube with weights more challenging?

If you're looking to make your HIIT workouts youtube with weights more challenging, there are a few things you can do. First, you can increase the weight of your dumbbells. This will make the exercises more challenging and help you build more muscle. Second, you can decrease the rest time between exercises. This will increase the intensity of your workout and help you burn more calories.

Workout 3: Tabata HIIT with Weights

For this workout, you'll need a pair of dumbbells. Complete each exercise for 20 seconds, with a 10-second rest in between. Repeat each circuit 4 times before moving on to the next circuit.

Circuit 1:

  • Dumbbell sumo squats
  • Dumbbell thrusters

Circuit 2:

  • Dumbbell push press
  • Dumbbell bicep curls

Circuit 3:

  • Dumbbell bent-over rows
  • Dumbbell deadlifts

Conclusion of HIIT Workouts Youtube with weights

HIIT workouts youtube with weights are an excellent way to burn fat, build muscle, and improve your overall fitness level. By combining cardio and strength training into one workout, you're able to see results faster than with traditional workouts. So, grab a pair of dumbbells and try one of these HIIT workouts today!

Question and Answer

Q: How many times a week should I do HIIT workouts youtube with weights?

A: It's recommended to do HIIT workouts youtube with weights 2-3 times a week, with at least one rest day in between.

Q: Can I do HIIT workouts youtube with weights if I'm a beginner?

A: Yes, but it's important to start with lighter weights and focus on proper form to prevent injury.

Q: How long should a HIIT workout youtube with weights be?

A: A typical HIIT workout youtube with weights should be around 20-30 minutes long.

Q: Can I do HIIT workouts youtube with weights at home?

A: Absolutely! All you need is a pair of dumbbells and some space to workout.
