Apps For Physical Therapy Exercises A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to keep up with your physical therapy exercises? Do you often forget the proper form or sequence of movements? Well, you're in luck because there are apps available that can help you stay on track and achieve your physical therapy goals.

Physical therapy exercises can be challenging, especially when you're dealing with an injury or chronic pain. It's important to have a structured routine and proper guidance to ensure you're making progress and not causing further harm. However, attending in-person physical therapy sessions can be time-consuming and expensive. This is where apps for physical therapy exercises come in handy.

Apps for physical therapy exercises provide a convenient and cost-effective way to access exercise programs, guidance, and progress tracking. These apps are designed to cater to different needs, from post-injury rehabilitation to chronic pain management. They can be used by anyone, regardless of age or fitness level.

In this article, we'll explore the best apps for physical therapy exercises and how they can help you achieve your health goals. We'll cover the features, benefits, and drawbacks of each app, so you can make an informed decision.

The Benefits of Using Apps for Physical Therapy Exercises

Personally, as an individual who has undergone physical therapy, I have seen the benefits of using apps for physical therapy exercises. These apps provide a convenient, low-cost solution to keep track of your progress and stay motivated.

Apps for physical therapy exercises offer several benefits, including:

  • Customized exercise programs based on your specific needs and goals
  • Guidance on proper form and technique to prevent injury
  • Progress tracking to monitor your improvements and stay motivated
  • Convenient access to exercise programs anytime, anywhere
  • Cost-effective solution compared to in-person physical therapy sessions

The Best Apps for Physical Therapy Exercises

Now that we've covered the benefits of using apps for physical therapy exercises, let's dive into the best apps available in the market:

1. PhysiTrack

PhysiTrack is an app designed for physical therapists, but it's also accessible to patients. The app provides personalized exercise programs, video demonstrations, and progress tracking. The app allows physical therapists to monitor their patients' progress remotely and make adjustments to their exercise programs. The app is available for iOS and Android.

2. PT Wired

PT Wired is an app designed for individuals with chronic pain. The app provides exercise programs tailored to specific pain conditions, such as lower back pain, neck pain, and shoulder pain. The app also provides educational resources on chronic pain management. It's available for iOS and Android.

3. Kinetisense

Kinetisense is an app that uses motion capture technology to assess movement patterns and provide exercise programs based on the results. The app provides real-time feedback on posture, balance, and functional movements. It's designed for individuals with musculoskeletal conditions, such as arthritis and joint pain. The app is available for iOS and Android.

4. PhysioAdvisor

PhysioAdvisor is an app that provides exercise programs and educational resources on injury prevention and rehabilitation. The app covers a wide range of conditions, from sports injuries to post-operative rehabilitation. The app is available for iOS and Android.

Question and Answer

Q. Are these apps suitable for all ages?

A. Yes, these apps are suitable for all ages, but it's important to consult with your physical therapist or healthcare provider before starting any exercise program.

Q. Can these apps replace in-person physical therapy sessions?

A. These apps can supplement in-person physical therapy sessions, but they cannot replace them entirely. In-person sessions provide personalized guidance and monitoring, which cannot be replicated by an app.

Q. Are these apps covered by insurance?

A. It depends on your insurance provider and policy. Some insurance providers may cover the cost of using these apps, but it's best to check with your provider.

Q. Can these apps be used for post-operative rehabilitation?

A. Yes, these apps can be used for post-operative rehabilitation, but it's important to consult with your physical therapist or healthcare provider before starting any exercise program.

Conclusion of Apps for Physical Therapy Exercises

Apps for physical therapy exercises offer a convenient and cost-effective solution to access exercise programs, guidance, and progress tracking. These apps are designed to cater to different needs, from post-injury rehabilitation to chronic pain management. However, it's important to consult with your physical therapist or healthcare provider before starting any exercise program. With the right guidance and support, these apps can help you achieve your physical therapy goals and improve your overall health and wellbeing.
