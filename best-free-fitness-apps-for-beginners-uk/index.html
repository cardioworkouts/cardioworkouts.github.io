Best Free Fitness Apps For Beginners Uk

Written by Christine Jul 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a beginner looking to start your fitness journey without having to spend big bucks on a personal trainer or gym membership? Do you find it challenging to stay motivated and accountable? Look no further! We have compiled a list of the best free fitness apps for beginners in the UK that will help you achieve your fitness goals.

The target of this post is to provide beginners with the best free fitness apps that are available in the UK. These apps will help beginners to stay motivated, provide accountability, and help them achieve their fitness goals.

In this post, we will discuss the best free fitness apps for beginners in the UK. We will also provide a brief overview of each app and explain how it can help beginners achieve their fitness goals.

Overall, these apps offer a wide range of features that cater to different fitness goals, such as weight loss, strength training, and mindfulness. They are also user-friendly and provide a variety of workout options, making it easy for beginners to find a routine that suits them.

1. MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is a popular app that helps users track their calorie intake and exercise. It has a comprehensive database of over 11 million foods and allows users to scan barcodes to quickly log their meals. The app also provides personalized meal plans and recipes based on users' dietary preferences and goals.

Personally, I have been using MyFitnessPal for the past two years to track my calorie intake and exercise. It has helped me stay accountable and achieve my weight loss goals. The app is straightforward to use and provides a wealth of information about the nutritional value of different foods.

2. Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club is a free app that offers a range of workouts designed by professional trainers. The app provides personalized training plans based on users' fitness goals and fitness level. It also offers a variety of workout types, such as endurance, strength, and mobility.

I have used Nike Training Club for the past year to supplement my gym workouts. The app is easy to use and provides a variety of workout options that can be done at home or in the gym. The app also allows users to track their progress and earn rewards for completing workouts.

3. Couch to 5K

Couch to 5K is a popular app for beginners who want to start running. The app provides a training plan that gradually increases users' running time over eight weeks. It also offers audio cues that guide users through their workouts and provides motivational tips to keep them going.

Couch to 5K is an excellent app for beginners who want to start running but are unsure where to start. The app provides a structured training plan that gradually builds endurance and helps users achieve their fitness goals.

4. Fitbit

Fitbit is a free app that syncs with Fitbit wearables to track users' activity and sleep. The app provides personalized fitness plans and challenges to keep users motivated. It also allows users to track their progress and set goals for themselves.

Fitbit is an excellent app for beginners who want to track their activity and sleep but do not want to invest in an expensive wearable. The app is user-friendly and provides a wealth of information about users' fitness and sleep habits.

Conclusion of Best Free Fitness Apps for Beginners UK

Overall, these apps are the best free fitness apps that are available in the UK for beginners. They offer a wide range of features that cater to different fitness goals and are user-friendly. By using these apps, beginners can stay motivated, accountable, and achieve their fitness goals.
