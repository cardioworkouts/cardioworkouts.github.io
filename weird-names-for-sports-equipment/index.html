Pickleball

One of the most popular and quirky sports in recent years is pickleball. This game is played on a court with a low net, similar to tennis or badminton. However, the paddles used in pickleball are much smaller than tennis rackets, and the ball is made of plastic with holes in it. The name "pickleball" is said to have come from the founder's dog, Pickles, who used to chase after the ball during the game. However, some people also believe that the name comes from the phrase "pickle boat," which refers to a boat that comes back to port without a catch, similar to how players in pickleball "fish" for the ball.

Wiffle Ball

Another popular and quirky game is Wiffle ball. This game is played with a plastic ball with holes in it and a plastic bat. The name "Wiffle" is a combination of the words "whiff," meaning to swing and miss, and "waffle," meaning to change direction frequently. The name is meant to reflect the unpredictable nature of the game, where the ball can curve and dip in unexpected ways.

Goggle Ball

Lesser-known but equally weird is goggle ball. This game is played with a soft foam ball and a pair of goggles with mesh lenses. The goggles are used to obscure the players' vision, adding an extra level of challenge to the game. The name "goggle ball" is simply a description of the equipment used in the game.

Z-Ball

Z-ball is a high-energy game played with a unique ball that bounces in unpredictable ways. The ball is made of a durable foam material and has a distinctive "Z" shape, which causes it to spin and bounce erratically. The name "Z-ball" is a reference to the ball's shape.

  • Ask for advice: Talk to coaches, trainers, or experienced players for recommendations on equipment.
  • Try before you buy: If possible, test out different equipment before making a purchase to find the right fit for you.

Roller Derby

One of the more unique sports out there is roller derby. This high-intensity game is played on roller skates and involves a lot of physical contact between players. One of the weird names associated with roller derby is the "jammer panty." This is a piece of fabric worn on a player's helmet that designates them as the "jammer," or the player who scores points for their team. Despite its strange name, the jammer panty is an essential piece of equipment in roller derby.
