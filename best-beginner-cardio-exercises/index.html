Best Beginner Cardio Exercises A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for cardio exercises that are suitable for beginners? Cardio exercises are an essential part of any fitness routine, especially for those who want to improve their cardiovascular health, lose weight, or build endurance. However, finding the right exercises that are not too challenging for beginners can be a daunting task.

Many people avoid cardio exercises because they find them too challenging, or they do not know where to start. Others may have tried some cardio exercises but gave up due to injuries or lack of motivation. If you are one of these people, you are not alone.

In this article, we will discuss the best beginner cardio exercises and how to perform them correctly, to help you achieve your fitness goals without feeling overwhelmed or discouraged.

Cardio exercises are physical activities that increase your heart rate and breathing rate, which in turn, improves your cardiovascular health. The American Heart Association recommends that adults should engage in at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic exercise or 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity aerobic exercise per week, or a combination of both.

Walking

Walking is one of the best beginner cardio exercises, and it's also one of the most accessible. All you need is a comfortable pair of shoes and a safe place to walk. Walking is a low-impact exercise that can help you burn calories, reduce stress, and improve your overall health. You can start with a 10-minute walk and gradually increase the duration and intensity over time.

Cycling

Cycling is another low-impact exercise that is ideal for beginners. You can cycle indoors on a stationary bike or outdoors on a regular bike. Cycling can help you build endurance, strengthen your leg muscles, and improve your cardiovascular health. You can start with a 20-minute ride and gradually increase the duration and intensity over time.

Jogging/Running

Jogging or running is a high-impact exercise that can be challenging for beginners, but it's also an effective way to improve your cardiovascular health and burn calories. If you are new to jogging or running, start with a slow pace and short distance, and gradually increase the duration and intensity over time. You can also incorporate walking intervals to reduce the impact on your joints.

Dancing

Dancing is a fun way to get your heart rate up and burn calories. You can dance at home, in a dance class, or at a party. Dancing can improve your balance, coordination, and flexibility, as well as your cardiovascular health. You can start with a 10-minute dance session and gradually increase the duration and intensity over time.

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I do cardio exercises as a beginner?

A: As a beginner, you should aim to do cardio exercises at least three times a week, gradually increasing the frequency as your fitness level improves.

Q: Can I do cardio exercises if I have joint pain?

A: If you have joint pain, low-impact exercises such as walking, cycling, or swimming may be more suitable for you than high-impact exercises such as running or jumping.

Q: How long should a cardio session last?

A: As a beginner, you can start with a 20 to 30-minute cardio session and gradually increase the duration and intensity over time.

Q: Can I do cardio exercises every day?

A: It's recommended to give your body a rest day between cardio sessions to allow for recovery and prevent injury. As you become fitter, you can gradually increase the frequency of your cardio sessions.

Conclusion of Best Beginner Cardio Exercises

In conclusion, cardio exercises are essential for improving your cardiovascular health, losing weight, and building endurance. As a beginner, it's important to start with low-impact exercises such as walking, cycling, or dancing, and gradually increase the duration and intensity over time. Remember to listen to your body and consult with a healthcare professional if you have any concerns or medical conditions. With consistency and patience, you can achieve your fitness goals and enjoy the benefits of a healthy lifestyle.
