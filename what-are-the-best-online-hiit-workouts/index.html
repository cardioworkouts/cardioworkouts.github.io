Get Your Sweat On The Best Online Hiit Workouts

Written by Christine Nov 12, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old workout routine? Do you want to get your heart pumping and your muscles burning in a quick and effective way? High-intensity interval training (HIIT) may be just what you need. But with so many options for online workouts, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. Don't worry, we've got you covered.

If you're looking for a workout that will challenge you both physically and mentally, HIIT is the way to go. HIIT involves short bursts of intense exercise followed by periods of rest or lower intensity exercise. This not only gets your heart rate up and burns calories, but it also helps improve your endurance and overall fitness level.

So, what are the best online HIIT workouts? Let's take a look at some of our favorites:

The Body Coach TV

I stumbled upon The Body Coach TV on YouTube a few months ago and it quickly became one of my go-to workouts. Joe Wicks, the creator of The Body Coach, has a contagious energy that will motivate you to push yourself through the workout. His videos range from 10-30 minutes and are perfect for anyone short on time. Plus, they require minimal equipment and can be done in the comfort of your own home.

Popsugar Fitness

Popsugar Fitness has a vast library of HIIT workouts on their website and YouTube channel. They offer a variety of styles, from dance-inspired HIIT to equipment-based HIIT. The instructors are knowledgeable and provide modifications for all fitness levels. The best part? Most of their workouts are 30 minutes or less.

Beachbody On Demand

Beachbody On Demand offers a wide range of workout programs, including some of the most popular HIIT workouts like Insanity and P90X. With a monthly subscription, you have access to hundreds of workouts, nutrition plans, and support groups. While some of the workouts require equipment, many can be done with just your bodyweight.

Fitness Blender

Fitness Blender is a great option for those who want a more budget-friendly workout. They offer hundreds of free HIIT workouts on their website and YouTube channel, as well as paid programs for more personalized workouts. The instructors provide clear instructions and modifications, making it easy to follow along.

Question and Answer:

Q: How often should I do HIIT workouts?

A: It's recommended to do HIIT workouts 2-3 times per week, with rest days in between. This allows your body to recover and prevents injury.

Q: Do I need equipment for HIIT workouts?

A: While some HIIT workouts require equipment, many can be done with just your bodyweight. However, investing in a set of dumbbells or resistance bands can add variety and intensity to your workouts.

Q: Can beginners do HIIT workouts?

A: Absolutely! Most HIIT workouts offer modifications for beginners, making it accessible to all fitness levels. However, if you're new to exercise or have any health concerns, it's always a good idea to consult with a physician before starting a new workout program.

Q: How long should a HIIT workout be?

A: A typical HIIT workout ranges from 20-45 minutes, depending on the program and your fitness level. However, even a 10-15 minute HIIT workout can be effective if done at high intensity.

Conclusion:

HIIT workouts are a great way to get a full-body workout in a short amount of time. With the plethora of online options available, there's no excuse not to give it a try. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned athlete, there's a HIIT workout out there for you. So, what are you waiting for? Get your sweat on!
