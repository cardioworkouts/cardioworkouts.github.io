If you're looking for something more electronic, then EDM (Electronic Dance Music) might be the genre for you. 3. The Benefits of Listening to Music During Workouts

Listening to music during workouts has many benefits. First and foremost, music can distract you from the pain and discomfort of exercise, making it easier to push through your routines. Additionally, music can improve your mood and enhance your overall workout experience. Studies have shown that listening to music can increase endurance, improve performance, and even reduce perceived exertion during exercise.

4. How to Create the Perfect Workout Playlist on Soundcloud
