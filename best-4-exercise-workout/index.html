Best 4 Exercise Workout For A Healthy Body

Written by Petter Jun 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of going to the gym and doing the same old exercises? Do you want to try something new and effective? Look no further, as we have compiled the best 4 exercise workout that will get you in shape in no time!

Many people struggle with finding the right exercises that will target all the muscle groups and provide a full-body workout. It can be overwhelming to choose from the numerous options available, but we have simplified it for you by selecting the best 4 exercise workout.

The best 4 exercise workout includes squats, push-ups, lunges, and planks. These exercises target all the major muscle groups, including the legs, glutes, chest, back, and core. They are also effective in burning calories and building strength.

Squats

Squats are a great exercise that targets the legs and glutes. To perform a squat, stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and your toes pointing forward. Slowly bend your knees and lower your body as if you are sitting on a chair. Make sure your knees do not extend past your toes. Hold for a few seconds and then return to the starting position.

I personally love squats as they have helped me tone my legs and glutes. It is important to maintain proper form to avoid injury and get the most out of the exercise.

Push-Ups

Push-ups are a classic exercise that targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps. To perform a push-up, start in a plank position with your hands shoulder-width apart. Slowly lower your body towards the ground by bending your elbows. Make sure your back is straight and your core is engaged. Push yourself back up to the starting position.

Push-ups have helped me build upper body strength and tone my arms. They can be challenging at first, but with practice, you can gradually increase the number of repetitions.

Lunges

Lunges are an effective exercise that targets the legs and glutes. To perform a lunge, stand with your feet hip-width apart and take a step forward with your right foot. Lower your body until your right knee is at a 90-degree angle. Your left knee should be slightly above the ground. Push yourself back up to the starting position and repeat on the other side.

Lunges have helped me improve my balance and strengthen my legs. It is important to maintain proper form and not let your knees extend past your toes.

Planks

Planks are a great exercise that targets the core muscles. To perform a plank, start in a push-up position and lower your forearms to the ground. Your elbows should be directly under your shoulders. Hold this position for as long as you can while keeping your back straight and your core engaged.

Planks have helped me improve my posture and strengthen my core muscles. It is important to maintain proper form and not let your hips sag.

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I do the best 4 exercise workout?

A: It is recommended to perform the best 4 exercise workout at least 3-4 times a week for maximum benefits.

Q: Can I modify the exercises if I am a beginner?

A: Yes, you can modify the exercises by starting with fewer repetitions or using lighter weights. It is important to listen to your body and gradually increase the intensity.

Q: Can I do the best 4 exercise workout at home?

A: Yes, the best 4 exercise workout can be done at home with little to no equipment. You can use your body weight or add weights to increase the intensity.

Q: How long should I hold a plank for?

A: It is recommended to hold a plank for at least 30 seconds and gradually increase the time as you get stronger.

Conclusion of Best 4 Exercise Workout

The best 4 exercise workout is a great way to target all the major muscle groups and improve your overall fitness level. Remember to maintain proper form, gradually increase the intensity, and listen to your body. With consistency and dedication, you will see results in no time!
